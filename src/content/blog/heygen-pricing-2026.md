[HeyGen](/tools/heygen/) is an AI video creation platform known for its exceptional video translation and personalization capabilities. But understanding its credit-based pricing system can be confusing. This guide breaks down exactly what you get at each tier and which plan makes financial sense for your needs.

## HeyGen Pricing Overview 2026

| Plan | Monthly Price | Video Credits | Seats | Key Differentiator |
|------|--------------|---------------|-------|-------------------|
| Free | $0 | 3 videos (1 min each) | 1 | Testing only |
| Creator | $24/mo | 15 min/mo | 1 | Full feature access |
| Business | $72/mo | 30 min/mo | 3 | Custom avatars, priority |
| Enterprise | Custom | Negotiable | Unlimited | SLA, SSO, dedicated support |

Annual billing offers a discount — typically 20% off monthly pricing.

## Free Plan

**What you get:**
- 3 video credits (up to 1 minute each)
- Access to basic stock avatars
- Text-to-speech in multiple languages
- Watermark on all outputs
- Basic editing tools

**Limitations:**
- Watermarked output (unusable for professional content)
- Only 3 total videos ever (not monthly)
- Limited avatar selection
- No video translation
- No custom avatars
- No API access
- 720p quality maximum

**Verdict**: The free plan exists purely for testing. The 3-video lifetime limit and watermark [make](/tools/make/) it impractical for any real use. Test avatar quality and decide quickly whether to upgrade.

## Creator Plan ($24/month)

**What you get:**
- 15 minutes of video per month
- 100+ stock avatars
- No watermark
- 1080p output
- All editing features
- Video translation (basic)
- Instant Avatar (create avatar from selfie video)
- AI script generation
- Priority rendering

**Best for**: Individual marketers, content creators, and small business owners producing occasional video content.

**How far 15 minutes goes**: Approximately 3-5 typical marketing or training videos per month (assuming 3-5 minutes each). For social media clips (30-60 seconds), you could produce 15-30 pieces.

**Cost per minute**: $1.60/minute of final video

## Business Plan ($72/month)

**What you get (beyond Creator):**
- 30 minutes of video per month
- 3 team seats included
- Custom avatar creation
- Brand [kit](/tools/kit/) (colors, logos, fonts)
- API access for automation
- Priority support
- Advanced video translation (lip-sync preservation)
- Personalization at scale (via API)

**Best for**: Marketing teams, sales organizations, and companies producing regular video content who need custom avatars and automation.

**Key unlock at this tier**: The API access is the real differentiator. You can programmatically generate personalized videos — imagine 500 sales prospecting videos, each with the recipient's name and company mentioned by the avatar. This is HeyGen's killer feature for sales teams.

**Cost per minute**: $2.40/minute of final video

## Enterprise Plan (Custom)

**What you get (beyond Business):**
- Negotiable video minutes (typically 100+ min/month)
- Unlimited seats
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Custom SLA with uptime guarantees
- SSO/SAML integration
- Priority rendering queue
- Custom model training
- Volume discounts on minutes

**Best for**: Large organizations with high-volume video needs, compliance requirements, or custom integration needs.

**When to consider**: If you're consistently using 30+ minutes/month and need more than 3 seats, Enterprise pricing often works out cheaper per minute than stacking Business seats.

## Understanding Credit Consumption

Not all video content uses credits equally:

| Action | Credit Usage |
|--------|-------------|
| Standard avatar video | 1 min credit = 1 min video |
| Video translation | ~2x credits (processing intensive) |
| Instant Avatar | 1 min credit = 1 min video |
| Custom Avatar | Included in Business+ creation |
| Re-rendering same script | Uses new credits |

**Important**: Unused credits do not roll over month-to-month. If you have 15 minutes and only use 8, those 7 remaining minutes disappear at renewal.

## Tips to Maximize Value

1. **Script thoroughly before recording**: Every re-[render](/tools/render/) costs credits. Get the script right first.
2. **Use shorter videos**: Social media clips (30-60s) give more content per credit than long-form.
3. **Batch production**: Plan all monthly videos upfront to avoid wasting credits near renewal.
4. **Use free tools for editing**: Generate raw avatar footage in HeyGen, then add effects/transitions in [CapCut](/tools/capcut/) to stretch content further.
5. **Annual billing**: Saves ~20% if you're committed to the platform.

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

**Choose Creator ($24/mo) if**: You're an individual creating occasional video content (3-5 videos/month), don't need custom avatars, and won't use the API.

**Choose Business ($72/mo) if**: You're a team needing custom avatars, API access for personalization, or producing 10+ videos monthly. The 3 included seats [make](/tools/make/) it cost-effective for small teams.

**Choose Enterprise if**: You need 50+ minutes/month, have compliance requirements, or want volume pricing.

**Consider [Synthesia](/tools/synthesia/) instead if**: Your primary need is corporate training content with a more polished editor and better template library.

HeyGen's pricing is competitive for what it delivers. The video translation and API personalization features justify the cost for teams that leverage them. Just be mindful of the credit expiration policy and plan production schedules accordingly.
