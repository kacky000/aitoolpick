For businesses, AI pricing isn't a single number — it's a **per-user cost multiplied across your whole team**, so a $10/seat difference compounds fast. This guide ranks the best AI tools for business in 2026 by per-user price, covering Microsoft Copilot, Gemini for Workspace, ChatGPT Team, and Claude — and which delivers the most value per seat.

## Per-User Pricing Overview (2026)

| Tool | Price/User/Mo | Best For |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Gemini for Workspace | ~$14–22 (bundled in plan) | Google-first teams |
| ChatGPT Team | $25–30 | Teams wanting a standalone AI |
| Claude Team | $25 | Teams doing heavy writing/analysis |
| Microsoft 365 Copilot | $30 (add-on) | Microsoft Office-first teams |

The single biggest factor in your cost: **which office suite your team already pays for.** AI built into the suite you use is almost always cheaper than bolting on a separate one.

## The Two Cheapest Routes for Teams

### Google-first: Gemini for Workspace
Google bundled Gemini into Workspace business plans instead of charging a separate AI seat. That makes it the **cheapest path to team-wide AI** — roughly $14/user for Business Standard with AI included, versus ~$34 under the old "suite + AI add-on" model. Full breakdown in [Google Workspace AI pricing](/blog/google-workspace-ai-pricing-2026/).

### Microsoft-first: Microsoft 365 Copilot
At **$30/user/month** (annual, on top of M365), Copilot is the premium option — but if your team lives in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook, it works where the work happens. See [Copilot vs Gemini pricing](/blog/copilot-vs-gemini-pricing-2026/) for the head-to-head.

## Standalone AI for Teams

If you don't want to tie AI to an office suite, the standalone team plans are:

- **[ChatGPT Team](/blog/chatgpt-for-business-2026/)** — $25–30/user/mo, the most general-purpose option with the broadest model lineup
- **[Claude Team](/blog/claude-for-business-2026/)** — $25/user/mo, the strongest for long-form writing, analysis, and document work

These make sense when you want **one AI tool everyone uses** regardless of which office suite they're in.

## Per-User Math That Actually Matters

For a 25-person team, here's the annual difference:

| Choice | Per User/Mo | Annual (25 seats) |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Gemini for Workspace (Standard) | ~$14 | ~$4,200 |
| ChatGPT / Claude Team | ~$25 | ~$7,500 |
| Microsoft 365 Copilot | ~$30 add-on | ~$9,000+ |

The spread is **more than double** between the cheapest and most expensive routes — which is why "which AI is best for business" usually starts with "which suite are you already on."

## How to Choose

| Your team... | Best Pick |
|--------------|-----------|
| Runs on Google Workspace | Gemini for Workspace (cheapest) |
| Runs on Microsoft 365 | M365 Copilot (fits Office) |
| Wants one standalone AI for everyone | ChatGPT Team or Claude Team |
| Does heavy writing & analysis | Claude Team |
| Wants the lowest total cost | Gemini for Workspace |

## The Bottom Line

The best AI tool for business by price is **the one built into the suite you already pay for** — Gemini if you're on Workspace, Copilot if you're on Microsoft 365. Only reach for a standalone plan (ChatGPT or Claude Team) when you specifically want one AI everyone shares. For most teams watching cost, Gemini for Workspace is the cheapest path to team-wide AI.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the cheapest AI tool for business?

Gemini for Workspace is typically cheapest because Google bundled the AI into business plans (~$14–22/user) instead of charging a separate add-on. Standalone plans (ChatGPT/Claude Team) run ~$25 and Microsoft Copilot is ~$30/user.

### Is Microsoft Copilot worth $30 per user?

For teams deeply embedded in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook, yes — it works inside the apps they already use. For Google-first teams, Gemini for Workspace delivers similar value for less.

### Should businesses use ChatGPT Team or a bundled AI?

Use a bundled AI (Gemini or Copilot) if your team lives in one office suite — it's cheaper and better integrated. Choose ChatGPT or Claude Team when you want a single standalone AI everyone uses regardless of suite.

### How much does AI cost per employee?

Expect roughly $14–30/user/month depending on the tool. The bundled office-suite options sit at the lower end; premium add-ons like Microsoft 365 Copilot at the higher end.
