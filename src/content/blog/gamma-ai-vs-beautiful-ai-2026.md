**Gamma and Beautiful.ai** are both AI-powered presentation tools that automate design, but they work very differently. Gamma generates full presentations from a text prompt in seconds. Beautiful.ai focuses on smart slide layouts that auto-adjust as you edit. Neither is PowerPoint — they're new-paradigm tools for faster, more consistent slide creation.

## Gamma: Create a Deck from a Prompt

Gamma's core feature is radical: type a topic, click Generate, and get a complete, designed presentation in about 30 seconds. The AI writes the outline, fills in content, chooses a visual design, and adds relevant images.

**What Gamma does well:**
- First-draft speed is unmatched — nothing else creates a working deck this fast
- Decks can be shared as live web URLs (track who views, how long)
- Documents and webpages, not just slides (one canvas, multiple formats)
- One-click restyle (change the entire visual theme instantly)
- Presentation analytics (views, time spent, engagement by slide)

**Gamma's limitations:**
- Less design control than traditional tools
- AI content requires editing for accuracy (it hallucinates details)
- Not ideal when you have a strict brand guide requiring pixel-perfect layouts

**Best for:** Salespeople, marketers, and founders who need to create decks quickly and iterate fast. Also strong for internal presentations where speed matters more than polish.

## Beautiful.ai: Smart Layouts That Auto-Adjust

Beautiful.ai takes a different approach: it doesn't write your content, but it makes your content look designed automatically. When you add a fourth bullet point, the slide layout automatically reorganizes to accommodate it. Text stays legible, spacing stays balanced, alignment stays consistent.

**What Beautiful.ai does well:**
- Professional, polished output with minimal design effort
- Consistent visual style across all slides
- Great for teams maintaining brand standards
- Solid template library across dozens of slide types
- Export to PowerPoint or PDF

**Beautiful.ai's limitations:**
- No AI text generation — you write all content
- No free plan (14-day trial converts to paid)
- Sharing requires downloading, not a live URL
- No presentation analytics
- Less flexible for non-standard slide formats

**Best for:** Teams maintaining consistent brand presentations, consultants creating client-facing decks, and professionals who want polish without needing design skills but are willing to write their own content.

Gamma's free plan gives you real access — 400 AI credits is enough to create several complete decks before hitting a limit. Beautiful.ai's trial converts to paid after 14 days with no ongoing free option.

## Which Should You Choose?

**Choose Gamma if:**
- You need to create presentations quickly (sales pitches, weekly updates, internal decks)
- You want to share presentations as links with viewer analytics
- You value speed of creation over fine-grained design control
- You want a free plan to start

**Choose Beautiful.ai if:**
- You're part of a team with strict brand guidelines
- You write your own content but want it to look professionally designed
- Your presentations are client-facing and need to match enterprise standards
- You want consistent templates across a large team

**Use neither if:** Your presentations require complex animations, highly custom layouts, or deep PowerPoint compatibility — use PowerPoint, Keynote, or Google Slides for those cases.

## The Real Trade-off

Gamma trades design control for speed. Beautiful.ai trades content creation for design consistency. They're solving different problems.

For most modern use cases — especially at startups, in sales roles, or for content marketing — Gamma's speed advantage is decisive. The ability to have a first draft in 30 seconds and iterate from there beats spending an hour in Beautiful.ai building slides from scratch.
