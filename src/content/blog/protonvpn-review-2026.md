Proton VPN comes from the team behind Proton Mail — the encrypted email service used by journalists, activists, and privacy advocates worldwide. But does its privacy pedigree translate into a VPN worth using daily?

After testing Proton VPN across multiple devices and use cases, here's our honest assessment for 2026.

## What Is Proton VPN?

Proton VPN is a Swiss-based VPN service that emphasizes transparency, privacy, and open-source code. Unlike most VPN providers, Proton VPN publishes its source code for public audit and operates under Swiss privacy laws — some of the strongest in the world.

It's one of the few VPNs offering a genuinely usable free tier with no data caps.

## Proton VPN Pricing

| Plan | Monthly | 1-Year | 2-Year |
|------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | — | — |
| **VPN Plus** | $9.99/mo | $3.99/mo | $2.99/mo |
| **Proton Unlimited** | $12.99/mo | $9.99/mo | $7.99/mo |

The VPN Plus plan is the sweet spot for most users. Proton Unlimited bundles Proton Mail, Drive, Calendar, and Pass — worth it only if you use the full Proton ecosystem.

## The Free Plan — Genuinely Good

This is Proton VPN's biggest differentiator. The free plan includes:

- **No data limits** — use as much bandwidth as you want
- **No speed throttling** — you get full speed on free servers
- **No ads** — no ad injection or tracking
- **5 countries** — US, Japan, Netherlands, Romania, Poland
- **1 device** at a time

Most "free VPNs" are either data-limited (Windscribe at 10GB/month) or ad-supported (some mobile VPNs). Proton VPN's free tier is legitimately usable for everyday browsing. The catch is the limited server selection — you can't choose specific cities, and popular servers can get crowded.

## Speed Performance

Proton VPN's speeds have improved significantly over the past year. On VPN Plus with nearby servers:

- **Download**: 400-600 Mbps (gigabit connection baseline)
- **Latency**: 15-25ms additional vs no VPN
- **Upload**: 200-350 Mbps

These are strong numbers — not quite ExpressVPN-fast, but faster than most competitors. The free plan is noticeably slower during peak hours due to server congestion, but still adequate for HD streaming and browsing.

## Security & Privacy

This is where Proton VPN genuinely excels:

- **Swiss jurisdiction** — outside 5/9/14 Eyes surveillance alliances
- **No-logs policy** — independently audited by Securitum
- **Open-source apps** — all client apps are open source and publicly audited
- **Secure Core** — routes traffic through privacy-friendly countries (Switzerland, Iceland, Sweden) before exiting
- **Perfect Forward Secrecy** — session keys can't be retroactively decrypted
- **Kill switch** — blocks all traffic if VPN drops

Proton VPN is one of the only VPN providers where you can verify their privacy claims by inspecting the code yourself. This level of transparency is rare and valuable.

## Features

### What Works Well
- **Secure Core** routing for high-risk users (journalists, activists)
- **NetShield** ad and malware blocker (Plus plan)
- **Split tunneling** on Android, Windows, and Linux
- **VPN Accelerator** for improved long-distance speeds
- **Tor over VPN** — access .onion sites without Tor Browser
- **Port forwarding** for P2P and gaming

### What's Missing
- **No dedicated IP option** — all users share IP addresses
- **No Smart DNS** — less convenient for streaming on devices that don't support VPN apps (smart TVs, consoles)
- **Limited free server locations** — only 5 countries

## Streaming & Torrenting

Proton VPN Plus reliably unblocks Netflix, Disney+, BBC iPlayer, and most major streaming services. The free plan does not support streaming — those servers are restricted to Plus subscribers.

P2P torrenting is fully supported on Plus plan servers with port forwarding, making it one of the better VPNs for file sharing.

## Who Should Use Proton VPN?

**Ideal for:**
- Privacy-focused users who value transparency and open-source
- Anyone wanting a reliable free VPN without catches
- Journalists and activists in restrictive regions (Secure Core)
- Users already in the Proton ecosystem (Mail, Drive, Calendar)

**Look elsewhere if:**
- You need the absolute fastest speeds (try ExpressVPN)
- You want a dedicated IP address
- You need Smart DNS for streaming on all devices
- Budget is your only concern (Surfshark is cheaper on paid plans)

## The Verdict

Proton VPN is the best VPN for privacy purists. No other provider matches its combination of Swiss jurisdiction, open-source transparency, independent audits, and Secure Core routing. The free plan is the most generous in the industry.

The trade-off is that it's not the cheapest paid VPN, and it lacks some convenience features (Smart DNS, dedicated IPs) that competitors offer. But if privacy is your priority — not just a marketing claim — Proton VPN backs it up with verifiable action.

**Rating: 4.5/5**
