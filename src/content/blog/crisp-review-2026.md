# Crisp Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Crisp is all-in-one customer messaging platform with live chat, chatbot, knowledge base, and shared inbox for small businesses. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Crisp?

Crisp is all-in-one customer messaging platform with live chat, chatbot, knowledge base, and shared inbox for small businesses. It is designed for startups, small businesses, saas companies and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the customer support space.

## Pros

- Very generous free tier (2 seats included)
- Per-workspace pricing instead of per-seat
- Built-in chatbot builder with no-code
- Clean and modern interface

## Cons
