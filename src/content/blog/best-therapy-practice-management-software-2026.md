Running a therapy or wellness practice means juggling scheduling, clinical notes, insurance claims, telehealth, and a client portal. The right practice management platform pulls all of that into one system. Here's our guide to the **best therapy practice management software** in 2026, with clear recommendations by practice type.

## Quick Comparison

| Tool | Starting price | Free plan | Best for |
|------|----------------|-----------|----------|
| TherapyNotes | $49/mo | No | Behavioral-health practices |
| SimplePractice | $29/mo | No | Solo therapists wanting polish |
| Jane App | $54/mo | No | Multi-discipline clinics |
| Practice Better | $25/mo | No | Wellness coaches & dietitians |

## 1. TherapyNotes — Best for Behavioral Health

[TherapyNotes](/pricing/therapynotes/) is purpose-built for therapists and counselors. Its clinical note templates map directly to behavioral-health documentation standards, and telehealth is bundled across plans. Pricing is $49/month solo and $59/month for groups (plus per-clinician fees).

**Best for:** therapists and group practices that bill insurance and want clinical-grade notes. Read the [full pricing breakdown](/blog/therapynotes-pricing-2026/).

## 2. SimplePractice — Best for Solo Practitioners

[SimplePractice](/pricing/simplepractice/) pairs solid clinical features with the most polished client experience in the category — online booking, a clean portal, and a strong mobile app. Pricing is $29 (Starter), $69 (Essential), and $99 (Plus with telehealth).

**Best for:** solo therapists and wellness pros who want ease of use. Read the [SimplePractice review](/blog/simplepractice-review-2026/) or the [pricing guide](/blog/simplepractice-pricing-2026/).

## 3. Jane App — Best for Multi-Discipline Clinics

[Jane App](/pricing/jane-app/) is the go-to for clinics combining physiotherapy, massage, acupuncture, and mental health. Its booking engine and discipline-specific charting are best-in-class. Pricing starts at $54/month (Base), $79 with insurance.

**Best for:** multi-practitioner, multi-discipline clinics that need serious scheduling.

## 4. Practice Better — Best for Wellness & Nutrition

[Practice Better](/pricing/practice-better/) targets health coaches, dietitians, and nutritionists with client management, programs, protocols, and telehealth. Pricing starts at $25/month, the lowest entry in this list.

**Best for:** wellness coaches and nutrition practices that run programs and don't need behavioral-health notes.

## How to Choose the Right Platform

Ask three questions:

1. **Do you bill insurance?** If yes, you need a plan with claim management — TherapyNotes ($49) or SimplePractice Essential ($69).
2. **What's your discipline?** Pure therapy → TherapyNotes or SimplePractice. Mixed clinic → Jane App. Wellness coaching → Practice Better.
3. **Is telehealth core?** TherapyNotes bundles it; SimplePractice puts it on Plus ($99); Jane offers it as an add-on.
