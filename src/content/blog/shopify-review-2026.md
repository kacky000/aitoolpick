# Shopify Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Shopify is leading e-commerce platform for building online stores with payments, shipping, and marketing tools. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Shopify?

Shopify is leading e-commerce platform for building online stores with payments, shipping, and marketing tools. It is designed for e commerce businesses, dropshippers, retail brands and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the ecommerce space.

## Pros

- Complete e-commerce solution out of box
- Massive app ecosystem (8000+ apps)
- Handles hosting, security, and speed
- Multi-channel selling (social, POS)

## Cons

- Transaction fees unless using Shopify Payments
- Monthly cost adds up with apps
- Customization limited without code
