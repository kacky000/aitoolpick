Jasper AI has settled into a three-tier pricing structure for 2026, with plans ranging from $39/month to custom enterprise pricing. Here's a full breakdown of what each plan includes and which one makes sense for your situation.

## Jasper AI Pricing at a Glance

| Plan | Monthly Billing | Annual Billing | Best For |
|------|----------------|----------------|----------|
| Creator | $49/month | $39/month | Solo writers and freelancers |
| Pro | $69/month | $59/month | Growing teams and power users |
| Business | Custom | Custom | Large teams and enterprises |

Annual billing saves roughly 20% across the board. Both Creator and Pro plans come with a **7-day free trial**, so you can test features before committing.

## Creator Plan — $49/month ($39/month annual)

The Creator plan is designed for individual writers and content creators who need a reliable AI writing assistant without team features.

**What's included:**

- 1 user seat
- Unlimited AI-generated words
- 50+ writing templates (blog posts, ads, emails, social media)
- Brand Voice (1 voice profile)
- SEO mode for basic keyword optimization
- Browser extension for Chrome and Edge
- Chat-based AI assistant
- Access to Jasper's latest language models

**What's missing:**

- No team collaboration features
- No custom workflows or automation
- Limited brand voice profiles (just 1)
- No API access
- No priority support

**Who should pick this:** Freelance writers, solopreneurs, and bloggers who produce content independently. If you're working alone and mainly need help drafting blog posts, emails, or social media copy, Creator covers the essentials. The single brand voice limitation only matters if you write for multiple brands.

## Pro Plan — $69/month ($59/month annual)

The Pro plan adds collaboration tools and more customization, making it suitable for small teams and professionals managing multiple brands.

**What's included:**

- Everything in Creator
- Up to 3 user seats
- 3 Brand Voice profiles
- Custom workflows and templates
- Art generation (AI image creation)
- Priority chat support
- Advanced analytics and performance insights
- Collaboration tools (shared projects, comments)
- Jasper API access (limited)

**The $20/month jump is worth it if:**

- You work with a team of 2-3 people
- You manage content for multiple brands or clients
- You need image generation alongside text
- Custom workflows would save you repetitive setup time

**Who should pick this:** Marketing teams, agencies handling a few clients, and content professionals who need more than basic drafting. The 3 brand voice profiles and collaboration features justify the upgrade for anyone working across multiple projects.

## Business Plan — Custom Pricing

The Business plan is Jasper's enterprise offering with no published pricing. You'll need to contact their sales team for a quote.

**What's included:**

- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited user seats
- Unlimited Brand Voice profiles
- Advanced security (SSO, SOC 2 compliance)
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom AI model fine-tuning
- Full API access
- Team management and permissions
- Custom onboarding and training
- Priority support with SLA guarantees

**What to expect on pricing:** Based on industry reports and user discussions, Business plans typically start around $500/month for small teams and scale with seat count and usage. Jasper doesn't publish these numbers, so the actual quote depends on your team size and needs.

**Who should pick this:** Companies with 10+ content creators, agencies managing many client brands, or organizations that need enterprise security features like SSO.

## Is the 7-Day Free Trial Worth It?

Yes. Both Creator and Pro plans offer a 7-day free trial with full feature access. You get unlimited word generation during the trial, which is enough time to test Jasper on real projects.

A few tips to get the most out of the trial:

1. **Prepare your test content in advance** — have 3-5 real writing tasks ready
2. **Set up Brand Voice immediately** — this is where Jasper differentiates itself
3. **Try multiple templates** — don't just test the chat; explore blog post, ad copy, and email templates
4. **Compare output quality** against your current workflow or other tools

## Nonprofit Discount

Jasper offers a **20% discount for nonprofits** across all plans. You'll need to verify your organization's nonprofit status through Jasper's application process. This brings the Creator plan down to about $31/month (annual) and Pro to about $47/month (annual) — a meaningful savings for organizations with tight budgets.

## How to Save on Jasper

1. **Go annual** — saves 20% compared to monthly billing
2. **Start with Creator** — upgrade to Pro only when you actually need team features
3. **Use the free trial strategically** — test with real projects, not throwaway experiments
4. **Check for nonprofit eligibility** — 20% off is significant over a year
5. **Negotiate Business plans** — enterprise pricing is always negotiable, especially for annual commitments

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

- **Solo content creator on a budget** → Creator ($39/month annual). Covers all the basics.
- **Small marketing team (2-3 people)** → Pro ($59/month annual). The collaboration tools and extra brand voices pay for themselves.
- **Agency or enterprise team** → Business (custom). Contact sales for a quote. Don't settle on the first offer.
- **Not sure yet** → Start with the 7-day free trial on Pro. You can always downgrade to Creator.
