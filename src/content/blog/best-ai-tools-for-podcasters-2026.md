# Best AI Tools for Podcasters in 2026

Podcasting in 2026 is faster than ever thanks to AI. From editing by text to automatic transcription and clip generation, these tools help creators produce polished episodes with less effort.

## Quick Picks

- **Best all-in-one**: Descript — edit audio by editing text.
- **Best for audio cleanup**: Adobe Podcast — studio-quality enhancement.
- **Best for transcription**: Otter.ai — accurate, fast transcripts.
- **Best for clips**: Opus Clip — auto-generate social shorts.

## What to Look For

- **Editing speed**: Text-based or AI-assisted editing.
- **Audio quality**: Noise removal and voice enhancement.
- **Transcription**: Accurate transcripts for show notes and SEO.
- **Repurposing**: Turn episodes into clips and posts.
- **Pricing**: Subscription vs per-use.

## Top AI Tools for Podcasters

### 1. Descript

Edit your podcast like a document—delete words, remove filler, and fix mistakes by editing the transcript. Includes Overdub voice cloning and multitrack support.

### 2. Adobe Podcast

Its Enhance Speech feature removes background noise and makes recordings sound studio-quality, even from basic mics.

### 3. Otter.ai

Generates accurate transcripts for show notes, searchable archives, and improved SEO on your episode pages.

### 4. Opus Clip

Turns long episodes into short, captioned clips optimized for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts—great for promotion.

### 5. Riverside

Records high-quality remote interviews with local recording and AI-powered editing and transcription built in.

## Building Your Podcast Stack

- **Record**: Riverside for remote interviews.
- **Clean up**: Adobe Podcast for audio.
- **Edit**: Descript for fast, text-based editing.
- **Promote**: Opus Clip for social clips, Otter for show notes.

## The Bottom Line

The best podcasting stack combines recording, cleanup, editing, and promotion tools. Descript anchors most workflows, with Adobe Podcast and Opus Clip adding polish and reach.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the best AI tool for editing a podcast?

Descript is the most popular for its text-based editing and built-in transcription.

### Can AI improve poor audio quality?

Yes—Adobe Podcast's Enhance Speech can dramatically clean up noisy or low-quality recordings.

### How do I turn episodes into social clips?

Opus Clip automatically finds highlights and generates captioned vertical clips for social platforms.
