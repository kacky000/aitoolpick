**[Grok](/tools/grok/)** and **[ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/)** price their AI in fundamentally different ways. Grok is bundled into X (formerly Twitter) Premium subscriptions — so if you already pay for X, you may effectively get Grok included. ChatGPT sells AI as a standalone product. That difference, not the headline number, decides which is cheaper for you.

This guide breaks down both pricing paths and which one wins based on what you already pay for.

## Pricing Overview

### Grok Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Limited Grok access |
| X Premium | ~$8/mo | Basic Grok + X features |
| X Premium+ | ~$40/mo | Full Grok + best X features |
| SuperGrok | ~$30/mo | Standalone Grok, higher limits |

### ChatGPT Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Capable default model |
| Go | $8/mo | Entry tier (select regions) |
| Plus | $20/mo | Advanced models + tools |
| Pro | $200/mo | Near-unlimited heavy use |

## The Key Difference: Bundle vs Standalone

This is the whole comparison.

**Grok is a bundle play.** Its strongest tier rides inside X Premium+ at roughly $40/month — but that price also buys you the full X social platform experience (ad reductions, longer posts, creator tools). If you are already an X Premium+ subscriber, Grok's best tier is *already paid for*. There is also SuperGrok (~$30/mo) for people who want Grok without the social bundle.

**ChatGPT is standalone.** Your $20 Plus subscription buys AI and only AI — advanced models, image generation, voice, custom GPTs, and the largest tool ecosystem. Nothing about a social platform is bundled in.

## Cost Scenario: Who Already Pays for X?

**If you already pay for X Premium+** (~$40/mo for the social platform), Grok's flagship AI is effectively free on top — making Grok the cheaper *marginal* cost for AI.

**If you do not use X**, paying ~$40/month for X Premium+ just to get Grok makes little sense. SuperGrok at ~$30/mo or ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo are the rational standalone choices — and ChatGPT Plus is the cheaper of those two.

So the decider is simple: **already on X Premium+? Grok wins on cost. Not on X? ChatGPT Plus is cheaper.**

## Feature Value at Each Price

- **Grok's edge**: Real-time access to X's live data feed, a less filtered conversational style, and bundling with a platform you may already use.
- **ChatGPT's edge**: The broadest tool ecosystem, custom GPTs, image and voice, and a mature $8 Go entry tier for light users.

## Verdict

**Choose Grok** if you already subscribe to X Premium or Premium+ — the AI comes bundled, making it the cheapest path to a capable assistant with live social data.

**Choose ChatGPT** if you want standalone AI with the widest feature set. At $20 (or $8 for Go), it is cheaper than buying X Premium+ purely for Grok.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Grok or ChatGPT cheaper?

It depends on whether you already pay for X. If you have X Premium+, Grok's best tier is bundled in. If not, ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo is cheaper than buying X Premium+ (~$40/mo) just for Grok.

### Can I get Grok without paying for X?

Yes. SuperGrok is a standalone Grok subscription (~$30/mo) that does not require an X social bundle, though it still costs more than ChatGPT Plus.

### What does ChatGPT Plus include that Grok does not?

A broader tool ecosystem, custom GPTs, image generation, voice mode, and the cheaper $8 Go entry tier. Grok's edge is live X data and platform bundling.

### Does free Grok compare to free ChatGPT?

Both offer limited free access. ChatGPT's free tier is more established across general tasks; Grok's free access is tied to your X account and is more limited.
