# Acorns Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Acorns is micro-investing app that automatically rounds up everyday purchases and invests the spare change into diversified etf portfolios, plus banking and retirement accounts. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Acorns?

Acorns is micro-investing app that automatically rounds up everyday purchases and invests the spare change into diversified etf portfolios, plus banking and retirement accounts. It is designed for investing beginners, young adults, passive investors and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the personal finance space.
