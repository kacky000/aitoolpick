Cursor has quickly become one of the most popular AI-powered code editors, but its pricing structure can be confusing — especially after the June 2025 switch from request-based to credit-based billing. Here's everything you need to know about Cursor's pricing in 2026.

## Cursor Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Credits | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| **Hobby** | Free | Limited | Trying Cursor out |
| **Pro** | $20/mo | $20 pool | Individual [developers](/best-for/developers/) |
| **Pro+** | $60/mo | $60 pool | Power users |
| **Ultra** | $200/mo | $200 pool | Heavy daily usage |
| **Teams** | $40/user/mo | $40 pool | Development teams |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Large organizations |

## Hobby (Free)

The free tier gives you a limited number of Agent requests and Tab completions. It's enough to test whether Cursor's AI features fit your workflow, but you'll hit limits quickly if you code full-time.

**What you get:**
- Limited Tab completions
- Limited Agent requests
- Access to basic models
- No credit card required

## Pro ($20/month)

This is Cursor's most popular plan and the sweet spot for most individual developers.

**What you get:**
- Unlimited Tab completions
- $20 monthly credit pool for premium models
- Access to [Claude](/tools/claude/) Sonnet, GPT-4o, and other frontier models
- Extended Agent limits

The credit pool works like a wallet — every AI interaction deducts credits based on the model used. Once your pool runs out, you can still use basic features but lose access to premium models until next month.

## Pro+ ($60/month)

Everything in Pro, but with 3x the credits. Designed for developers who consistently burn through the $20 pool before month-end.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Pro
- $60 monthly credit pool (3x Pro)
- Same model access

## Ultra ($200/month)

The top individual tier with 10x the usage of Pro. If you're building multiple projects or pair-programming with AI all day, this keeps you from ever hitting limits.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Pro+
- $200 monthly credit pool (10x Pro)
- Priority access to new features

## Teams ($40/user/month)

Built for organizations that want centralized control.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Pro for each user
- Centralized billing
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Admin controls and user management

## Enterprise (Custom)

For large companies needing compliance features.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Teams
- Pooled usage across the organization
- Invoice/PO billing
- SCIM seat management
- AI code tracking API and audit logs
- Granular admin and model controls
- Priority support and dedicated account management

## How the Credit System Works

In June 2025, Cursor moved from counting "premium requests" to a credit-based system:

1. Every paid plan includes a monthly credit pool equal to the plan price in dollars
2. Each AI interaction costs credits based on the model and token usage
3. Cheaper models (like GPT-4o Mini) consume fewer credits than frontier models (like Claude Opus)
4. Tab completions are unlimited and don't consume credits on paid plans
5. Unused credits don't roll over
