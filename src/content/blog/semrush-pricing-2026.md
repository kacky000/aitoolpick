Semrush starts at $140/month — nearly 5x Ahrefs' Starter plan. Here's exactly what you're paying for and whether it's worth it.

## Semrush Pricing Plans 2026

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Users | Keyword Tracking |
|------|---------|--------------------|-------|-----------------|
| Pro | $140 | $117 | 1 | 500 |
| Guru | $250 | $208 | 1 | 1,500 |
| Business | $500 | $416 | 3 | 5,000 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom |

Annual billing saves ~16%.

## What Each Plan Includes

### Pro — $140/month ($117 annual)

The entry plan, but it's already feature-rich compared to competitors at this price.

**Includes:**
- Keyword research (unlimited searches, 500 tracked keywords)
- Site audit (100,000 pages/month)
- Position tracking (500 keywords)
- Backlink analysis
- On-page SEO checker
- Social media tracker
- 5 projects

**Limitations:**
- 1 user only
- No historical data (only 12 months rolling)
- No Content Marketing Platform
- No API access

**Verdict:** Good for individual consultants and small business owners doing all-in-one SEO.

### Guru — $250/month ($208 annual)

The most popular plan for marketing teams.

**Adds over Pro:**
- Content Marketing Platform (SEO writing assistant, topic research)
- Historical data (full Semrush database history)
- 15 projects
- 1,500 tracked keywords
- Multi-location tracking
- Looker Studio integration

**Verdict:** The upgrade that matters most is the Content Marketing Platform + historical data. If you create content regularly, Guru pays for itself.

### Business — $500/month ($416 annual)

For agencies and large teams.

**Adds over Guru:**
- 3 user seats (additional: $45/user/month)
- 40 projects
- 5,000 tracked keywords
- API access (10,000 units/month)
- White-label reports
- Extended limits across all tools

**Verdict:** Makes sense for agencies billing clients for SEO. White-label reports alone justify the upgrade.

## Hidden Costs to Watch

1. **Additional users**: $45/user/month (Business) — can add up fast for teams
2. **Local SEO add-on**: $20–$40/month per location
3. **Agency Growth Kit**: Custom pricing for client management
4. **ImpactHero**: $200/month for content funnel analysis
5. **Semrush Social**: $30/month for social media management

A fully loaded Guru plan with a team of 3 easily hits $400+/month.

## Does Semrush Have a Free Plan?

Yes — a limited free account allows:
- 10 keyword searches/day
- 10 domain analytics reports/day
- 1 project

The free plan is useful for occasional checks but not for active SEO campaigns.

**7-day free trial**: Available for Pro and Guru plans with a credit card.

## Semrush vs. Ahrefs: Which Is Worth More?

| Feature | Semrush | Ahrefs |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Entry price | $140/mo | $29/mo |
| Keyword volume data | Better (US-focused) | Good |
| Backlink data | Good | Better |
| Content tools | Excellent (Guru+) | Limited |
| PPC research | Best-in-class | Basic |
| Local SEO | Add-on available | Limited |

**Choose Semrush if:** You run PPC campaigns, need an all-in-one marketing suite, or use content marketing heavily.

**Choose Ahrefs if:** Backlink analysis is your priority, you want the best link data, or you need a lower entry price.

## Is Semrush Worth $140/Month?

For a business generating $10,000+/month from organic traffic, yes — absolutely. For a blogger or side project, the $29 Ahrefs Starter or even free tools might be sufficient.

**Use Semrush if:**
- You're running paid campaigns and need PPC intelligence
- Your team needs an all-in-one marketing platform
- Content creation is central to your strategy (Guru plan)
- You need local SEO tools for multiple locations

**Consider alternatives if:**
- You only need backlink analysis → Ahrefs Lite ($129)
- You're on a tight budget → Mangools ($29) or free tools
- You need basic rank tracking only → SE Ranking ($55/month)

## Bottom Line

Semrush is the most comprehensive SEO platform available — but it's priced as such. At $140/month for Pro, you're paying for breadth (keyword research + site audit + social + competitive analysis + PPC) rather than depth. If you need the full suite, it's worth every dollar. If you only need SEO, consider Ahrefs Standard ($249) for better value.
