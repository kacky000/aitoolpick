AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings with automatic action items. - **Live Transcription**

- **Highlights**

## Pricing

Otter.ai offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Pro**: $16.99/user/month
- **Business**: $30/user/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing (contact sales)

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Real-time transcription during meetings
- Joins [Zoom](/tools/zoom-meetings/)/Teams/Meet automatically
- AI-generated action items and summaries
- Searchable transcript archive

### Cons

- Accuracy drops with accents or crosstalk
- Free tier limited to 300 minutes/month
- Occasional missed speaker attribution
