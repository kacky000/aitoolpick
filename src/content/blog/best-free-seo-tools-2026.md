You don't need a $140/month Semrush subscription to do SEO. Here are 15 genuinely free tools that cover everything from keyword research to technical audits.

## Free Keyword Research Tools

### 1. Google Search Console (Free, Essential)

The single most important free SEO tool. Shows exactly which queries your site ranks for, click-through rates, and impressions. Directly from Google — no tool approximates it.

**Best for:** Finding keyword opportunities in your existing content, tracking ranking changes.

**Limitation:** Only works for your own site. No competitor data.

### 2. Google Keyword Planner (Free with Ads account)

Google's own keyword research tool shows search volumes and competition data. Requires a Google Ads account but you don't need to run ads.

**Best for:** Finding search volumes for new keyword targets.

**Limitation:** Volumes are shown as ranges (100–1K, 1K–10K) unless you spend money on ads.

### 3. Ahrefs Free Tools (Actually Free)

Ahrefs offers several free tools at ahrefs.com/free-seo-tools:
- **Ahrefs Webmaster Tools**: Site audit + backlink data for your own site (free forever)
- **Keyword Generator**: 100 keyword ideas per search
- **SERP Checker**: See top 10 rankings for any keyword
- **Backlink Checker**: Top 100 backlinks for any domain

[Full Ahrefs pricing →](/blog/ahrefs-pricing-2026/)

### 4. Ubersuggest (Free Tier)

Ubersuggest offers 3 free searches/day with keyword ideas, search volume, and difficulty scores.

**Best for:** Quick keyword checks when you don't need deep data.

### 5. Answer The Public (2 free searches/day)

Visual tool that shows the questions and phrases people search around any keyword. Excellent for content ideation.

**Best for:** Finding long-tail question keywords for blog posts.

## Free Backlink Analysis Tools

### 6. Ahrefs Backlink Checker (Free)

Top 100 backlinks for any domain, completely free. Powered by Ahrefs' database — the same data you'd pay $129/month for in the Lite plan.

**Access:** ahrefs.com/backlink-checker

### 7. Moz Link Explorer (10 free queries/month)

Moz's DA (Domain Authority) checker and basic backlink data. Requires a free account.

**Best for:** Checking DA for competitor or potential link partner sites.

## Free Technical SEO Tools

### 8. Google PageSpeed Insights (Free)

Tests your page speed and Core Web Vitals directly from Google. Shows exactly what's slowing down your pages with fix recommendations.

**Best for:** Pre-launch site audits and finding CWV issues.

### 9. Screaming Frog SEO Spider (Free up to 500 URLs)

The most powerful free technical SEO crawler. Finds broken links, duplicate content, missing meta tags, redirect chains, and much more.

**Best for:** Technical audits for sites under 500 pages (free version limit).

**Limitation:** 500 URL cap. For larger sites, the paid version is £149/year.

### 10. GTmetrix (Free Tier)

More detailed page speed analysis than PageSpeed Insights, with waterfall charts and video recordings of page load.

**Best for:** Diagnosing specific performance bottlenecks.

### 11. Bing Webmaster Tools (Free)

Often overlooked, but Bing's tools include a free backlink report and keyword research that sometimes surface different data than Google.

**Best for:** Getting a second perspective on your technical health and keyword opportunities.

## Free Rank Tracking Tools

### 12. Google Search Console (Again)

GSC's Performance report is the most accurate rank tracker for your own queries — because it's Google's actual data.

**Best for:** Tracking ranking changes over time for keywords you already rank for.

### 13. Rank Math (Free WordPress Plugin)

Combines on-page SEO analysis, schema markup, sitemap generation, and rank tracking in one free WordPress plugin.

**Best for:** WordPress sites needing all-in-one on-page optimization.

## Free Site Audit Tools

### 14. Ahrefs Site Audit via Webmaster Tools (Free)

The full Ahrefs site audit crawls your site and identifies 100+ technical issues — crawl errors, slow pages, missing meta descriptions, broken links. Free for your own sites.

**Access:** Sign up for Ahrefs Webmaster Tools (free, verify domain ownership).

### 15. Semrush Site Audit (10 free crawls)

Semrush's free account includes 10 site audits with 100 pages each. Good for a monthly health check.

## The Best Free SEO Stack (Zero Budget)

Combine these for a full free SEO setup:

| Need | Tool | Cost |
|------|------|------|
| Traffic & ranking data | Google Search Console | Free |
| Keyword research | Ahrefs Keyword Generator + Answer The Public | Free |
| Backlink analysis | Ahrefs Backlink Checker + Webmaster Tools | Free |
| Technical audit | Screaming Frog (500 URLs) + GSC | Free |
| Page speed | PageSpeed Insights + GTmetrix | Free |
| On-page optimization | Rank Math (WordPress) | Free |

This stack covers 80% of what you'd do with a paid tool. You only need to pay when you need:
- Competitor keyword data
- More than 500 pages in technical audits
- Historical data trends
- Ongoing rank tracking for 100+ keywords

## When to Upgrade to a Paid Tool

**Consider paying when:**
- You need competitor keyword data (what keywords competitors rank for)
- You're tracking 50+ keywords consistently
- You're doing site audits for client sites (not just your own)
- You need backlink prospecting for link building campaigns

For most of these, **Ahrefs Starter ($29/month)** or **Semrush Pro ($140/month)** is the next step up.
