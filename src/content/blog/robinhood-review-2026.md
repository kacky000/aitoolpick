# Robinhood Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Robinhood is commission-free stock, etf, options, and cryptocurrency trading platform with a mobile-first design aimed at making investing accessible to everyone. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Robinhood?

Robinhood is commission-free stock, etf, options, and cryptocurrency trading platform with a mobile-first design aimed at making investing accessible to everyone. It is designed for beginner investors, mobile traders, young adults and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the personal finance space.
