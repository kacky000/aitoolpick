# Teachable Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Teachable is online course platform for creators to build and sell courses, coaching, and digital downloads with no coding required. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Teachable?

Teachable is online course platform for creators to build and sell courses, coaching, and digital downloads with no coding required. It is designed for course creators, coaches, educators and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the education space.

## Pricing

There is a generous free tier available. Paid plans start at $59/mo (monthly) and go up to $249/mo for premium features.

## Pros

- Easy course builder with drag-and-drop
- Built-in payment processing and affiliates
- Custom domain and branding on all plans
- Coaching and community features included

## Cons

- Limited site customization options
