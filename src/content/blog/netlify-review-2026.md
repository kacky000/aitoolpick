# Netlify Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Web development platform for deploying modern sites with serverless functions, forms, and identity management. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the devops space.

## What is Netlify?

Web development platform for deploying modern sites with serverless functions, forms, and identity management. Founded in 2014, it has established itself as a significant player in the devops category. With a user rating of 4.5/5, it primarily serves Developers, Agencies, Small Teams, Jamstack Developers.

## Key Features

- **Git Deploy**
- **Serverless Functions**
- **Forms**
- **Identity**
- **Split Testing**
- **Edge Functions**
- **Analytics**

## Pricing

Netlify offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Pro**: $19/user/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing (contact sales)

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Extremely generous free tier (100GB bandwidth)
- Built-in forms without backend code
- Split testing (A/B) built-in
- Framework-agnostic — works with anything

### Cons

- Build times can be slow for large sites
- Serverless functions less powerful than [Vercel](/tools/vercel/)'s
- UI can be confusing for complex setups

## Who is Netlify Best For?

Netlify is ideal for Developers, Agencies, Small Teams, Jamstack Developers. If you fall into any of these categories, Netlify deserves serious consideration for your workflow.

## Netlify Alternatives

### Lovable

### Bubble

## Final Verdict

With a 4.5/5 user rating, Netlify has proven itself as a strong choice in the devops category. It particularly shines for Developers, Agencies, offering a compelling mix of features and value. While there are areas for improvement, the overall package makes Netlify worth trying if you need a reliable devops solution in 2026.
