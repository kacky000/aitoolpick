# Hootsuite Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Enterprise social media management platform with scheduling, analytics, social listening, and team collaboration. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the social media space.

## What is Hootsuite?

Enterprise social media management platform with scheduling, analytics, social listening, and team collaboration. Founded in 2008, it has established itself as a significant player in the social media category. With a user rating of 4.2/5, it primarily serves Enterprise, Agencies, Marketing Teams, Social Media Managers.

## Key Features

- **Scheduling**
- **Analytics**
- **Social Listening**
- **Inbox**
- **Advertising**
- **Ai Writing**
- **Team Management**

## Pricing

Hootsuite offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Not available (trial may be offered)
- **Professional**: $99/month
- **Team**: $249/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing (contact sales)

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Supports all major social platforms
- Social listening for brand monitoring
- Comprehensive analytics and reporting
- OwlyWriter AI for content creation

### Cons

- Expensive — no free tier
- Interface feels dated
- Steep learning curve for full features

## Who is Hootsuite Best For?

Hootsuite is ideal for Enterprise, Agencies, Marketing Teams, Social Media Managers. If you fall into any of these categories, Hootsuite deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
