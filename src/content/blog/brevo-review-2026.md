# Brevo Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Brevo is all-in-one marketing platform (formerly sendinblue) offering email, sms, whatsapp campaigns, crm, and transactional messaging with generous free tier. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Brevo?

Brevo is all-in-one marketing platform (formerly sendinblue) offering email, sms, whatsapp campaigns, crm, and transactional messaging with generous free tier. It is designed for small businesses, startups, ecommerce and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the email marketing space.

## Pros

- Generous free plan with 300 emails per day
- Multi-channel: email, SMS, WhatsApp, push in one tool
- Built-in CRM included in all plans
- Transactional email/SMS API for developers

## Cons

- Email editor less polished than Mailchimp
- Automation features less advanced than ActiveCampaign
- Customer support can be slow on free plan
