# Monday.com Pricing 2026: Complete Breakdown by Plan

Monday.com is one of the most popular work management platforms, but its pricing can be confusing thanks to per-seat minimums and tiered features. Here's everything you need to know about what you'll actually pay in 2026.

## Quick Pricing Overview

| Plan | Monthly (per seat) | Minimum Seats | Minimum Monthly Cost |
|------|-------------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Free | $0 | 2 max | $0 |
| Basic | $9 | 3 | $27 |
| Standard | $12 | 3 | $36 |
| Pro | $19 | 3 | $57 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom |

*Prices shown are with annual billing. Monthly billing adds approximately 18–20%.*

## Free Plan

Monday.com's Free plan supports up to **2 seats** — that's it. You get 3 boards, unlimited docs, and 200+ templates. It's extremely limited but fine for personal use.

## Basic Plan — $9/seat/month

The Basic plan unlocks unlimited boards, items, and viewers. You also get 5GB of file storage and basic dashboards. However, you still don't get automations, integrations, or timeline views.

**Best for:** Very small teams that just need a visual task board.

## Standard Plan — $12/seat/month

This is where Monday.com starts to shine. The Standard plan adds **automations** (250 actions/month), **integrations** (250 actions/month), timeline and Gantt views, calendar view, and guest access.

**Best for:** Growing teams that need workflow automation and multiple project views.

## Pro Plan — $19/seat/month

The Pro plan is designed for teams managing complex workflows. You get **25,000 automations/month**, **25,000 integrations/month**, time tracking, formula columns, chart view, and private boards.

**Best for:** Larger teams, agencies, and project-heavy organizations.

## Enterprise Plan

Custom pricing with advanced security features, multi-level permissions, enterprise-grade analytics, and premium support. Contact Monday.com's sales team for a quote.

## Hidden Costs to Watch

1. **Seat minimums**: You buy in multiples (3, then 5, 10, 15, etc.). You can't buy exactly 4 seats — you'll pay for 5.
2. **Monthly vs annual**: Monthly billing costs 18–20% more. Always go annual if you're committed.
3. **Add-ons**: Monday WorkForms, Monday CRM, and other products have separate pricing.
