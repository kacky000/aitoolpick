# Figma vs Penpot 2026: Which Design Tool Should You Use?

Figma is the industry-standard collaborative design tool; Penpot is the open-source, self-hostable challenger that's grown fast since Figma's pricing changes. In 2026, the choice often comes down to ecosystem versus ownership.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Figma** if you want the deepest features, plugins, and a designer-developer ecosystem everyone already uses. Choose **Penpot** if you want a free, open-source tool you can self-host and fully control.

## Pricing Compared

| Plan | Figma | Penpot |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free | Limited files/editors | Unlimited (cloud or self-host) |
| Paid | Professional $12+/editor/mo | $0 (self-host costs only) |

Penpot is free and open source—your only cost is hosting if you self-deploy. Figma charges per editor as teams scale. See the full [Figma pricing breakdown](/blog/figma-pricing-2026) for current tiers.

## Where Figma Wins

- **Ecosystem**: Massive plugin library and near-universal adoption.
- **Dev handoff**: Mature inspect, variables, and code-export tooling.
- **Advanced features**: Auto layout, prototyping, and design systems are best-in-class.

## Where Penpot Wins

- **Open source**: Free to use and modify; no vendor lock-in.
- **Self-hosting**: Keep all design data on your own infrastructure.
- **Web standards**: Built on SVG/CSS, friendly to developers.

## Head-to-Head

| Factor | Figma | Penpot |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Features | Deepest | Strong, catching up |
| Plugins | Huge | Growing |
| Ownership | SaaS | Open source / self-host |
| Best for | Teams at scale | Privacy & control |

## Who Should Choose What?

**Choose Figma if you:**
- Need the richest feature set and plugins
- Collaborate across a large design-dev org
- Want the tool everyone already knows

**Choose Penpot if you:**
- Want an open-source, self-hostable tool
- Care about data ownership and cost
- Are comfortable trading some polish for control

If you want the full landscape, see [Best Figma Alternatives](/blog/best-figma-alternatives-2026).

## The Bottom Line

**Figma** is the feature-rich industry standard; **Penpot** is the open-source, self-hostable alternative for teams that value ownership. Pick Figma for ecosystem, Penpot for control.
