# Midjourney Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

AI image generation tool known for creating stunning, artistic visuals from text prompts. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the image generation space.

## What is Midjourney?

AI image generation tool known for creating stunning, artistic visuals from text prompts. Founded in 2022, it has established itself as a significant player in the image generation category. With a user rating of 4.7/5, it primarily serves Artists, Designers, Content Creators, Marketers, Game Developers.

- **Image Variation**
- **Upscaling**
- **Style Reference**

## Pricing

Midjourney offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Not available (trial may be offered)
- **Basic**: $10/month
- **Standard**: $30/month
- **Pro**: $60/month
- **Mega**: $120/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Best-in-class aesthetic quality
- Highly consistent artistic style
- Active community for inspiration
- Fast generation times

### Cons

- No free tier anymore
- [Discord](/tools/discord/)-only interface (web beta limited)
- Less control over exact compositions
- Struggles with text in images
