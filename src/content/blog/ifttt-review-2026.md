# IFTTT Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

IFTTT is simple automation platform connecting 800+ apps and devices with if-this-then-that logic. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is IFTTT?

IFTTT is simple automation platform connecting 800+ apps and devices with if-this-then-that logic. It is designed for individuals, smart home users, beginners and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the automation space.
