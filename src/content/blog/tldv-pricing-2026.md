## What Each Plan Actually Gets You

- **Free ($0)** — Unlimited recordings and transcripts in 30+ languages, plus **10 AI summaries per month**. The unlimited recording is genuinely useful; the summary cap is the nudge toward Pro.
- **Pro ($29/mo, $18 annual)** — Removes the AI summary cap entirely and unlocks integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Slack, and Zapier. The standard tier for individuals who rely on AI notes.
- **Business ($59/mo annual)** — Adds **AI coaching** for sales teams, call analytics, admin controls, and native CRM playbooks. Built for revenue teams that review reps' calls.
- **Enterprise (custom)** — Privately hosted AI, advanced admin controls, a dedicated CSM, and custom invoicing for large orgs with security requirements.

## Tips to Lower Your tl;dv Cost

1. **Pay annually** and watch for promos — Pro drops from $29 to $18/user, often lower during sales.
2. **Stay free if you record but rarely summarize** — unlimited recordings cost nothing; only the 10-summary cap pushes you to Pro.
3. **Only buy Business for coaching** — if you don't manage a sales team, Pro covers the AI features.
4. **Consolidate seats** — per-user pricing adds up; license only the reps who need it.
