**[Beehiiv](/tools/beehiiv/)** uses a flat monthly fee based on subscriber count rather than taking a cut of your revenue. For growing newsletters that's a major advantage over Substack — but the price scales with list size, so understanding the tiers matters. Here's the full breakdown.

## Beehiiv Plans and Pricing in 2026

| Plan | Price (annual) | Subscriber cap | Right for |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| Launch | $0 | Up to 2,500 | Starting out |
| Scale | ~$39/mo | Up to 10,000 | Monetizing newsletters |
| Max | ~$99/mo | Up to 100,000 | Established publishers |
| Enterprise | Custom | 100,000+ | Large media operations |

Prices increase as your subscriber count grows within each tier. Monthly billing adds roughly 20% over annual. Crucially, **Beehiiv takes 0% of your subscription or ad revenue** — unlike [Substack](/blog/substack-pricing-2026)'s 10% cut.

## Launch Plan (Free): Genuinely Usable

Beehiiv's free tier is one of the most generous in the newsletter space:

- Up to 2,500 subscribers
- Unlimited sends
- Basic email + web publishing
- Subscriber tagging and segmentation
- Newsletter recommendations network

**Not in Launch:** paid subscriptions, custom domain, the ad network, advanced analytics, and most automations. For a newsletter that isn't monetizing yet, Launch is a real free product, not a trial.

## Scale Plan (~$39/month)

Scale unlocks the monetization stack that makes Beehiiv worth paying for:

- **Paid subscriptions** — keep 100% (minus Stripe fees)
- **Ad Network** — Beehiiv places ads in your newsletter and pays you
- Custom domain and full branding removal
- 3 team seats
- Advanced segmentation and automations (Magic links, polls, surveys)
- API access

The **Ad Network** is the sleeper feature. Beehiiv automatically matches sponsors to your newsletter, generating revenue without you selling ads. For many newsletters this offsets the entire subscription cost.

## Max Plan (~$99/month)

Max targets publishers running newsletters as a business:

- Up to 100,000 subscribers
- 10 team seats
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics and benchmarking
- NewsletterXP course and white-glove tools
- Multiple publications

If you're below 10,000 subscribers, Scale is almost always the right choice. Max makes sense once list size or team needs push you past Scale's limits.

## Beehiiv vs Substack: The Core Decision

| | [Beehiiv](/tools/beehiiv/) | [Substack](/tools/substack/) |
|------|------|------|
| Cost model | Flat monthly fee | 10% of revenue |
| Revenue cut | 0% | 10% |
| Ad network | Yes | No |
| Best at | $1,000+/mo revenue | Discovery, starting out |

The math is decisive at scale. A newsletter earning $5,000/month pays Substack ~$500/month but Beehiiv just ~$99/month flat. Below $1,000/month, Substack's percentage is cheaper and simpler. Above it, Beehiiv wins clearly.

## Beehiiv vs Kit (ConvertKit)

[Kit](/tools/kit/) is the other major flat-fee competitor. Kit excels at automation and selling digital products to an existing audience; Beehiiv excels at newsletter growth, web publishing, and the built-in ad network. Creators focused on email automation lean Kit; creators focused on media-style growth lean Beehiiv.

## The Hidden Cost: Subscriber-Based Pricing

Beehiiv's price scales with subscriber count, including unengaged subscribers. A list with 9,000 subscribers where only 3,000 open emails still pays the 10,000-tier price. Regularly pruning inactive subscribers keeps you in a lower tier — a small cost-control habit that flat-fee tools reward and percentage tools don't.

## Is Beehiiv Worth the Price?

**Free → Scale is worth it the moment you monetize.** Paid subscriptions plus the ad network typically cover the $39/month several times over.

**Scale → Max is worth it** only when you cross 10,000 subscribers or need more team seats. Don't upgrade for features you won't use.

**Beehiiv vs Substack:** if you're past $1,000/month in revenue and own your audience, Beehiiv's flat fee saves real money. If you're still growing and rely on discovery, start on Substack and migrate later.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Beehiiv free?

Yes. The Launch plan is free for up to 2,500 subscribers with unlimited sends, though it excludes paid subscriptions, custom domains, and the ad network.

### Does Beehiiv take a cut of my revenue?

No. Unlike Substack's 10% fee, Beehiiv charges a flat monthly subscription and takes 0% of your subscription or ad revenue (standard Stripe processing fees still apply).

### Is Beehiiv cheaper than Substack?

It depends on revenue. Above roughly $1,000/month in subscription income, Beehiiv's flat fee is far cheaper than Substack's 10% cut. Below that, Substack is usually cheaper.
