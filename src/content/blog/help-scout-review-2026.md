# Help Scout Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Help Scout is customer support platform built around shared email inbox with knowledge base, live chat, and a human-focused approach. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Help Scout?

Help Scout is customer support platform built around shared email inbox with knowledge base, live chat, and a human-focused approach. It is designed for small businesses, saas companies, remote teams and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the customer support space.

## Pricing

There is a generous free tier available. Paid plans start at $25/mo (per user per month) and go up to $65/mo for premium features.

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Free | Yes |
| Standard | $25/mo |
| Plus | $50/mo |
| Pro | $65/mo |

## Pros

- Feels like email to customers (no ticket numbers)
- Excellent knowledge base (Docs)
- Beacon widget for contextual help
- Simple and fast — minimal training needed

## Cons

- Limited automation compared to Zendesk
- No built-in phone or video support
- Reporting less advanced on lower plans

## Who Should Use Help Scout?
