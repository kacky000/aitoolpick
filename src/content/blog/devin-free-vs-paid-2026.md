If you're searching for a Devin free plan in 2026, here's the honest answer: there isn't one. Unlike most AI coding tools, Devin starts at the **$20 Core plan** with pay-as-you-go usage on top. This guide breaks down exactly what each Devin tier gets you, how the ACU billing actually works, and when paying more is worth it.

## Is There a Free Devin Plan?

No. Devin (by Cognition) is positioned as an autonomous AI engineer, not a casual chatbot, so there's no free tier and no perpetual trial. The lowest-cost way in is the **$20 Core plan**, which then bills usage per ACU. If you want to *try* autonomous coding for free first, look at tools with genuine free tiers in our [best Devin alternatives guide](/blog/best-devin-alternatives-2026/).

## What Core ($20) Gives You

Core is the entry point for individuals and small teams testing Devin on real work:

- **Pay-as-you-go ACUs at $2.25 each** — you fund usage as you go
- **Up to 10 concurrent sessions**
- Best for smaller features, bug fixes, and limited projects

An **ACU (Agentic Computing Unit)** ≈ 15 minutes of active autonomous work. So the $20 sticker is really an access fee — your true cost depends on how many ACUs your tasks consume. A quick bug fix might be a fraction of an ACU; a multi-file feature can be several.

## What You Unlock With Team ($500/month)

Team is for developers running Devin daily as a real teammate:

- **250 ACUs included** at the cheaper $2.00 rate
- **Unlimited concurrent sessions** (no 10-session cap)
- Early feature releases and research previews
- Optional onboarding call with the Cognition team

The unlimited sessions matter most — you can fan out many tasks in parallel, which is the whole point of an autonomous engineer. Compare this delegation model against an interactive IDE in our [Devin vs Cursor breakdown](/blog/devin-vs-cursor-2026/).

## Which Should You Pick?

- **Just want to try autonomous coding free?** Devin isn't it — start with a free-tier alternative.
- **Testing Devin on a few tasks?** Core at $20 plus pay-as-you-go ACUs.
- **Running Devin daily across many tickets?** Team at $500 for unlimited sessions and cheaper ACUs.
- **Large org with compliance needs?** Enterprise (custom quote).

## FAQ

**Is Devin free?** No — there's no free plan. The cheapest entry is the $20 Core tier with pay-as-you-go ACU billing.

**What does the $20 actually buy?** Access to Core with up to 10 concurrent sessions; ACU usage is billed separately at $2.25 each.

**How much is an ACU?** $2.25 on Core, $2.00 on Team. One ACU is roughly 15 minutes of active autonomous work.

**When is Team worth $500?** Once you need unlimited parallel sessions or regularly use close to 250 ACUs a month — the lower $2.00 rate then pays off.
