Runway is one of the most capable AI video generation platforms available, but its credit-based pricing can be hard to predict. How many credits does a 10-second Gen-3 Alpha clip actually cost? How fast do you burn through a monthly allocation? This guide breaks down every Runway plan and the real cost of using it in 2026.

## Runway Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Credits | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 125 (one-time) | Trying Runway out |
| **Standard** | $12/user/mo | 625/mo | Casual creators |
| **Pro** | $28/user/mo | 2,250/mo | Regular video production |
| **Unlimited** | $76/user/mo | Unlimited | Heavy daily usage |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Teams and organizations |

*Prices are for annual billing. Monthly billing costs approximately 20% more.*

## Free Plan — 125 Credits

Runway gives new accounts 125 credits to start. This is a one-time allocation — not monthly. It's enough to generate a handful of short clips and test the platform's capabilities, but not enough for any real project.

**What you get:**
- 125 credits (one-time, non-renewing)
- Access to Gen-3 Alpha and all AI tools
- 3 video projects
- 5GB of storage
- Export up to 720p
- Watermarked outputs

**How far 125 credits go:** With Gen-3 Alpha costing roughly 10 credits per second of video at standard resolution, you'll get about 12 seconds of generated footage. That's two or three clips.

## Standard — $12/user/month

The entry-level paid plan. Standard gives you 625 credits per month, removes watermarks, and bumps export quality to 1080p. For creators who generate a few clips per week, this works.

**What you get:**
- 625 credits/month
- No watermarks
- 1080p exports
- 15GB of storage
- All AI tools (Gen-3 Alpha, image generation, inpainting, etc.)
- 5 video projects

**How far 625 credits go:** Roughly 60 seconds of Gen-3 Alpha video at standard settings. That's about 6–12 clips depending on length. If you're generating daily, you'll likely run out mid-month.

## Pro — $28/user/month

The most popular plan for active creators. Pro gives you 2,250 credits per month — roughly 3.6x the Standard allocation. You also get higher resolution exports and more storage.

**What you get:**
- 2,250 credits/month
- Everything in Standard
- 4K exports (upscaled)
- 100GB of storage
- Unlimited video projects
- Priority generation queue

**How far 2,250 credits go:** Approximately 3.5 minutes of Gen-3 Alpha video. For most individual creators producing weekly content, this is sufficient. If you're generating multiple videos per day for client work, you'll still hit limits.

**Best for:** YouTubers, social media creators, and freelance video producers who use AI-generated clips as part of their workflow.

## Unlimited — $76/user/month

No credit cap. Unlimited removes the anxiety of watching your credit counter tick down. You get unrestricted access to all generation tools without worrying about monthly limits.

**What you get:**
- Unlimited credits
- Everything in Pro
- 500GB of storage
- Relaxed generation (standard speed during peak hours)
- All future model releases included

**Important caveat:** "Unlimited" comes with fair-use policies. Runway reserves the right to throttle generation speeds during peak usage. In practice, this means your generations may queue longer than on Pro during busy hours, but you won't be cut off.

**Best for:** Studios, agencies, and full-time video creators who generate content daily and don't want to think about credits.

## Enterprise — Custom Pricing

For teams and organizations. Enterprise adds collaboration features, dedicated support, and custom model training.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Unlimited
- Custom credit allocations
- Team management and shared workspaces
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom model fine-tuning
- SSO and enterprise security
- Priority support and SLA

**Best for:** Production studios, marketing agencies, and media companies with multiple editors.

## Gen-3 Alpha Credit Costs

This is where Runway pricing gets complicated. Different tools and settings consume credits at different rates.

### Video Generation (Gen-3 Alpha)

| Setting | Credits per Second |
|---------|-------------------|
| Standard quality (720p) | ~5 credits/sec |
| High quality (1080p) | ~10 credits/sec |
| Extended duration (10 sec) | Same per-second rate |
| Image-to-video | Same as text-to-video |

A typical 5-second Gen-3 Alpha clip at 1080p costs about 50 credits. A 10-second clip costs about 100 credits.

### Other Tools

Runway includes 30+ creative AI tools beyond video generation. Most of these — image generation, background removal, green screen, motion tracking — cost significantly fewer credits than video generation. If you mainly use these tools, the Standard plan goes much further.

### Resolution and Cost

Higher resolution means higher credit consumption. If you're on Standard or Pro, generating at 720p and upscaling externally can stretch your credits 2x compared to generating at 1080p natively.

## Hidden Costs and Gotchas

### 1. Credits Don't Roll Over
Unused monthly credits expire at the end of each billing cycle. There's no accumulation — use them or lose them.

### 2. Failed Generations Still Cost Credits
If Runway generates a clip you don't like, those credits are gone. You can retry, but each attempt costs the same. Budget for 2–3x the credits you think you'll need, because not every generation is usable.

### 3. Storage Limits Matter
Videos take up significant storage space. If you hit your storage cap, you'll need to delete older projects or upgrade.

### 4. Annual vs. Monthly
Monthly billing adds roughly 20% to the cost. The Unlimited plan jumps from $76/month (annual) to over $90/month (monthly). Commit annually if you're sure you'll stick with Runway.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Runway free?

Runway offers 125 one-time credits for free. After that, you need a paid plan. There's no ongoing free tier.

### How many videos can I make with Runway Pro?

With 2,250 credits on the Pro plan, you can generate approximately 22 clips at 10 seconds each in 1080p, or roughly 45 clips at 720p. Actual numbers depend on your resolution and generation settings.

### Is Runway Unlimited really unlimited?

Yes, in terms of credits — but Runway applies fair-use throttling during peak hours. You won't be cut off, but generation times may increase during busy periods.
