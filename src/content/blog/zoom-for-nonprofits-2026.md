**[Zoom](/tools/zoom/)** offers eligible nonprofits **50% off** select products through its Zoom Cares program. But the way you claim it changed in 2025 — the long-running TechSoup partnership ended, and nonprofits now apply directly through Zoom Cares. Here's how the discount works in 2026.

## The Big Change: TechSoup Is Out

Through early 2025, most nonprofits got Zoom discounts via TechSoup. **In May 2025 that partnership ended**, and Zoom for Nonprofits is no longer available on TechSoup. If you're working from an old guide that points to TechSoup, it's outdated.

Nonprofits now work **directly with Zoom Cares**, using **Goodstack** as the validation partner that confirms charitable status.

## What the Discount Includes

| Detail | Terms |
|--------|-------|
| Discount | **50% off** select products |
| Eligibility | Operating budget of **$10 million or less** |
| Billing | **Annual plans only** |
| Validation | Zoom Cares + Goodstack |

Qualifying products at 50% off include:

- **Zoom Workplace Pro** and **Zoom Workplace Business**
- **Large Meeting** add-on (up to 1,000 participants)
- **Zoom Webinar** (up to 5,000 attendees)

For reference on full list prices, see our [Zoom pricing guide](/blog/zoom-pricing-2026/): Pro normally runs about $13.33/user/month annually and Business about $18.33/user/month. A 50% cut brings Pro to roughly $6.67/user/month — meaningful savings for a webinar-heavy org.

## Cost Example: 10-Person Nonprofit

- **Zoom Workplace Pro (standard)**: 10 × $13.33 = ~$133/month
- **With 50% nonprofit discount**: ~$67/month
- **Annual savings**: roughly **$800/year** for a 10-seat team

The savings scale fastest for organizations running large webinars or town halls, where the Webinar and Large Meeting add-ons are the expensive line items.

## Eligibility Requirements

- Recognized **charitable/nonprofit status** in your country
- **Operating budget of $10 million or less**
- Validation completed through **Goodstack**

Larger nonprofits above the $10M threshold may still negotiate directly with Zoom sales, but the standard 50% Zoom Cares discount targets small and mid-size organizations.

## How to Apply

1. Go to the **Zoom Cares / Zoom for Nonprofits** page.
2. Submit your organization for **Goodstack validation**.
3. Once verified, apply the discount to an **annual** Workplace plan.

Note the annual-only requirement — the discount does not apply to month-to-month billing.

## Verdict

Zoom for Nonprofits is a clean **50% off** for organizations under $10M in budget, but two things trip people up in 2026: it's **annual-only**, and it's **no longer on TechSoup** — apply through Zoom Cares directly. If your nonprofit relies on webinars or large meetings, the discount on those add-ons is where the real value sits.
