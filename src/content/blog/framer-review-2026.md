# Framer Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Design-first website builder with stunning animations, CMS, and AI page generation for marketing sites. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the website builder space.

## What is Framer?

Design-first website builder with stunning animations, CMS, and AI page generation for marketing sites. Founded in 2014, it has established itself as a significant player in the website builder category. With a user rating of 4.6/5, it primarily serves Designers, Startups, Freelancers, Agencies, Portfolio Creators.

- **Visual Editor**
- **Animations**
- **Cms**

- **Responsive Design**

- **Custom Domains**

## Pricing

Framer offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Mini**: $5/site/month
- **Basic**: $15/site/month
- **Pro**: $30/site/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Beautiful scroll animations out-of-[box](/tools/box/)
