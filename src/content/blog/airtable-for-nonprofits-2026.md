## Who Qualifies?

Airtable verifies via the same nonprofit verification providers used across SaaS:

- Registered nonprofit / 501(c)(3) or international equivalent
- Mission-aligned (charitable, educational, religious, scientific)
- Not a government agency or political org
- Schools and hospitals usually qualify with proof

If you're listed in TechSoup or Goodstack, you're set.

## Three Templates Most Nonprofits Need

### 1. Donor CRM

A `Donors` table linked to a `Gifts` table. Custom fields for donor segment, last contact date, lifetime giving. Views: Major Donors (>$1,000), Lapsed (no gift in 12 months), Stewardship Due (last contact > 90 days). Automations email reminders to development staff.

### 2. Grant Pipeline

A `Grants` table with stages: Researching → LOI Submitted → Application → Awarded → Reporting. Kanban view by stage. Linked `Deliverables` table per grant. Calendar view for deadlines.

### 3. Program Participants

A `People` table linked to a `Programs` table via a `Enrollments` junction table (people ↔ programs). Outcome fields per enrollment. Interfaces give program staff a clean entry view without exposing raw tables.

## Where Airtable Beats Other Tools for Nonprofits

- **Linked records** model the real world (people ↔ orgs ↔ programs)
- **Interfaces** = build internal apps without code
- **Automations** replace 90% of what nonprofits use Zapier for
- **Sync** pulls data from Salesforce/HubSpot/Notion into one source of truth

## FAQ

**Is the Free plan enough for a small nonprofit?** Often yes — up to 1,000 records and 5 editors. Donor lists hit 1,000 fast though, so plan for Team at $10/user.

**Does the discount cover automation add-ons?** The 50% applies to base plans. AI features and add-on premium automations are typically full price.

**How fast is verification?** Usually 2–5 business days via Goodstack/Percent.

**Airtable or Salesforce NPSP?** Salesforce is deeper for fundraising (gift entry, batch giving, NPSP-specific reporting). Airtable is easier to learn and cheaper. Many small/mid orgs do fine on Airtable.
