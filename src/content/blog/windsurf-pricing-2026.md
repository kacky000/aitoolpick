Windsurf's pricing has changed significantly in 2026. The old credit system is gone, replaced by daily quotas that reset every 24 hours. If you're trying to figure out which plan is worth your money, here's the full breakdown.

## The Big Change: Credits to Quotas

In March 2026, Windsurf ditched its credit-based pricing and moved to a quota system. Instead of tracking a monthly credit balance that depletes with every interaction, you now get a fixed number of premium AI interactions per day. Once the day resets, your quota refills — no more watching a balance drain to zero mid-month.

This was a direct response to user complaints about unpredictable credit consumption. With daily quotas, you know exactly what you get each day.

Note: some older Windsurf documentation and blog posts may still reference the previous credit-based tiers ($20/$40/$200). The quota system described below is what's currently in effect.

## Windsurf Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Daily Quota | Best For |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Light quota | Trying Windsurf out |
| **Pro** | $15/mo | 50 premium interactions/day | Individual developers |
| **Teams** | $30/user/mo | 50+ interactions/day | Development teams |

## Free ($0)

The free tier gives you a light daily quota of AI interactions plus unlimited Tab completions. It's designed to let you test Windsurf's Cascade agent and see if the workflow fits before committing any money.

**What you get:**
- Light daily quota for AI interactions
- Unlimited Tab completions
- Access to basic models
- No credit card required
- Full IDE functionality (VS Code fork)

The free tier is genuinely usable for light coding sessions, but you'll hit the quota ceiling fast if you rely on Cascade for anything beyond quick questions.

## Pro ($15/month)

This is Windsurf's core paid plan and where most individual developers land.

**What you get:**
- 50 premium AI interactions per day (refreshes daily)
- Unlimited Tab completions
- Unlimited Command completions
- Access to all premium models: SWE-1, SWE-1.5, Claude Sonnet 4.6, GPT-5, Gemini 3.1 Pro
- Full Cascade agent capabilities
- Multi-file editing
- Terminal AI integration

The daily refresh is the key selling point. On Windsurf's old system (and on [Cursor's](/tools/cursor/) current credit model), heavy usage early in the month could leave you rationed for the rest. With 50 daily interactions, you get a consistent experience every day.

At $15/month, Pro undercuts [Cursor's $20/month Pro plan](/blog/cursor-pricing-2026/) while offering access to the same tier of frontier models.

## Teams ($30/user/month)

Built for organizations that need centralized management on top of Pro features.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Pro for each team member
- Centralized billing and seat management
- Admin controls
- Usage analytics across the team
- Priority support

Teams pricing is competitive with [GitHub Copilot Business at $19/user/month](/blog/github-copilot-pricing-2026/), though Copilot Business includes deeper GitHub ecosystem integration that Windsurf doesn't match.

## Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

For large companies with compliance and security requirements.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Teams
- Custom quotas and usage limits
- SSO and SCIM provisioning
- On-premise or VPC deployment options
- Dedicated account management
- SLA guarantees
- Audit logs and compliance controls

Pricing is negotiated based on team size and requirements. Expect to contact Windsurf sales directly.

## How Daily Quotas Work

The quota system is straightforward:

1. Each paid plan gives you a set number of premium AI interactions per day
2. An "interaction" is one Cascade flow, one inline edit, or one chat exchange using a premium model
3. Tab completions and Command completions are unlimited and don't count against your quota
4. Quotas reset every 24 hours (based on your account timezone)
5. Unused quota does not roll over to the next day
6. After hitting your daily limit, you can still use Tab completions and basic models

The 50-interaction daily cap on Pro is generous for most workflows. A typical developer doing focused coding with occasional Cascade assistance will rarely hit it. Power users who run multi-step Cascade flows throughout the day may bump up against the limit.

## Model Access

One of Windsurf's advantages is broad model access on the Pro tier. You're not locked into a single provider:

- **SWE-1 and SWE-1.5** — Windsurf's own coding-optimized models
- **Claude Sonnet 4.6** — Anthropic's latest, strong at reasoning and code generation
- **GPT-5** — OpenAI's flagship model
- **Gemini 3.1 Pro** — Google's multimodal model

You can switch models per interaction, which lets you pick the right tool for the job — use SWE-1.5 for quick edits, Claude Sonnet 4.6 for complex refactors, GPT-5 for broad reasoning tasks.

## Annual Billing

Windsurf offers annual billing at a discount. Expect roughly 15-20% savings when paying upfront, bringing the effective Pro cost closer to $12-13/month.

## Which Plan Should You Pick?

- **Exploring AI-assisted coding for the first time?** Start with Free
- **Solo developer, daily coding?** Pro at $15/month is the sweet spot
- **Team of 3+?** Teams for centralized billing and admin controls
- **Enterprise compliance needs?** Contact sales for custom pricing
