Intercom and Tidio both put live chat and AI bots on your website, but they serve very different businesses. Intercom is an enterprise-grade customer service platform with a powerful AI agent; Tidio is an affordable, fast-to-deploy chat and bot tool aimed at small businesses and e-commerce. Here's how **Intercom vs Tidio** compares in 2026.

## Quick Comparison

| | Intercom | Tidio |
|---|----------|-------|
| **Strength** | AI agent + full support suite | Affordable, simple setup |
| **Best for** | Scaling SaaS & support teams | SMBs, e-commerce stores |
| **AI bot** | Fin (advanced) | Lyro (solid, cheaper) |
| **Price** | Higher, scales with use | Budget-friendly |
| **Free tier** | Trial-based | Yes |

## Where Intercom Wins

Intercom is a complete customer service platform. Its Fin AI agent resolves a large share of tickets autonomously, and the surrounding suite — shared inbox, help center, ticketing, workflows — is built for teams that handle real support volume. If support is a core function and you're scaling, Intercom's depth justifies the price. See the [Intercom pricing details](/pricing/intercom/).

## Where Tidio Wins

Tidio wins on accessibility. It installs in minutes, the interface is approachable, and its Lyro AI bot handles common questions at a fraction of Intercom's cost. For small businesses and online stores that want live chat plus a capable bot without an enterprise budget, Tidio is the pragmatic choice. See the [Tidio pricing details](/pricing/tidio/).

## Pricing

This is the clearest dividing line. Tidio's plans stay affordable and predictable for SMB volumes, while Intercom's pricing — especially with Fin resolutions — climbs as you scale. Map your monthly conversation and resolution volume against each tool's metering before deciding; the gap is usually significant.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Choose Intercom** if support is core to your business and you're scaling a team.
- **Choose Tidio** if you're an SMB or store that wants capable chat on a budget.
