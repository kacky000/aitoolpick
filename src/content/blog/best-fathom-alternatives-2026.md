Fathom is a popular free AI notetaker, but it isn't the right fit for everyone. Some teams need deeper CRM automation, others want stronger search across hundreds of calls, and many want noise cancellation baked in. If you're shopping around, here are the **best Fathom alternatives in 2026** and who each one suits.

## Quick Comparison

| Tool | Best for | Standout feature |
|------|----------|------------------|
| **Fireflies** | Sales & CS teams | Search + CRM automation |
| **Otter** | Live note readers | Real-time transcript |
| **Krisp** | Noisy environments | Noise cancellation + notes |

## 1. Fireflies — Best for Sales and CRM Automation

If you've outgrown Fathom because you need to *act* on meetings later, Fireflies is the natural step up. It logs calls to your CRM automatically, surfaces talk-time and topic analytics, and makes every past conversation searchable. It's the strongest pick for revenue teams. See the [Fireflies pricing details](/pricing/fireflies-ai/).

## 2. Otter — Best Live Transcription

Otter shines in the moment. Its real-time transcript is fast and readable as the meeting unfolds, and its summaries and action items are clean. If you want to follow along live and share notes the second a call ends, Otter beats Fathom's after-the-fact model. Read the [Otter pricing breakdown](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026/).

## 3. Krisp — Best for Noisy Calls

Krisp started as best-in-class noise cancellation and added meeting notes on top. If your calls happen in cafes, open offices, or with patchy audio, Krisp cleans the audio *and* transcribes it — something a notes-only tool like Fathom can't do. Compare it in [Krisp vs Otter](/blog/krisp-vs-otter-2026/) or see [the best Krisp alternatives](/blog/best-krisp-alternatives-2026/).

## How to Choose

- **Need CRM sync and analytics?** → Fireflies
- **Want the best live transcript?** → Otter
- **Calls in noisy spaces?** → Krisp
- **Happy with free, simple notes?** → Stick with Fathom
