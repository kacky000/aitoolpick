# Heroku Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Heroku is platform-as-a-service for deploying, managing, and scaling applications with git-push deploys, managed databases, and a rich add-on marketplace. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Heroku?

Heroku is platform-as-a-service for deploying, managing, and scaling applications with git-push deploys, managed databases, and a rich add-on marketplace. It is designed for startups, prototyping, small teams and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the devops space.
