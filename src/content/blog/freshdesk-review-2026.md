# Freshdesk Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Customer support software with ticketing, self-service portal, and AI-powered automation by Freshworks. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the customer support space.

## What is Freshdesk?

Customer support software with ticketing, self-service portal, and AI-powered automation by Freshworks. Founded in 2010, it has established itself as a significant player in the customer support category. With a user rating of 4.4/5, it primarily serves Small Businesses, Growing Teams, E Commerce, Startups.

## Key Features

- **Ticketing**
- **Knowledge Base**
- **Ai Automation**
- **Sla Management**
- **Team Collaboration**
- **Multichannel**
- **Analytics**

## Pricing

Freshdesk offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Growth**: $15/agent/month
- **Pro**: $49/agent/month
- **Enterprise**: $79/agent/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Free tier with up to 2 agents
- Intuitive and easy to set up
- Freddy AI for ticket classification
- Gamification for support teams

### Cons

- Advanced features only on expensive plans
- Less customizable than [Zendesk](/tools/zendesk/)
- Reporting depth limited on lower tiers

## Who is Freshdesk Best For?

Freshdesk is ideal for Small Businesses, Growing Teams, E Commerce, Startups. If you fall into any of these categories, Freshdesk deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
