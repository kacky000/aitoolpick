**[QuickBooks Online](/tools/quickbooks/)** is the default small-business accounting tool in the US — and after Intuit's July 2025 price increase, it's also one of the more expensive. Here's exactly what each of the five 2026 plans costs, what you actually get, and where the hidden fees hide.

## QuickBooks Online Pricing in 2026

| Plan | Monthly cost | Users | Best for |
|------|-------------|-------|----------|
| Solopreneur | **$20/mo** | 1 | Sole proprietors, Schedule C filers |
| Simple Start | **$38/mo** | 1 | New businesses, basic bookkeeping |
| Essentials | **$75/mo** | 3 | Businesses paying bills + tracking time |
| Plus | **$115/mo** | 5 | Inventory and project profitability |
| Advanced | **$275/mo** | 25 | Scaling businesses needing analytics |

The higher rates above took effect with Intuit's July 2025 increase and apply to all 2026 renewals. New customers can usually take a **30-day free trial** or **up to 50% off for the first 3 months** — but not both, and the discount expires into the full rate.

## What Each Plan Actually Gets You

- **Solopreneur ($20)** — A stripped-down tier for one-person businesses that file a Schedule C. Income/expense tracking, basic invoicing, and tax estimates. No balance sheet.
- **Simple Start ($38)** — Full double-entry accounting for one user: invoicing, expense tracking, mileage, basic reports, and 1099 contractor management.
- **Essentials ($75)** — Adds bill management (accounts payable), time tracking, and multi-currency. Three users included.
- **Plus ($115)** — The most popular tier. Adds inventory tracking and project profitability — essential for product sellers and agencies billing by project.
- **Advanced ($275)** — Adds business analytics (powered by Fathom), batch invoicing, custom user roles, a dedicated account team, and 24/7 support.

## The Hidden Costs

The sticker price is rarely your real bill:

- **Payroll** — QuickBooks Payroll is a separate subscription, starting around $50/mo + $6 per employee.
- **Payments** — Card processing runs ~2.9% + $0.30 per online transaction.
- **Per-user limits** — Need a sixth user on Plus? You're pushed to Advanced at $275.
- **Add-ons** — Live Bookkeeping, QuickBooks Time, and apps from the marketplace all stack on top.

## Is QuickBooks Online Worth It?

### It's worth it if:

- Your accountant or bookkeeper already uses QuickBooks
- You need **inventory** or **project profitability** (Plus tier)
- You want the deepest app ecosystem and US tax integration
- You're scaling and need analytics + role-based access (Advanced)

### Look elsewhere if:

- You're a **freelancer** who mostly sends invoices — [FreshBooks](/blog/freshbooks-pricing-2026) is friendlier and cheaper
- You have a **tight budget** — [Wave](/blog/wave-pricing-2026) covers invoicing and accounting for $0
- You want **unlimited users** without jumping tiers — [Xero](/blog/xero-pricing-2026) includes them

## Tips to Lower Your QuickBooks Cost

1. **Take the 50% off, then reassess** — the discount lasts 3 months; downgrade or switch before the full rate hits.
2. **Don't over-buy tiers** — most small businesses never need Advanced. Stay on Plus unless you need 6+ users or analytics.
3. **Skip bundled payroll** if you have few employees — a standalone provider is often cheaper.
4. **Annual prepay** isn't offered the way it is with competitors, so model the monthly rate honestly.

## The Bottom Line

QuickBooks Online in 2026 is powerful but pricey, with most real businesses landing on **Simple Start ($38)** or **Plus ($115)**. You're paying for the ecosystem and accountant familiarity more than the software itself. If those matter to you, it's worth it. If you mostly invoice clients or want to spend nothing, [FreshBooks](/blog/freshbooks-pricing-2026) and [Wave](/blog/wave-pricing-2026) deserve a hard look first.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does QuickBooks Online cost in 2026?

Plans range from $20/mo (Solopreneur) to $275/mo (Advanced). The most common tiers are Simple Start at $38/mo and Plus at $115/mo, following Intuit's July 2025 price increase.

### Is there a free version of QuickBooks Online?

No. QuickBooks offers a 30-day free trial or up to 50% off for three months, but no permanent free plan. For free accounting, [Wave](/blog/wave-pricing-2026) is the closest alternative.

### What's the difference between Simple Start and Plus?

Simple Start ($38) covers core bookkeeping for one user. Plus ($115) adds inventory tracking, project profitability, and supports up to five users — the reason most growing businesses upgrade.
