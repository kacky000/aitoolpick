# Copy.ai Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

## What is Copy.ai?

AI-powered GTM platform for automating sales copy, [outreach](/tools/outreach-sales/) emails, and marketing workflows. Founded in 2020, it has established itself as a significant player in the ai writing category. With a user rating of 4.2/5, it primarily serves Sales Teams, Marketers, Agencies, Growth Teams.

## Pricing

Copy.ai offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Starter**: $49/month
- **Advanced**: $249/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Powerful workflow automation for GTM teams
- Great for sales email sequences
- Free tier with 2000 words/month
- Pre-built workflow templates

### Cons

- Expensive premium tiers
- Generic output without customization
