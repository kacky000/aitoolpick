ClickUp's free plan is one of the most feature-rich in project management. But there's a catch: the free tier comes with storage limits, restricted automations, and a few missing features that might push you to upgrade. Is the Unlimited plan at $7/month worth it?

Here's the real breakdown.

*Prices per user/month, billed annually.*

## What the Free Plan Gets Right

ClickUp Free Forever includes:

- **Unlimited tasks and members** — No artificial user caps
- **Multiple views** — List, Board, Calendar, and more
- **Docs** — Built-in document editor (unique among free PM tools)
- **Real-time collaboration** — Comments, mentions, chat
- **Integrations** — Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, and more
- **24/7 support** — Even on the free plan

For a free tool, this is remarkably complete. You get more features in ClickUp Free than in many paid plans of competitors.

## Where the Free Plan Falls Short

### 1. Storage: 100 MB Total
This is the biggest pain point. 100 MB for your entire workspace means you can upload maybe 20-30 files before running out. If your team shares screenshots, documents, or designs, you'll hit this in a week.

### 2. Automations: 100/Month
100 automations sounds fine until you realize that a single rule triggered 10 times = 10 automations used. Active teams burn through 100 in days.

### 3. No Goals
Goal tracking is locked behind the Unlimited plan. If OKRs or quarterly targets are part of your workflow, free won't cut it.

### 4. Limited Dashboards
Free gives you basic dashboards, but the customizable widgets that make dashboards actually useful require Unlimited.

### 5. Guest Limit (5)
Fine for internal teams, but if you work with clients or contractors, 5 guests is restrictive.

## When to Upgrade to Unlimited ($7/month)

The Unlimited plan solves every free-tier frustration:

- **Unlimited storage** — Upload everything
- **1,000 automations/month** — Enough for most small-medium teams
- **Goals and milestones** — Track OKRs and targets
- **Timeline view** — Gantt-style project planning
- **Unlimited dashboards** — Custom reporting
- **Unlimited guests** — Invite clients freely

At $7/user/month, this is one of the best deals in project management. Competing tools like [Asana](/tools/asana) charge $10.99+/user for similar features.

**Our recommendation:** If you have more than 2 team members, upgrade to Unlimited. The storage limit alone makes the free plan impractical for teams.

## When to Upgrade to Business ($12/month)

Business makes sense for:

- **Large teams (20+)** needing advanced permissions
- **Agencies** that need portfolios to manage multiple client projects
- **Teams running 1,000+ automations/month**
- **Organizations requiring SSO** or advanced security

Most small teams won't need Business. Unlimited covers 90% of use cases.

## ClickUp Brain (AI): Worth the $9 Add-On?

ClickUp Brain costs $9/user/month on top of any paid plan. It adds:

- AI-generated task summaries and updates
- Natural language project queries ("What's overdue this sprint?")
- AI writing assistant in Docs
- Automated standup reports

**Our take:** Useful for managers who want instant project status updates. But at $9/user, a team of 10 pays $90/month extra — consider whether [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt) at $20/mo flat would serve the same purpose.

## Bottom Line

ClickUp's free plan is the most feature-packed free PM tool available — but the 100 MB storage cap makes it impractical for real team use. At $7/user/month, the Unlimited plan is an excellent value and the sweet spot for most teams.

Skip Business unless you need portfolios, advanced permissions, or 10,000+ monthly automations.
