# Squarespace Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Squarespace is premium website builder known for stunning design templates, built-in e-commerce, and brand-focused aesthetics. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

Squarespace is premium website builder known for stunning design templates, built-in e-commerce, and brand-focused aesthetics. It is designed for creatives, photographers, artists and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the website builder space.

## Pricing

Paid plans start at $16/mo (monthly) and go up to $65/mo for premium features.

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Free | No |
| Personal | $16/mo |
| Business | $33/mo |
| Commerce Basic | $36/mo |
| Commerce Advanced | $65/mo |

## Pros

- Most beautiful templates in the industry
- All-in-one (domain, email, analytics)
- Great for portfolios and creative brands
- Excellent blogging platform

## Cons

- Less flexible than Webflow or WordPress
- No free tier (14-day trial only)
- Transaction fees unless on Commerce plans
