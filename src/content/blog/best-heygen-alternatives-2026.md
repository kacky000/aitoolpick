HeyGen is a powerful AI video platform, but its Creator plan at $24/month may not fit every workflow or budget. Whether you need cheaper avatar videos, better training features, or full video editing, these alternatives have you covered.

## 1. Synthesia

**Best for:** Enterprise-grade training and compliance videos

Synthesia is the market leader in AI avatar videos for corporate environments. With 230+ diverse avatars and 140+ language support, it's the go-to for multinational companies producing training content at scale.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Starter $22/mo, Creator $67/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Larger avatar library, stronger enterprise features
- **Trade-off:** More expensive Creator tier; less focus on marketing personalization

[Compare HeyGen vs Synthesia →](/compare/heygen-vs-synthesia)

## 2. D-ID

**Best for:** Lowest entry price for talking avatar videos

D-ID offers the cheapest way to create AI avatar videos. Upload any face photo, add your script, and get a talking-head video. The Lite plan at $5.90/month is ideal for testing the waters.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Lite $5.90/mo, Pro $29.99/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Significantly cheaper; photo-to-video is uniquely flexible
- **Trade-off:** Avatar realism doesn't match HeyGen's studio-quality output

## 3. Colossyan

**Best for:** L&D teams building interactive training

Colossyan goes beyond simple avatar videos with interactive scenarios, quizzes, and SCORM-compatible exports. If your primary use case is workplace learning, Colossyan is purpose-built for it.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Starter $28/mo, Pro $96/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Built-in scenario builder and LMS integration
- **Trade-off:** Higher Pro price; less suited for marketing content

## 4. Runway

**Best for:** Creative video generation beyond avatars

Runway's Gen-3 Alpha model generates cinematic video from text and images — not avatar talking-heads, but actual video footage. It's a different approach entirely, suited for creative professionals.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Standard $12/mo, Pro $28/mo, Unlimited $76/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Cinematic video generation, VFX capabilities
- **Trade-off:** No avatar/talking-head feature; creative tool, not corporate

[Read our Runway review →](/blog/runway-review-2026)

## 5. Fliki

**Best for:** Quick social media videos with voiceover

Fliki combines AI video generation, text-to-speech in 75+ languages, and stock footage into a streamlined creator tool. It's faster than HeyGen for creating short-form social content.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Standard $28/mo, Premium $88/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Simpler workflow for social media content
- **Trade-off:** Fewer avatar customization options

## 6. Pictory

**Best for:** Repurposing written content into video

Pictory automatically turns blog posts, articles, and scripts into engaging videos with stock footage and AI narration. It doesn't use avatars, but it's highly efficient for content marketing teams.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Starter $19/mo, Professional $39/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Better for text-to-video content repurposing
- **Trade-off:** No AI avatars — relies on stock footage and text overlays

## 7. Descript

**Best for:** Creators who need editing + AI in one tool

Descript combines a full video/audio editor with AI features like voice cloning and filler word removal. If you're editing existing footage and want AI enhancement rather than pure generation, Descript is the better fit.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Hobbyist $24/mo, Creator $33/mo, Business $40/mo
- **Why choose over HeyGen:** Full editing suite; transcript-based editing is unique
- **Trade-off:** Not designed for AI avatar video generation
