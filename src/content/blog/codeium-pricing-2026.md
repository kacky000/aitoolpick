Codeium is one of the best free AI coding tools available — with unlimited autocomplete at no cost. But how does it compare to GitHub Copilot's paid plans, and when does upgrading to Teams make sense?

## Codeium Pricing Plans 2026

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 (unlimited) | Individual developers |
| Teams | $12/user/month | Small to mid-size teams |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large organizations |

*Unlike GitHub Copilot, Codeium's free plan has no usage limits.*

## What the Free Plan Actually Includes

This is where Codeium stands out: the free plan is genuinely unlimited for individual developers.

**Free plan features:**
- Unlimited AI code completions
- 70+ programming language support
- VS Code, JetBrains, Neovim, Vim, Emacs, and more (15+ IDE integrations)
- Codeium Chat (ask questions about your code)
- Command (inline code editing from natural language)
- Search (semantic code search)
- No telemetry option

**Limitations of the free plan:**
- Individual use only (cannot share organization context)
- Standard model (not the most powerful available)
- No team admin controls
- No private deployment options

**Verdict on the free plan**: For solo developers, it's genuinely competitive with GitHub Copilot Pro ($10/month). The completions are fast and accurate for most everyday coding tasks.

## Codeium Teams Plan ($12/user/month)

The Teams plan adds organization-level features:

- Everything in Free
- Shared context across your organization's codebase
- Team admin dashboard and controls
- Usage analytics
- Priority support
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Centralized billing

**Is Teams worth $12/user/month?** At $12/user vs GitHub Copilot Business at $19/user, Codeium Teams is significantly cheaper. The organization context feature — where the AI understands your shared codebase — is the key value-add over the free plan.

## Codeium Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

Enterprise adds:
- On-premise deployment (self-hosted)
- Air-gapped deployment (fully disconnected from internet)
- Custom fine-tuning on your codebase
- Advanced security and compliance (SOC 2, GDPR)
- Dedicated support and SLA
- Audit logs

**When Enterprise makes sense**: Regulated industries (finance, healthcare, defense) where code cannot leave the company's infrastructure. Similar positioning to Tabnine Enterprise.

## Codeium vs GitHub Copilot: Price Comparison

| Plan | Codeium | GitHub Copilot |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Free | $0 (unlimited) | $0 (2,000 completions/month) |
| Individual | $0 | $10/month |
| Teams | $12/user | $19/user |
| Enterprise | Custom | $39/user |

**Key takeaway**: Codeium is dramatically cheaper at every tier. The free plans are where the biggest difference appears — Copilot's free tier is capped at 2,000 completions/month, while Codeium is truly unlimited.

## Is Codeium as Good as GitHub Copilot?

For everyday coding tasks (autocomplete, simple refactors), the quality is comparable. GitHub Copilot has a slight edge on:
- Newer API and framework suggestions
- Complex multi-line completions
- Training data recency

Codeium has an edge on:
- Price (free vs $10/month for individuals)
- IDE compatibility (broader support)
- Privacy controls

**For most individual developers**: Codeium free is the obvious choice over Copilot's free tier. The unlimited usage makes a significant difference in workflow.

**For teams**: Codeium Teams at $12/user vs Copilot Business at $19/user — Codeium saves 37% per user with comparable quality.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Codeium really free forever?**  
Yes — the individual plan has no usage caps and no planned end date for the free tier. Codeium's business model relies on Teams and Enterprise revenue.

**Does Codeium work in VS Code?**  
Yes — Codeium has a VS Code extension with 2M+ installs. It also works in JetBrains IDEs, Neovim, Vim, Emacs, and more.

**What data does Codeium collect?**  
By default, Codeium collects usage data. You can opt out of telemetry. For teams with strict requirements, the Enterprise on-premise option sends no data externally.

**Is Codeium safe for private code?**  
Standard plans send code context to Codeium's servers for processing. Enterprise on-premise keeps everything internal. Review their privacy policy if handling sensitive IP.
