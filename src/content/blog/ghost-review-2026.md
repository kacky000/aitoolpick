# Ghost Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Ghost is open-source publishing platform for professional blogs and newsletters with native memberships and email delivery. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Ghost?

Ghost is open-source publishing platform for professional blogs and newsletters with native memberships and email delivery. It is designed for professional bloggers, independent publishers, news sites and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the email marketing space.

## Pros

- Open-source and self-hostable (free)
- Native membership and paid subscription support
- Fast and SEO-friendly by default
- Clean writing experience without bloat

## Cons

- Themes require code knowledge to customize
- Smaller plugin ecosystem than WordPress
- Self-hosting requires technical maintenance
