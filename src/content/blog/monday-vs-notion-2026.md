# Monday.com vs Notion 2026: Which Workspace Wins?

Monday.com and Notion both want to be your team's home base, but they solve different problems. Monday is a visual work operating system for running projects; Notion is a flexible canvas for docs, wikis, and databases.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Monday.com** if you need to manage projects, pipelines, and team operations visually. Choose **Notion** if you need a flexible space for documentation, knowledge, and lightweight task tracking.

## Pricing Compared

| Plan | Monday.com | Notion |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Free | Up to 2 seats | Generous (personal) |
| Entry paid | $9/seat/mo (Basic) | $10/user/mo (Plus) |
| Mid tier | $12/seat/mo (Standard) | $20/user/mo (Business, AI included) |

Monday bills in seat tiers (minimum 3 seats). See the [Monday.com pricing breakdown](/blog/monday-pricing-2026) and [Notion pricing breakdown](/blog/notion-pricing-2026).

## Where Monday.com Wins

- **Operations**: Purpose-built for running projects, CRMs, and pipelines.
- **Visual dashboards**: Strong widgets and status views out of the box.
- **Automation**: Native no-code automations for repetitive work.

## Where Notion Wins

- **Docs and wikis**: Best-in-class writing and knowledge management.
- **Flexible databases**: Relational databases that adapt to anything.
- **AI**: Notion AI is built into notes and databases—see [how to use Notion AI](/blog/how-to-use-notion-ai-2026).

## Head-to-Head

| Factor | Monday.com | Notion |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Project ops | Excellent | Good |
| Docs/knowledge | Basic | Excellent |
| Free plan | 2 seats | Generous |
| Best for | Running work | Documenting work |

## Who Should Choose What?

**Choose Monday.com if you:**
- Run structured projects and pipelines
- Want visual dashboards and automations
- Need an operations-first platform

**Choose Notion if you:**
- Center your team on docs and wikis
- Want flexible databases and AI writing
- Need light task tracking, not heavy PM

If task depth matters more than docs, also compare [ClickUp vs Notion](/blog/clickup-vs-notion-2026).

## The Bottom Line

**Monday.com** is the operations-first work platform; **Notion** is the docs-and-knowledge canvas with light tasks. Pick Monday to run projects, Notion to document and organize them.
