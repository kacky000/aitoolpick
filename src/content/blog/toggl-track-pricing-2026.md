[Toggl Track](/tools/toggl-track/) is one of the most popular time tracking tools available, known for its polished interface and powerful reporting. But understanding which plan you actually need can be confusing. This guide breaks down every tier so you can make an informed decision without overpaying.

## Toggl Track Pricing Overview 2026

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Users |
|------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Up to 5 |
| Starter | $9/user/mo | $9/user/mo | Unlimited |
| Premium | $18/user/mo | $18/user/mo | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

All paid plans are billed per user. Toggl offers the same price whether you pay monthly or annually — a rarity in SaaS pricing.

## Free Plan ($0/month)

**Best for**: Solo users and tiny teams testing the platform.

**Includes**:
- Up to 5 team members
- Unlimited time tracking
- Unlimited projects and clients
- Basic reports (summary, detailed, weekly)
- CSV export
- Pomodoro timer
- Calendar integration
- Browser extensions and apps
- Idle detection

**Limitations**:
- No billable rates
- No project estimates or budgets
- No saved reports
- No team groups or permissions
- 5-user maximum

**Verdict**: The free plan is a capable time tracker for individuals or micro-teams. You can track time, organize by project, and generate basic reports. If you don't need billable rate calculations or team management, this handles the fundamentals well.

## Starter Plan ($9/user/month)

**Best for**: Freelancers and small agencies billing by the hour.

**Everything in Free, plus**:
- Unlimited team members
- Billable rates (per user, project, or workspace)
- Project time estimates with alerts
- Fixed fee projects
- Saved reports
- Time rounding
- Project templates
- Rich project dashboard
- Export to Excel

**Key upgrade triggers**:
- You need to calculate billable amounts
- Your team exceeds 5 people
- You want project budget tracking
- You need saved/scheduled reports

**Value assessment**: At $9/user/month, Starter is competitively priced for what it offers. The billable rates feature alone justifies the cost for anyone billing clients. Compare to [Clockify](/tools/clockify/) Basic at $3.99 (cheaper but less polished) or [Harvest](/tools/harvest/) Pro at $10.80 (similar price, includes invoicing).

## Premium Plan ($18/user/month)

**Best for**: Growing agencies and teams that need profitability insights.

**Everything in Starter, plus**:
- Fixed fee projects
- Time audits (flag suspicious entries)
- Insights panel (profitability per project/client)
- Project forecasting
- Required fields enforcement
- Scheduled reports via email
- Priority support
- Multiple workspace management

**Key upgrade triggers**:
- You manage many projects and need profitability visibility
- You want automatic time audit alerts
- You need forecasting to plan resources
- Your team requires stricter time entry standards

**Value assessment**: Premium doubles the cost of Starter but adds meaningful business intelligence. The Insights feature — showing project profitability, team utilization, and forecasted overruns — provides data that can easily save thousands in prevented scope creep. Worth it for agencies billing $100K+/year.

## Enterprise Plan (Custom pricing)

**Best for**: Large organizations with compliance and security requirements.

**Everything in Premium, plus**:
- SSO (SAML)
- Data residency options
- Custom onboarding
- Dedicated success manager
- Priority support with SLA
- Audit log
- Advanced user provisioning

Contact Toggl's sales team for pricing — typically negotiated based on team size and requirements.

Toggl Track sits in the middle — more expensive than Clockify but cheaper than Harvest. The trade-off is UX quality (Toggl wins) vs feature inclusion (Clockify includes scheduling, Harvest includes invoicing).

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

**Choose Free if**: You're a solo user or team under 5 who just needs to log hours without billing calculations.

**Choose Starter if**: You bill clients and need rate calculations, project estimates, and professional reporting. This covers 80% of freelancer and small agency needs.

**Choose Premium if**: You manage a team of 10+ and need profitability insights, time audits, and forecasting to make informed business decisions.

**Choose Enterprise if**: You have 50+ users with SSO requirements, compliance needs, or want dedicated support.

## Tips to Save on Toggl Track

1. **Start free**: The free plan is legitimately useful. Don't upgrade until you hit the 5-user limit or need billable rates.
2. **Skip Premium initially**: Most teams can thrive on Starter for a year before needing Insights.
3. **Consider alternatives**: If budget is tight, [Clockify](/tools/clockify/) offers similar core features at half the price.
4. **Check non-profit discounts**: Toggl offers discounts for qualifying non-profits and educational institutions.

## Final Thoughts

Toggl Track's pricing is fair for the experience it delivers. You're paying a premium over [Clockify](/tools/clockify/) for better design, smoother UX, and superior reporting. Whether that premium is worth it depends on how much you value polish versus pure functionality. For most freelancers and agencies, the Starter plan at $9/user/month hits the sweet spot.
