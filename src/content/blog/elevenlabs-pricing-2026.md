# ElevenLabs Pricing 2026: Complete Breakdown

ElevenLabs has become the gold standard for AI voice generation — but its credit-based pricing can be confusing. This guide breaks down every plan, what you actually get, and which tier makes sense for different use cases.

## ElevenLabs Pricing Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Characters/Month |
|------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 10,000 |
| **Starter** | $5/mo | $4.16/mo | 30,000 |
| **Creator** | $22/mo | $18.33/mo | 100,000 |
| **Pro** | $99/mo | $82.50/mo | 500,000 |
| **Scale** | $299/mo | $249/mo | 2,000,000 |
| **Business** | Custom | Custom | Custom |

*Annual billing saves approximately 17% across all paid plans.*

## What Are "Characters"?

ElevenLabs charges by character count — each letter, number, space, and punctuation mark in your text counts. Some quick reference points:

- **10,000 characters** ≈ 5–7 minutes of audio (Free plan)
- **30,000 characters** ≈ 15–20 minutes of audio (Starter)
- **100,000 characters** ≈ 50–60 minutes of audio (Creator)
- **500,000 characters** ≈ 4–5 hours of audio (Pro)
- **2,000,000 characters** ≈ 16–20 hours of audio (Scale)

The variation in audio length depends on speech speed (words per minute). Faster speakers generate more audio per character.

## Plan-by-Plan Breakdown

### Free Plan — $0/month

The free tier gives you 10,000 characters per month, which is enough to test the product but not much for real production use. You get access to standard voices and can create up to 3 custom voices.

**Best for:** Testing ElevenLabs before committing to a paid plan.

**Limitations:**
- No commercial license (cannot use outputs in commercial projects)
- Watermarked API output
- Limited voice library access
- No voice cloning (Instant Voice Cloning requires Starter+)

### Starter Plan — $5/month

At $5/month, this is one of the most affordable premium AI voice plans available. You get 30,000 characters, commercial usage rights, and access to Instant Voice Cloning. This is enough for a short YouTube video series, a weekly podcast episode, or regular social media voiceovers.

**Best for:** Individual creators monetizing content on a budget.

**Unlocks:**
- Commercial license
- Instant Voice Cloning (up to 10 custom voices)
- API access at commercial rates
- All stock voices

### Creator Plan — $22/month

The Creator plan is the sweet spot for most active content creators. 100,000 characters covers roughly 50–60 minutes of audio per month — enough for podcast production, multiple YouTube videos, or a short audiobook chapter.

**Best for:** YouTubers, podcasters, and content creators with regular output.

**Unlocks:**
- Up to 30 custom voices
- Higher quality audio generation
- Priority queue for faster generation
- Access to all models including Turbo (low-latency)

### Pro Plan — $99/month

At 500,000 characters, the Pro plan is built for volume. Publishers, agencies, and e-learning companies producing multiple hours of audio per month will find this the right tier. Professional Voice Cloning becomes available here — producing results indistinguishable from a human speaker.

**Best for:** Studios, publishers, agencies, and high-volume creators.

**Unlocks:**
- Professional Voice Cloning (PVC) — requires 30+ min sample
- Up to 160 custom voices
- Usage analytics dashboard
- 44kHz audio output (studio quality)

### Scale Plan — $299/month

The Scale plan targets businesses running voice generation at scale — SaaS products with TTS features, enterprise e-learning platforms, or media companies producing hundreds of hours of content monthly.

**Best for:** B2B SaaS, enterprise media, high-volume API consumers.

**Unlocks:**
- Up to 660 custom voices
- Highest priority queue
- Custom invoicing options
- Dedicated support

## Is ElevenLabs Pricing Worth It?

**Compared to hiring voice actors:** A professional voice actor typically charges $150–$500 per finished hour. ElevenLabs' Creator plan ($22/month) gives you roughly 1 hour of audio — dramatically cheaper, though without the creative nuance of a human performance.

**Compared to Amazon Polly:** Amazon Polly charges $4 per million characters for standard voices and $16 per million for neural voices. ElevenLabs Creator plan (~$220 per million characters) is far more expensive but produces substantially better quality.

**Compared to Play.ht and Murf:** Competing tools like Play.ht (~$31/mo for 500k chars) and Murf (~$29/mo for 100k chars) are slightly cheaper per character but generally rated lower on output naturalness.

## How to Choose the Right ElevenLabs Plan

| If you need this... | Choose this plan |

| Just testing | Free |
| Short voiceovers, testing commercial use | Starter |
| Regular YouTube/podcast/social content | Creator |
| Audiobook production or agency work | Pro |
| SaaS product with TTS or high-volume production | Scale |

## ElevenLabs API Pricing

For developers, ElevenLabs charges per character on API calls at the same rate as the plan's monthly allocation. Overage charges apply when you exceed your plan limit:

- Starter: $0.30 per 1,000 extra characters
- Creator: $0.24 per 1,000 extra characters
- Pro: $0.18 per 1,000 extra characters

## Verdict

For most individual creators, the **Creator plan at $22/month** offers the best value — enough output, voice cloning, and commercial rights for serious content production.

Businesses integrating voice generation into products should evaluate the **Pro or Scale plans** based on monthly character volume.
