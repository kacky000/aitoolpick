Surfshark and NordVPN are both owned by Nord Security, yet they compete directly. Surfshark undercuts NordVPN on price and offers unlimited devices. NordVPN fights back with faster speeds and more features. Which one actually deserves your money?

| Feature | Surfshark | NordVPN |

| Cheapest Plan | $2.49/mo (2yr Starter) | $3.49/mo (2yr Basic) |
| Simultaneous Devices | **Unlimited** | 10 |
| Servers | 3,200+ in 100 countries | 6,000+ in 60+ countries |
| Free Plan | No | No |
| Speed (nearby) | 350-500 Mbps | 550-700 Mbps |
| Ad Blocker | CleanWeb | Threat Protection |
| Jurisdiction | Netherlands | Panama |
| Antivirus | One plan | Not included |

## Pricing Breakdown

| Tier | Surfshark | NordVPN |
|------|----------|---------|

## Unlimited Devices — Surfshark's Killer Feature

The single biggest differentiator: **Surfshark lets you connect unlimited devices simultaneously**. NordVPN caps at 10.

For individuals, 10 devices is usually enough. But for families? A household with two adults and two teens, each with a phone and laptop, already hits 8 devices — before adding tablets, smart TVs, and gaming consoles.

Surfshark's unlimited policy means one $2.49/month subscription covers your entire household. With NordVPN, larger families may need a router-level setup or a second subscription.

## Speed: NordVPN Wins

NordVPN is consistently faster thanks to its proprietary NordLynx protocol:

| Test | Surfshark | NordVPN |
|------|----------|---------|
| Nearby server | 400 Mbps avg | 600 Mbps avg |
| Cross-continent | 180 Mbps avg | 280 Mbps avg |
| Latency impact | +15-25ms | +10-15ms |

For everyday browsing and streaming, both are more than fast enough. The gap matters mainly for:
- Competitive online gaming (lower latency)
- Large file downloads/torrenting (higher throughput)
- Connections from slower base ISP speeds

NordVPN also has nearly double the server count (6,000+ vs 3,200+), which means less congestion and more server options per country.

## Security Comparison

Both VPNs use AES-256 encryption and WireGuard-based protocols. The differences:

| Security Feature | Surfshark | NordVPN |
|-----------------|----------|---------|
| No-logs audit | Deloitte | Deloitte (3x) |
| RAM-only servers | ✅ | ✅ |
| Kill switch | ✅ | ✅ |
| Multi-hop | MultiHop (2 servers) | Double VPN (2 servers) |
| Threat blocking | CleanWeb (DNS-level) | Threat Protection Pro (file scanning) |
| Meshnet | ❌ | ✅ |
| Dedicated IP | ❌ | ✅ (add-on) |

NordVPN's **Threat Protection Pro** is more advanced than Surfshark's CleanWeb. It scans downloaded files for malware and blocks phishing URLs — CleanWeb only blocks at the DNS level. NordVPN has also completed three separate no-logs audits vs Surfshark's one.

NordVPN's **Meshnet** feature lets you create encrypted private networks between your devices — useful for remote access, LAN gaming, and file sharing.

## Streaming

Both VPNs handle streaming well:

| Service | Surfshark | NordVPN |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Netflix US | ✅ | ✅ |
| Disney+ | ✅ | ✅ |
| BBC iPlayer | ✅ | ✅ |
| Hulu | ✅ | ✅ |
| Smart DNS | ❌ | ✅ |

NordVPN's Smart DNS feature lets you unblock streaming on devices that can't run VPN apps (older smart TVs, consoles). Surfshark lacks this, requiring router-level VPN setup for those devices.

## The Same Parent Company — Does It Matter?

Since 2022, Surfshark and NordVPN operate under Nord Security. Both brands maintain separate infrastructure, apps, and teams. But the shared ownership means:

- Both offer Incogni (data removal) as a premium add-on
- Technology sharing likely happens behind the scenes
- Neither is truly an "independent alternative" to the other

If corporate independence matters to you, consider Proton VPN or Mullvad instead.

## Who Should Choose Surfshark?

- **Budget-first buyers** — $2.49/mo is hard to beat
- **Large families** — unlimited devices on one plan
- **Users who want VPN + antivirus** bundled (One plan)
- **Anyone** who doesn't need the absolute fastest speeds

## Who Should Choose NordVPN?

- **Speed-sensitive users** — gaming, torrenting, or slow ISP connections
- **Streamers** who need Smart DNS support
- **Security-focused users** wanting advanced threat protection
- **Power users** who value Meshnet and dedicated IP options

## Our Bottom Line

**Surfshark is better value.** For $1/month less, you get unlimited devices and competent VPN protection. The speed gap exists but doesn't matter for most use cases.

**NordVPN is the better VPN.** Faster, more servers, better security features, triple-audited. If you can afford the extra $1/month, NordVPN delivers a premium experience.

The honest answer: both are owned by the same company and both are excellent. Pick Surfshark to save money. Pick NordVPN for the best possible experience.
