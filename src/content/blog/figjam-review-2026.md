## What is FigJam?

FigJam is collaborative whiteboard by figma for brainstorming, diagramming, and team workshops with sticky notes, stamps, drawing tools, and integrations with figma design files. It is designed for design teams, product teams, workshop facilitators and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the whiteboard space.
