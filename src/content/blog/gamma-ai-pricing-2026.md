**Gamma AI pricing** in 2026 uses a credit-based system on the free plan and unlimited generation on paid tiers. The free plan gives you enough to evaluate the product. The Plus plan at $8/month is where Gamma becomes a daily-driver tool for anyone who builds decks regularly.

## Gamma Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Credits | Key Features |
|------|-------|---------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 400 credits | Unlimited viewers, basic export |
| Plus | $8/month | Unlimited AI | Custom domain, analytics, no watermark |
| Pro | $15/month | Unlimited AI | Priority AI, advanced analytics, custom fonts |

All paid prices are per user per month, billed annually. Monthly billing is approximately 20% higher.

## Free Plan: What 400 Credits Gets You

Gamma's free tier is genuinely generous for trying the product:

- **400 AI credits**: Each complete presentation generation costs roughly 40-100 credits. You can generate 4-8 full presentations before running out.
- **Unlimited viewers**: Anyone you share a Gamma link with can view it — no account required, no viewer limits.
- **Unlimited presentations**: You can keep creating and editing manually after credits run out, just not AI generation.
- **PDF export**: Download any presentation as a PDF.
- **Gamma watermark**: Free presentations have a small Gamma watermark visible in sharing.
- **Basic themes**: Access to core theme library.

**What the free plan lacks:**
- Continuous AI generation (400 credits depletes)
- Custom domain (e.g., your branding on the URL)
- Audience analytics (who viewed, how long on each slide)
- Priority AI access (slower generation during peak times)
- Custom fonts

The free plan is ideal for occasional use — generating a presentation for a one-time [pitch](/tools/pitch/) or testing whether Gamma fits your workflow before committing.

## Plus Plan: $8/month (Annual) — Most Popular

Plus removes the credit ceiling and adds features that matter for regular use:

- **Unlimited AI generation**: No credit counting, no hesitation about regenerating sections
- **No Gamma watermark**: Clean sharing URL without Gamma branding
- **Custom domain**: Host your presentation at your own subdomain
- **Audience analytics**: See view counts, time-on-slide data, and unique viewer statistics
- **All themes and templates**: Full access to the theme library
- **Priority support**

At $8/month, Plus pays for itself quickly if you're generating more than 4-5 presentations per month. The unlimited AI removes the psychological friction of wondering whether to use a credit on an edit.

**Who should get Plus:**
- [Marketers](/best-for/marketers/) who build decks regularly for internal and external audiences
- Consultants who create client presentations
- Founders who iterate on [pitch](/tools/pitch/) decks frequently
- Educators building course materials

## Pro Plan: $15/month (Annual)

Pro adds features for power users and team use:

- Everything in Plus
- **Priority AI access**: Faster generation during high-demand periods (though Plus generation is already fast in practice)
- **Advanced analytics**: More detailed engagement data
- **Custom fonts**: Upload and use your brand's own typefaces
- **Earlier access to new features**
- **Priority support queue**

The Pro plan is primarily valuable for agencies and sales teams who need custom fonts to maintain brand standards, and for power users who want the fastest possible AI generation at peak times.

At $15/month, Pro is worth it if custom fonts are a requirement (brand consistency for client-facing work) or if you're generating 20+ presentations per month and want guaranteed speed.

## Credit System Explained

On the free plan, different actions consume different numbers of credits:

| Action | Approximate Credits |
|--------|---------------------|
| Generate full presentation (text prompt) | 40-100 |
| Generate from outline | 30-60 |
| Regenerate single card | 5-15 |
| AI rewrite of text block | 2-5 |
| Create from imported document | 40-80 |

Credits don't expire, but they only refresh if you upgrade to a paid plan — the free plan gives you 400 credits once, not per month.

## Annual vs Monthly Billing

| Plan | Annual | Monthly | Annual Savings |
|------|--------|---------|---------------|
| Plus | $8/month ($96/year) | ~$10/month | ~$24/year |
| Pro | $15/month ($180/year) | ~$18/month | ~$36/year |

Annual billing saves 20% for both plans. The savings are modest in absolute terms — a cup of coffee per month — but annual commitment makes sense if you know you'll use Gamma consistently.

## Is Gamma Worth Paying For?

**Stick with free if:** You build presentations occasionally (a few per year) and don't mind regenerating from your credit pool or finishing editing manually after generation.

**Get Plus at $8/month if:** You build presentations more than once or twice a month. The unlimited generation removes friction, and the analytics tell you which [slides](/tools/slides-com/) your audience actually reads.

**Get Pro at $15/month if:** You work for a brand with specific typography requirements, or you're building presentations professionally for multiple clients.

## Bottom Line

Gamma's free plan is one of the more honest free tiers in AI tools — 400 credits is enough to evaluate the product properly and build several real presentations. The Plus plan at $8/month is exceptional value for anyone who builds decks regularly.
