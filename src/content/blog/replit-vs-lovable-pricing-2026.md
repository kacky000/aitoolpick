# Replit vs Lovable Pricing 2026: $20 Credits vs $25 Credits, Very Different Ceilings

Replit and Lovable both sell the same dream — type a description, get a working app — and both meter their AI with credits. Their entry plans sit just $5 apart: Replit Core at $20/month, Lovable Starter at $25/month. But the two define "a credit" very differently, scale in opposite directions, and target different builders. Here's how the pricing actually compares in 2026.

## The Plans Side by Side

| | Replit | Lovable |
|---|--------|---------|
| **Free** | Starter — limited daily Agent credits, 1,200 min/mo dev time | Free — 5 credits/day (~30/mo), public projects only |
| **Entry paid** | Core — $20/mo, $20 usage credits | Starter — $25/mo, 100 credits, private projects |
| **Mid tier** | Pro — $100/mo flat, up to 15 builders | Pro — $50/mo, 400 credits |
| **Top tier** | Enterprise | Scale — 800 credits |
| **Credit model** | Usage-based ($ of AI interactions) | Discrete credits (per AI interaction) |

## The Free Tiers

**Replit Starter** offers limited daily Agent credits that reset every 24 hours, plus 1,200 minutes of monthly dev time. It's a full environment — you can write code, run projects, and try Agent — but the daily cap stops you finishing anything substantial.

**Lovable Free** gives 5 credits per day, roughly 30 per month if you use it daily. Critically, all projects are **public** — your code is visible to everyone. A single complex prompt can eat more than one credit, so you may get only 3–4 meaningful iterations a day. Unused daily credits don't accumulate.

Verdict on free: both are evaluation-only. Lovable's public-only restriction is the bigger limitation if you're building anything you don't want exposed.

## Entry Paid: Core ($20) vs Starter ($25)

**Replit Core — $20/month.** Full Replit Agent access, $20 of usage credits included, and autonomous long-running builds that scaffold, install, debug, and deploy while you're away. Credits are usage-based — once the $20 allowance is spent, Agent pauses but you can still code manually. Core was dropped from $25 to $20 in late 2025, matching [Cursor Pro](/blog/cursor-pricing-2026/).

**Lovable Starter — $25/month.** The jump from Free's ~30 monthly credits to 100 is significant, and private projects unlock. 100 credits translates to roughly 80–100 meaningful AI interactions per month — usually enough to take a single SaaS prototype (landing page, auth, database, a few features) from idea to deployed MVP.

The difference: Replit meters in dollars of usage, Lovable in discrete credits. Replit gives you a full IDE and hosting platform; Lovable is more tightly focused on the prompt-to-app flow. For $5 less, Replit Core also gives you the broader development environment — but if your work is purely "describe app, get app," Lovable's credit model is easier to reason about.

## Mid and Top Tiers: Opposite Scaling

This is where the two diverge sharply.

**Replit Pro — $100/month flat** covers up to 15 builders. Per-user cost drops under $7/month, with credit rollover added. It's built for teams.

**Lovable Pro — $50/month** stays single-user but quadruples credits to 400 — built for an individual who builds regularly. **Lovable Scale** pushes to 800 credits for agencies and power users.

So the tiers answer different questions. Replit scales by **headcount** (cheap seats for a team). Lovable scales by **volume** (more credits for one heavy builder). If you're a solo power-builder, Lovable Pro at $50 for 400 credits is a focused upgrade. If you're a small team, Replit Pro at $100 flat for 15 people is unbeatable per-seat.

## Which Should You Pick?

- **Solo builder wanting a full IDE + hosting at the lowest price?** Replit Core at $20/mo.
- **Solo builder who lives in prompt-to-app and wants predictable credit counts?** Lovable Starter $25/mo, upgrading to Pro $50 when 100 credits run short.
- **Heavy individual builder?** Lovable Pro ($50, 400 credits) or Scale (800).
- **Team of 3–15?** Replit Pro at $100 flat wins on per-seat cost.
- **Privacy matters and you're testing?** Skip Lovable Free (public only) — start with Replit Starter or pay for Lovable Starter to unlock private projects.

## The Bottom Line

Both meter with credits, but they scale in opposite directions. Replit gets cheaper as you add people; Lovable gets richer as one person builds more. Pick Replit if you want a full environment or a team plan; pick Lovable if you're a focused solo builder who wants the prompt-to-app flow and clear credit budgeting — just remember its free tier makes everything public.
