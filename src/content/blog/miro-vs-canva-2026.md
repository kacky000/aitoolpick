# Miro vs Canva: Which Visual Tool Is Right for You?

[Miro](/tools/miro) and [Canva](/tools/canva) are both visual tools, but they solve different problems. Miro is a collaborative whiteboard for brainstorming and planning. Canva is a design tool for creating polished graphics and presentations.

Here's when to use each — and when you might need both.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Miro | Canva |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Primary use** | Whiteboarding & collaboration | Graphic design & content creation |
| **Best for** | Brainstorming, workshops, planning | Social media, presentations, marketing |
| **Free plan** | 3 boards | Generous (many templates) |
| **Starting price** | $8/user/mo | $15/user/mo (Pro) |
| **Collaboration** | Real-time, excellent | Real-time, good |
| **Templates** | Workshops, diagrams, frameworks | Designs, social posts, presentations |
| **Learning curve** | Medium | Low |

## When to Choose Miro

### 1. Team Brainstorming and Workshops
Miro's infinite canvas is built for collaborative thinking. Sticky notes, voting, timers, and facilitation tools make remote workshops feel productive.

### 2. Process Mapping and Diagrams
Flowcharts, user journey maps, system architecture diagrams — Miro has purpose-built shapes and connectors that Canva can't match.

### 3. Agile and Sprint Planning
Miro integrates with [Jira](/tools/linear), Asana, and other PM tools. Use it for sprint retrospectives, story mapping, and roadmap planning.

### 4. Research and UX Design
Affinity diagrams, persona boards, and usability test synthesis all work naturally on Miro's canvas.

## When to Choose Canva

### 1. Social Media Content
Canva's template library is unmatched for Instagram posts, LinkedIn carousels, TikTok thumbnails, and Pinterest pins. Resize any design for any platform in one click.

### 2. Presentations
Canva's presentation templates are more polished than Miro's. If your audience is external (clients, investors), Canva produces more professional results.

### 3. Marketing Materials
Flyers, business cards, email headers, banner ads — Canva handles all of these with drag-and-drop simplicity.

### 4. Brand Kit Management
Canva Pro lets you save brand colors, fonts, and logos. Apply them consistently across every design your team creates.

## Pricing Comparison

| Plan | Miro | Canva |
|------|------|-------|
| Free | 3 boards, unlimited members | 250,000+ templates, 5GB storage |
| Starter/Pro | $8/user/mo | $15/user/mo |
| Business | $16/user/mo | $20/user/mo (Teams) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

**Verdict:** Miro is cheaper per user, but Canva's free tier is more generous for individual use.

## Feature Deep Dive

### Collaboration
Both tools support real-time collaboration, but the experience differs:
- **Miro:** Cursor tracking, voting, timer, presentation mode, video chat — optimized for *working together* in real-time
- **Canva:** Comments, shared folders, brand kit — optimized for *creating together* asynchronously

### Templates
- **Miro:** 2,500+ templates focused on workshops, agile, strategy, and diagramming
- **Canva:** 250,000+ templates focused on design, marketing, and content creation

### AI Features
- **Miro AI:** Cluster sticky notes, generate diagrams, summarize boards
- **Canva AI:** Magic Design (generate designs from prompts), Magic Write, background remover, image generation

### Integrations
- **Miro:** Slack, Jira, Asana, [Notion](/tools/notion), Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams
- **Canva:** Google Drive, Dropbox, social media platforms, Mailchimp, HubSpot

## Can You Use Both?

Yes — and many teams do. A common setup:

1. **Miro** for internal work: brainstorming, planning, workshops
2. **Canva** for external output: social media, client presentations, marketing

They complement each other rather than compete.

## Verdict

| Your Need | Choose |
|-----------|--------|
| Brainstorming & workshops | [Miro](/tools/miro) |
| Social media graphics | [Canva](/tools/canva) |
| Process diagrams | Miro |
| Presentations for clients | Canva |
| UX research synthesis | Miro |
| Marketing materials | Canva |
| Both internal planning + external content | Use both |

**Bottom line:** If you're choosing just one, ask: "Am I primarily *thinking together* or *creating polished output*?" Miro is for thinking. Canva is for creating.
