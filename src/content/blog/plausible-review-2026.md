# Plausible Analytics Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Privacy-friendly, lightweight Google Analytics alternative that doesn't use cookies or track personal data. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the analytics space.

## What is Plausible Analytics?

Privacy-friendly, lightweight Google Analytics alternative that doesn't use cookies or track personal data. Founded in 2019, it has established itself as a significant player in the analytics category. With a user rating of 4.6/5, it primarily serves Privacy Conscious Businesses, Bloggers, Developers, Eu Businesses.

## Key Features

- **Pageviews**
- **Referrers**
- **Goals**
- **Custom Events**
- **Utm Tracking**
- **Api**
- **Self Hosting**

## Pricing

Plausible Analytics offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Not available (trial may be offered)
- **Growth**: $9/month
- **Business**: $19/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- No cookies — GDPR compliant without consent banner
- Script is under 1KB (vs GA's 45KB)
- Open-source and transparent
- Simple dashboard — no overwhelming data

### Cons

- Less detailed than Google Analytics
- No free tier (but affordable)
- Limited segmentation and custom reports

## Who is Plausible Analytics Best For?

Plausible Analytics is ideal for Privacy Conscious Businesses, Bloggers, Developers, Eu Businesses. If you fall into any of these categories, Plausible Analytics deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
