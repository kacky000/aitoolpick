# Wix Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Wix is drag-and-drop website builder with 900+ templates, e-commerce, and ai site generation. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Wix?

Wix is drag-and-drop website builder with 900+ templates, e-commerce, and ai site generation. It is designed for small businesses, beginners, restaurants and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the website builder space.

## Pros

- Easiest website builder for beginners
- 900+ designer-made templates
- AI generates entire sites from prompts
- All-in-one (hosting, domain, email)

## Cons

- Sites can be slow to load
