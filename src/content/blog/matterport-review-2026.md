# Matterport Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Matterport is 3d capture technology platform that creates immersive digital twins of physical spaces for real estate, construction, hospitality, and retail industries. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Matterport?

Matterport is 3d capture technology platform that creates immersive digital twins of physical spaces for real estate, construction, hospitality, and retail industries. It is designed for real estate agents, property managers, architects and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the real estate space.

## Pros

- Industry-leading 3D virtual tour quality
- Works with smartphone cameras or Pro3 camera
- Measurement tools for accurate floor plans
- Embeddable tours for websites and listings
- AI-powered property insights

## Cons

- Pro3 camera is expensive ($3,395)
- Large spaces require many scan points
- Monthly hosting fees for active spaces
