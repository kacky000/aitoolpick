# Suno vs Udio 2026: Which AI Music Generator Is Better?

Suno and Udio are the two standout AI music generators of 2026. Both turn text prompts into full songs with vocals, but they differ in sound character, control, and workflow.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Suno** if you want fast, complete songs across many genres with an easy workflow. Choose **Udio** if you prioritize audio fidelity and finer control over vocals and arrangement.

## Pricing Compared

| Plan | Suno | Udio |
|------|------|------|
| Free | Yes (daily credits) | Yes (monthly credits) |
| Entry paid | ~$10/mo | ~$10/mo |
| Scales by | Song credits | Song credits |

Both use a credit-based model at similar price points. The decision usually comes down to sound and control rather than cost.

## Where Suno Wins

- **Speed & ease**: Prompt to finished track in seconds, beginner-friendly.
- **Genre range**: Strong across pop, hip-hop, rock, and more.
- **Full songs**: Coherent structure with intros, verses, and choruses.
- **Iteration**: Quick to regenerate and extend sections.

## Where Udio Wins

- **Audio fidelity**: Often cleaner, more detailed production.
- **Vocal quality**: Natural-sounding vocals with nuance.
- **Control**: Finer steering of style, sections, and lyrics.
- **Remixing**: Strong tools for extending and reshaping tracks.

**Choose Suno if you:**
- Want quick, complete songs with minimal effort

**Choose Udio if you:**
