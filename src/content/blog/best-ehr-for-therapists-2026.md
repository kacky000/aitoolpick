Choosing an EHR (electronic health record) and practice management platform is one of the highest-leverage decisions a therapist makes. The right system saves hours of admin every week; the wrong one fights you on every note and claim. This guide ranks the **best EHR for therapists** in 2026, with real pricing and clear guidance on who each fits.

## What therapists actually need from an EHR

Before comparing tools, know your non-negotiables:

- **Clinical notes** — psychotherapy-specific templates (intake, progress, treatment plans)
- **Scheduling & reminders** — to cut no-shows
- **Billing** — insurance claims or cash-pay invoicing
- **Patient portal** — secure messaging, intake forms, and payments
- **Telehealth** — built-in video for remote sessions
- **Compliance** — HIPAA-ready security and audit trails

## 1. TherapyNotes — best overall for therapists

[TherapyNotes](/blog/therapynotes-pricing-2026/) is purpose-built for behavioral health, which is exactly why it tops this list. Notes, scheduling, billing, and a patient portal are all tuned to how therapists actually work.

- **Pricing:** Solo $49, Group $59 per clinician
- **Best for:** Solo therapists and behavioral-health group practices
- **Why it wins:** Specialization beats generalization for clinical documentation

## 2. SimplePractice — best for solo & mixed wellness practices

[SimplePractice](/pricing/simplepractice/) is a polished all-rounder used widely by therapists, counselors, and dietitians. Its Starter tier is the cheapest entry point on this list.

- **Pricing:** Starter $29, Essential $69, Plus $99
- **Best for:** Solo practitioners wanting an affordable, easy start
- **Watch:** Full features live on the $99 Plus tier

Compare the two leaders: [SimplePractice vs TherapyNotes](/compare/simplepractice-vs-therapynotes/).

## 3. Jane App — best for multidisciplinary clinics

[Jane App](/blog/jane-app-pricing-2026/) is the pick when therapy shares space with physiotherapy, massage, or nutrition. Its per-location pricing makes it the value leader for teams.

- **Pricing:** Base $54, Insurance $79, Corporate $99 per location
- **Best for:** Multidisciplinary and allied-health clinics
- **Why it wins:** Flat per-location pricing as you add practitioners

## 4. Practice Better — best for nutrition & coaching

[Practice Better](/pricing/practice-better/) suits dietitians, nutritionists, and wellness coaches with client programs and protocols built in.

- **Pricing:** Starter $25, Professional $59, Plus $89
- **Best for:** Nutrition and health-coaching practices

## 5. Tebra (Kareo) — best for EHR-heavy medical practices

[Tebra](/pricing/kareo/) (formerly Kareo) is built for independent medical providers needing a full EHR plus billing.

- **Pricing:** Custom quote
- **Best for:** Larger or medically oriented practices
