# Best AI App Builders Pricing 2026: Replit, Bolt, Lovable, and v0 Compared

"Describe an app, get a working app" is now a crowded category. Replit, Bolt.new, Lovable, and v0 all let you go from a prompt to a deployed project — and they all price within a few dollars of each other at the entry tier. The headline numbers ($20–$25/month) barely differ. What actually separates them is how they meter your AI usage, how their free tiers throttle you, and whether they scale by team or by individual volume. This guide lines them up so you can pick on the dimension that matters for your build.

## How They Meter: The Real Differentiator

The single most important thing to understand before subscribing is **how each tool counts your usage** — because that, not the sticker price, determines your real monthly cost.

- **Replit** meters in **usage credits** measured in dollars ($20 of credits on Core). Credits expire monthly except on the Pro team plan, where they roll over. Run out and Agent pauses, but you can still code manually.
- **Bolt.new** meters in **tokens** — every action (generation, preview, deploy) consumes them. The free plan's 300K daily cap is the real bottleneck; paid plans remove the daily cap and add rollover, making 10M tokens go further.
- **Lovable** meters in **discrete credits** (100 on Starter, 400 on Pro, 800 on Scale). A complex prompt can cost more than one credit, so estimate ~80–100 meaningful interactions per 100 credits.
- **v0** uses a **credit system** tied to generations.

If you iterate intensely, token rollover (Bolt) or a larger credit pool (Lovable Pro/Scale) protects you from mid-month throttling. If your usage is moderate, Replit's $20 dollar-credits and full hosting environment is the cheapest complete package.

## The Free Tiers: All Evaluation-Only

None of these free plans is a production tool, but they throttle differently:

- **Replit Starter** — daily Agent credit cap + 1,200 monthly dev minutes. Full environment, but won't finish anything past a prototype.
- **Bolt Free** — 1M tokens/mo with a 300K daily cap, Bolt branding on all sites, 10 MB uploads, no custom domains.
- **Lovable Free** — 5 credits/day (~30/mo), and crucially **all projects are public**. Privacy requires a paid plan.
- **v0 Free** — limited credits for testing generations.

Lovable's public-only restriction is the harshest if you're building something private; Bolt's daily token cap is the tightest for heavy iteration.

## Entry Paid: $20 vs $25

- **Replit Core ($20/mo)** — full Agent, autonomous long-running builds, browser-hosted with deploy built in. Cheapest entry, and the only one that bundles a complete IDE + hosting stack. See [Replit pricing](/blog/replit-pricing-2026/).
- **Bolt Pro ($25/mo)** — 10M tokens, no daily cap, token rollover, custom domains, branding removed. Best for heavy single-session generation. See [Bolt.new pricing](/blog/bolt-pricing-2026/).
- **Lovable Starter ($25/mo)** — 100 credits, private projects unlocked. Best for focused prompt-to-app builders. See [Lovable pricing](/blog/lovable-pricing-2026/).
- **v0** — credit-based paid tier, strongest for Next.js/React UI generation. See [v0 pricing](/blog/v0-pricing-2026/).

## Scaling Up: Team vs Volume

Two philosophies split the field:

- **Replit scales by headcount** — Pro at **$100/month flat for up to 15 builders** (under $7/seat) is the cheapest way to equip a team.
- **Bolt scales per-user** — **$30/user/month**, each seat with its own 10M tokens.
- **Lovable scales by individual volume** — Pro **$50/mo (400 credits)**, Scale (800 credits) for one heavy builder.

For a team, Replit's flat $100 wins decisively. For a solo power-builder, Lovable Pro's 400 credits or Bolt's rolled-over tokens give more individual headroom.

## Recommendations by Use Case

- **Cheapest complete build-and-host platform:** Replit Core ($20).
- **Heavy single-session generation with rollover:** Bolt Pro ($25).
- **Focused prompt-to-app with predictable credits:** Lovable Starter ($25) → Pro ($50).
- **Polished React/Next.js UI generation:** v0.
- **Equipping a 3–15 person team:** Replit Pro ($100 flat).

## The Bottom Line

At the entry tier these tools are separated by $5 and a metering model, not by capability. Decide on three questions: Do you need hosting built in (Replit) or just UI generation (v0)? Do you iterate in heavy bursts (Bolt's rollover) or steady sessions (Lovable's credits)? And are you solo (volume-based plans) or a team (Replit's flat seat pricing)? Answer those and the right $20–$25 plan picks itself.
