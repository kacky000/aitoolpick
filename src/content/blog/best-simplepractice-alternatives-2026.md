SimplePractice is a strong all-rounder for therapists and wellness practitioners, but it isn't the right fit for everyone. As your plan climbs to the **Plus tier at $99/month**, or if you need multidisciplinary scheduling or cheaper team pricing, it's worth knowing your options. Here are the best **SimplePractice alternatives** in 2026, with honest notes on who each one suits.

## Why look beyond SimplePractice?

SimplePractice prices in three tiers — [Starter $29, Essential $69, Plus $99](/pricing/simplepractice/) — all per clinician. Common reasons clinics switch:

- **Cost at scale** — per-clinician pricing adds up for groups.
- **Discipline fit** — allied-health and multidisciplinary clinics need broader scheduling.
- **Feature gaps** — some practices want stronger insurance billing or behavioral-health-specific templates.

## 1. TherapyNotes — best for behavioral health

If you're a therapist or counselor, [TherapyNotes](/blog/therapynotes-pricing-2026/) is the most direct alternative. It's built specifically for behavioral health with psychotherapy note templates, billing, and a patient portal.

- **Pricing:** Solo $49, Group $59 per clinician
- **Best for:** Therapists and behavioral-health groups
- **Trade-off:** Less suited to non-therapy disciplines

Compare them directly: [SimplePractice vs TherapyNotes](/compare/simplepractice-vs-therapynotes/).

## 2. Jane App — best for multidisciplinary clinics

[Jane App](/blog/jane-app-pricing-2026/) shines for allied-health and multidisciplinary teams. Its standout advantage is **per-location pricing**, which makes it far cheaper than per-clinician tools once you have several practitioners.

- **Pricing:** Base $54, Insurance $79, Corporate $99 per location
- **Best for:** Physiotherapy, chiropractic, massage, and mixed-discipline clinics
- **Trade-off:** SMS reminders cost extra

Compare them: [Jane App vs SimplePractice](/compare/jane-app-vs-simplepractice/).

## 3. Practice Better — best for nutrition & wellness coaches

[Practice Better](/pricing/practice-better/) targets dietitians, nutritionists, and health coaches, with client programs, food journaling, and protocols built in.

- **Pricing:** Starter $25, Professional $59, Plus $89
- **Best for:** Nutrition, functional medicine, and wellness coaching
- **Trade-off:** Less focused on traditional medical billing

See how it compares: [Practice Better vs SimplePractice](/compare/practice-better-vs-simplepractice/).

## 4. Tebra (Kareo) — best for larger medical practices

[Tebra](/pricing/kareo/) (formerly Kareo) is an all-in-one platform aimed at independent medical providers, with EHR, billing, and practice management.

- **Pricing:** Custom quote
- **Best for:** Medical practices needing full EHR + billing
- **Trade-off:** Custom pricing means less transparency upfront

## How to choose

| If you are a... | Choose |
|---|---|
| Therapist / counselor | TherapyNotes |
| Multidisciplinary clinic | Jane App |
| Nutrition / wellness coach | Practice Better |
| Medical practice (EHR-heavy) | Tebra (Kareo) |
| Solo practitioner wanting cheapest entry | SimplePractice Starter ($29) |

## Bottom line

SimplePractice remains a solid default, but the best alternative depends on your discipline and team size. Behavioral-health specialists should look at TherapyNotes, multidisciplinary clinics at Jane App, and wellness coaches at Practice Better.
