NordVPN's pricing can be confusing — four plan tiers, three billing cycles, and constantly rotating promotions. Here's a clear breakdown of exactly what each plan costs and includes in 2026.

## NordVPN Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Monthly | 1-Year | 2-Year |
|------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Basic** | $12.99/mo | $4.59/mo | $3.49/mo |
| **Plus** | $13.99/mo | $5.59/mo | $4.49/mo |
| **Complete** | $14.99/mo | $6.59/mo | $5.49/mo |
| **Prime** | $17.99/mo | $7.59/mo | $6.49/mo |

All plans come with a **30-day money-back guarantee**, so you can try any tier risk-free.

## What Each Plan Includes

### Basic ($3.49/mo on 2-year plan)
The core VPN experience:
- VPN encryption across 6,000+ servers in 60+ countries
- Connect up to 10 devices simultaneously
- Kill switch and split tunneling
- No-logs policy (independently audited)

This is all most people need. If you just want a fast, reliable VPN for privacy and streaming, stop here.

### Plus ($4.49/mo on 2-year plan)
Everything in Basic, plus:
- **Threat Protection Pro** — blocks malware, trackers, and ads even when VPN is off
- **Dark Web Monitor** — alerts if your credentials appear in data breaches
- **Password manager (NordPass)** — cross-platform password vault

The Plus plan makes sense if you'd otherwise buy a separate ad-blocker or password manager. NordPass alone costs $2.49/month, so bundling saves money.

### Complete ($5.49/mo on 2-year plan)
Everything in Plus, plus:
- **NordLocker** (1TB) — encrypted cloud storage
- **File encryption** for sensitive documents

Worth it if you need secure cloud storage. Otherwise, the Plus plan covers most use cases.

### Prime ($6.49/mo on 2-year plan)
Everything in Complete, plus:
- **Incogni** — automated personal data removal from data brokers
- Requests data deletion from 150+ brokers on your behalf

Incogni is a standalone product that costs $7.49/month separately, making Prime a significant deal if data removal matters to you.

## Monthly vs Annual vs 2-Year: Which Term?

The math is straightforward:

| Term | Basic Cost | You Save |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Monthly | $12.99/mo ($155.88/yr) | — |
| 1-Year | $4.59/mo ($55.08/yr) | 65% |
| 2-Year | $3.49/mo ($83.76 total) | 73% |

The 2-year plan offers the best per-month rate, but you're committing $83.76 upfront. The 1-year plan is the sweet spot for most people — significant savings without a two-year lock-in.

**Important**: After your initial term, NordVPN renews at a higher rate (typically the 1-year price). Set a calendar reminder before renewal to re-evaluate or re-purchase.

## Is NordVPN Worth the Price?

**Yes, for most users.** NordVPN consistently ranks among the fastest VPNs, reliably unblocks streaming services, and has passed multiple independent no-logs audits. The Plus plan ($4.49/mo) is particularly strong value when you factor in the included password manager and threat protection.

**Skip NordVPN if**: You want a free VPN (try Proton VPN), you're purely budget-driven (Surfshark is cheaper), or you prioritize maximum speed above all else (ExpressVPN edges NordVPN in some speed tests).

## Best Way to Buy

1. **Choose the 1-year or 2-year plan** — the monthly plan is never worth it
2. **Start with Basic** unless you know you need the extras
3. **Use the 30-day guarantee** as a real trial period
4. **Set a renewal reminder** to avoid surprise price jumps
