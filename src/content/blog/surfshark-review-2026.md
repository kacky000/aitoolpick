Surfshark promises premium VPN features at budget prices — plus unlimited device connections. In a market where most VPNs limit you to 5-10 devices, that's a bold pitch. But does Surfshark actually deliver?

Here's our full review for 2026.

## What Is Surfshark?

Surfshark is a Netherlands-based VPN that launched in 2018 and rapidly grew by offering competitive pricing and an unlimited simultaneous connections policy. In 2022, Surfshark merged with Nord Security (the parent company of NordVPN), though both brands continue operating independently.

## Pricing

| Plan | Monthly | 1-Year | 2-Year |
|------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Starter** | $15.45/mo | $3.19/mo | $2.49/mo |
| **One** | $17.95/mo | $3.49/mo | $2.79/mo |

All plans include a 30-day money-back guarantee. The 2-year Starter plan at $2.49/month is one of the cheapest VPN options available — and frequently includes 3 bonus months in promotions.

### What's in Each Tier

**Starter**: Full VPN, ad blocker (CleanWeb), cookie pop-up blocker
**One**: Everything in Starter + Surfshark Antivirus + Alternative ID (generated email/name for signups) + data breach alerts
**One+**: Everything in One + Incogni data removal service (removes your info from data brokers)

## Speed Test Results

Surfshark's speeds are solid for a budget VPN:

- **Nearby servers**: 350-500 Mbps (on gigabit connection)
- **US servers from Europe**: 150-250 Mbps
- **Latency impact**: 10-20ms additional

These numbers put Surfshark in the upper-middle tier — behind NordVPN and ExpressVPN, but ahead of many competitors. For streaming, browsing, and even gaming, these speeds are more than sufficient.

The WireGuard protocol (Surfshark's default) delivers the best performance. Avoid OpenVPN unless you specifically need it.

## Unlimited Devices — The Real Deal

Surfshark's headline feature is genuine: one subscription covers every device you own. Phone, laptop, tablet, smart TV, router — no limit. For families or anyone with multiple devices, this eliminates the "which devices get VPN protection?" calculation.

Most competitors cap at 5-10 devices. NordVPN allows 10, ExpressVPN allows 8. Surfshark's unlimited policy is a genuine advantage.

## Security & Privacy

**Strengths:**
- **RAM-only servers** — data wiped on every reboot
- **No-logs policy** — audited by Deloitte
- **AES-256 encryption** with WireGuard and OpenVPN
- **Kill switch** on all platforms
- **MultiHop** — route through 2 VPN servers for extra privacy
- **NoBorders mode** — works in restrictive regions (China, UAE)

**Concerns:**
- **Netherlands jurisdiction** — part of the 14 Eyes alliance (though the no-logs audit mitigates this)
- **Nord Security ownership** — some privacy advocates question the consolidation of VPN providers under one corporate umbrella

## CleanWeb Ad Blocker

Surfshark's built-in ad blocker works at the DNS level, blocking ads, trackers, and malware domains before they load. It's not as granular as a browser-based ad blocker like uBlock Origin, but it works across all apps on your device — not just the browser.

In testing, CleanWeb blocked roughly 70-80% of ads, which is good for a VPN-integrated blocker. For total ad elimination, pair it with a browser extension.

## Streaming & Torrenting

**Streaming**: Surfshark reliably unblocks Netflix (US, UK, Japan, and more), Disney+, HBO Max, BBC iPlayer, and Amazon Prime Video. It works consistently — though occasional server switches may be needed when services update their blocking.

**Torrenting**: P2P is allowed on all servers. Download speeds are adequate, and the kill switch protects against IP leaks during file sharing.

## Apps & Usability

Surfshark's apps are clean and intuitive across Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, Linux, and browser extensions. The interface is beginner-friendly — connect with one click, and the app selects the fastest nearby server automatically.

Advanced users can manually choose servers, enable MultiHop, configure split tunneling (called Bypasser), or toggle CleanWeb.

## Who Should Choose Surfshark?

**Ideal for:**
- Budget-conscious users wanting a premium VPN
- Families needing unlimited device connections
- Users who want VPN + antivirus in one subscription (One plan)
- Streamers who need reliable geo-unblocking

**Look elsewhere if:**
- You need the fastest possible speeds (NordVPN or ExpressVPN edge ahead)
- Privacy jurisdiction matters to you (Proton VPN's Swiss base is stronger)
- You want a free tier to try before paying (Proton VPN offers this)

## The Verdict

Surfshark delivers remarkable value. At $2.49/month with unlimited devices, it's hard to find a VPN that offers more for less. Speed, security, and streaming support are all solid — not best-in-class, but well above average.

The main trade-offs are its Netherlands jurisdiction and slightly inconsistent speeds on distant servers. But for the price? Surfshark is one of the best deals in the VPN market.

**Rating: 4.4/5**
