Ideogram has quietly become one of the most capable AI image generators available, especially for anyone who needs legible text in generated images. But with five tiers — Free, Basic, Plus, Pro, and Team — the pricing structure is not immediately obvious. Here is exactly what each plan costs and includes in 2026.

## Quick Pricing Overview

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Priority Credits/Month | Slow Credits | Commercial License |
|------|--------------|-------------|----------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | 0 | 10/day | No |
| Basic | $7 | $4/mo ($48/yr) | 400 | 100/day | Yes |
| Plus | $16 | $10/mo ($120/yr) | 1,000 | Unlimited | Yes |
| Pro | $60 | $42/mo ($504/yr) | 3,000 | Unlimited | Yes |
| Team | $60/seat | $42/seat/mo | 3,000/seat | Unlimited | Yes |

*Annual billing saves roughly 30-40% depending on the tier. Prices reflect current rates as of May 2026.*

**Note:** The Basic plan is a legacy tier and is no longer available for new subscribers. Existing Basic subscribers retain access.

## How Credits Work

Ideogram uses a dual-credit system. **Priority credits** give you near-instant generation. **Slow credits** put you in a queue that can take 30-60 seconds per image depending on server load. Each credit generates one prompt, which produces up to four image variations.

Unused priority credits expire at the end of each billing cycle. If you run out, you can purchase top-up packs at $4 each — the number of credits per pack depends on your plan: 100 for Basic, 150 for Plus, and 250 for Pro/Team.

## Free Plan

The free tier gives you 10 slow credits per day. That translates to roughly 40 images daily (4 variations per prompt), which is generous compared to most competitors.

**Limitations:**
- All generations are public and visible in Ideogram's community gallery
- No reference image uploads (no remixing, style transfer, or inpainting)
- Slow queue only — no priority processing
- No commercial license for generated images
- Limited access to advanced editing features

The free plan works well for casual exploration and personal projects. If you need private generations or plan to use images commercially, you will need to upgrade.

## Basic Plan — $7/month (Legacy)

The Basic plan is no longer available for new sign-ups but remains active for existing subscribers. It includes 400 priority credits per month plus 100 slow credits per day.

**Includes:**
- Priority generation queue
- Ideogram Editor access
- Original quality PNG downloads
- Upscaling and tiling
- Commercial license
- Private generations

For anyone currently on Basic who needs more capacity, the jump to Plus is the natural next step.

## Plus Plan — $16/month

Plus is Ideogram's mid-tier plan and the most popular choice for individual creators. You get 1,000 priority credits per month and unlimited slow credits.

**Includes everything in Basic, plus:**
- 2.5x more priority credits than Basic
- Unlimited slow generations
- Private generation by default (unpublish previously published images)
- All editing and enhancement tools
- Full commercial license

**Best for:** Freelance designers, social media managers, and content creators who generate images regularly but not at production scale.

At $10/month with annual billing, Plus offers strong value — especially since unlimited slow credits mean you never truly run out of generations.

## Pro Plan — $60/month

Pro is built for high-volume users. With 3,000 priority credits per month and unlimited slow credits, it is designed for professionals and small studios that rely on AI image generation as a core part of their workflow.

**Includes everything in Plus, plus:**
- 3x more priority credits than Plus
- Batch Generation — generate up to 500 prompts at once via CSV upload
- API access for integration into custom pipelines
- Highest priority in the generation queue

**Best for:** Design agencies, e-commerce teams producing product imagery at scale, and developers building on top of Ideogram's API.

## Team Plan — $60/seat/month

The Team plan mirrors Pro features but adds multi-user management. A single admin account manages billing and access for all team members.

**Includes everything in Pro, plus:**
- Centralized billing and administration
- Individual credit allocations per team member
- Shared workspace features
- Consistent commercial licensing across all members

**Best for:** Studios, marketing departments, and any organization where multiple people need Ideogram access under one account.

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

**You want to try Ideogram:** Start with Free. Ten prompts a day is enough to evaluate quality and text rendering.

**You create images for clients or commercial projects:** Plus is the minimum. The commercial license and private generations are essential for professional work, and $10/month (annual) is competitive.

**You generate images daily at volume:** Pro. The 3,000 priority credits and batch generation feature eliminate bottlenecks. The API access also opens up automation possibilities.

**Your team needs shared access:** Team. The per-seat pricing matches Pro, but centralized management saves administrative overhead.

## How Does Ideogram Compare on Price?

Ideogram sits in the mid-range of AI image generator pricing. Midjourney's Basic plan starts at $10/month for roughly 200 images, making Ideogram's free tier and Plus plan comparatively generous on output volume. However, Midjourney still leads on artistic quality for certain styles, while Ideogram dominates in text rendering accuracy.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Ideogram free to use?

Yes. The free plan provides 10 slow credits per day (up to 40 images). Generations are public and there is no commercial license, but it is a functional free tier with no time limit.

### Can I use Ideogram images commercially?

Only on paid plans (Basic and above). Free-tier images do not include a commercial license.

### What happens if I run out of priority credits?

You can still generate images using slow credits (unlimited on Plus and Pro). Alternatively, purchase top-up packs at $4 each for additional priority credits.

### Is annual billing worth it?

Yes. Annual billing on Pro saves over $200/year compared to monthly billing. Even on Plus, you save roughly $72/year. If you plan to use Ideogram for more than a couple of months, annual billing is the clear choice.
