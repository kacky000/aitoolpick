# Pipedrive Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Sales-focused CRM designed by salespeople with visual pipeline management and activity-based selling. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the crm space.

## What is Pipedrive?

Sales-focused CRM designed by salespeople with visual pipeline management and activity-based selling. Founded in 2010, it has established itself as a significant player in the crm category. With a user rating of 4.4/5, it primarily serves Sales Teams, Small Businesses, Startups, Agencies.

## Key Features

- **Visual Pipeline**
- **Activity Tracking**
- **Email Integration**
- **Ai Assistant**
- **Automations**
- **Reporting**
- **Web Forms**

## Pricing

Pipedrive offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Not available (trial may be offered)
- **Essential**: $14/user/month
- **Advanced**: $34/user/month
- **Professional**: $49/user/month
- **Power**: $64/user/month
- **Enterprise**: $99/user/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Visual pipeline is intuitive
- Activity-based selling methodology
- Easy to set up and use
- AI Sales Assistant included

### Cons

- No free tier
- Marketing features are add-ons
- Reporting less powerful than [HubSpot](/tools/hubspot/)

## Who is Pipedrive Best For?

Pipedrive is ideal for Sales Teams, Small Businesses, Startups, Agencies. If you fall into any of these categories, Pipedrive deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
