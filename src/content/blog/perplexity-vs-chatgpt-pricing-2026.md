**[Perplexity](/tools/perplexity/)** and **[ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/)** both anchor their paid plans at exactly $20/month — but you are buying two different things. Perplexity sells cited, search-grounded answers; ChatGPT sells a general-purpose assistant with the widest tool ecosystem. At the same price, the better value depends entirely on what you do all day.

This guide compares every tier, the free limits, and which $20 subscription delivers more for your money.

## Pricing Overview

### Perplexity Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Right For |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Casual search, ~5 Pro searches/day |
| Pro | $20/mo ($200/yr) | Researchers, daily search users |
| Max | ~$200/mo | Power users wanting top models + priority |

### ChatGPT Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Right For |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Casual use |
| Go | $8/mo | Light users (select regions) |
| Plus | $20/mo | Power users, professionals |
| Pro | $200/mo | Heavy/research users |

## The Key Difference: Cited Search vs General Assistant

At $20, the two plans solve different problems.

**Perplexity Pro** unlocks unlimited Pro searches that pull from live web sources and return answers with citations. You also get model choice (GPT, Claude, and Perplexity's own models), file uploads for analysis, and deeper academic/financial focus modes. It is built for people who need *sourced, current* answers.

**ChatGPT Plus** is a general assistant: advanced reasoning models, image generation, voice mode, custom GPTs, data analysis, and the largest third-party tool library. It is built for people who need *one tool that does everything*.

So the real question is not "which is cheaper" — they cost the same — but "do you need citations or breadth?"

## Cost Scenario: One Year of Daily Use

- **Perplexity Pro**: $20 × 12 = **$240/year**, or **$200** on the annual plan (~$16.67/mo)
- **ChatGPT Plus**: $20 × 12 = **$240/year** (no annual discount on Plus)

Perplexity's annual plan is the only way to get the headline tier below $20/month. If you commit yearly, Perplexity is effectively the cheaper of the two.

## The Free Tiers Compared

- **Perplexity Free**: Full search interface with roughly 5 Pro-level searches per day; standard searches are unlimited. Genuinely useful for light research.
- **ChatGPT Free**: Access to a capable default model with usage caps and limited advanced features.

If your main need is *answering questions with sources*, Perplexity's free tier already covers casual use. If you want a do-everything assistant, ChatGPT's free tier stretches further across tasks.

## The Premium Tier

Both top out near $200/month, but for different buyers:

- **Perplexity Max (~$200/mo)**: Priority access to the newest models, higher limits, and early features for heavy researchers.
- **ChatGPT Pro ($200/mo)**: Near-unlimited access to the most advanced reasoning models for research-heavy workloads.

## Verdict

**Choose Perplexity** if your work is research, fact-finding, and anything where *citations matter*. The annual plan also makes it the cheaper option at ~$16.67/month.

**Choose ChatGPT** if you want one assistant for writing, coding, images, voice, and custom workflows. The $8 Go tier also gives ChatGPT a cheaper entry point that Perplexity does not match.

Many users run both: Perplexity for sourced research, ChatGPT for everything else. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Perplexity or ChatGPT cheaper?

Both Pro/Plus tiers cost $20/month. Perplexity is cheaper if you pay annually ($200/year ≈ $16.67/mo), while ChatGPT offers a cheaper $8 Go entry tier.

### What does Perplexity Pro add over free?

Unlimited Pro searches, model choice, file uploads, and deeper focus modes. The free tier caps Pro searches at roughly 5 per day.

### Can I use both Perplexity and ChatGPT together?

Yes, and many do. Use Perplexity for cited research and ChatGPT for general assistant tasks like writing, coding, and image generation.

### Which has the better free tier?

It depends on your use. Perplexity's free tier is stronger for sourced search; ChatGPT's free tier covers a broader range of general tasks.
