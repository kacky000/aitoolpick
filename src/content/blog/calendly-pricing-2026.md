**Calendly pricing** in 2026 follows a four-tier model: Free, Standard, Teams, and Enterprise. The free plan is functional for individuals with simple needs. The paid plans add features that matter most for sales teams and organizations managing multiple meeting types.

## Calendly Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Individuals with one meeting type |
| Standard | $10/month | Individuals needing unlimited event types |
| Teams | $16/month | Sales teams, routing, round-robin |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large organizations, SSO, compliance |

All paid prices are per user per month, billed annually. Monthly billing adds approximately 20%.

## Free Plan: What's Included

Calendly's free plan gives you a working scheduling solution with these features:

- **1 event type** (e.g., 30-minute call)
- 1 calendar connection (Google, Outlook, or iCloud)
- Basic availability settings
- Automatic email confirmations
- Calendly-branded booking page
- Unlimited meeting bookings

The free plan is genuinely useful for one specific use case — sharing a single booking link for one meeting type. Freelancers who need a "Book a discovery call" link and nothing else can run on free indefinitely.

What you cannot do on free:
- Create a second event type (e.g., separate 60-minute consultation link)
- Connect multiple calendars
- Remove Calendly branding
- Set up workflows or automated follow-ups
- Collect payments via Stripe

## Standard Plan: $10/month

Standard unlocks most of what makes Calendly genuinely powerful:

- **Unlimited event types** (15-min calls, 30-min demos, 60-min strategy sessions)
- 2 calendar connections
- **Workflows**: Automated email and SMS reminders, follow-ups, and no-show sequences
- **Payment collection** via Stripe (charge for consultations, workshops, or coaching sessions)
- Embed in your website
- Group events (multiple people booking the same slot)
- Remove Calendly branding
- Basic reporting

At $10/month, Standard is the tier where Calendly starts earning its keep. If you're a consultant, coach, or salesperson sharing meeting links regularly, the workflow automations alone reduce no-shows enough to justify the cost.

## Teams Plan: $16/month

Teams adds collaborative features that sales and customer success teams need:

- Everything in Standard
- **Routing forms**: Qualify prospects and route them to the right team member automatically
- **Round-robin scheduling**: Distribute inbound meetings evenly across your team
- **Collective scheduling**: Require multiple team members to be available for a meeting
- Team admin controls and managed events
- Salesforce and HubSpot CRM integration (auto-log meetings)
- Interchangeable links (reassign event types between team members)
- Advanced reporting and analytics

The routing forms feature is the standout at this tier. When prospects book a sales call, they answer qualifying questions first — company size, use case, current tool — and Calendly routes them to the right rep automatically. For sales teams with multiple reps or specializations, this is a significant efficiency gain.

## Enterprise Plan: Custom Pricing

Enterprise adds compliance, security, and administration features required by larger organizations:

- SSO (SAML/OIDC)
- Advanced security controls
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Priority support
- Legal data processing agreements (GDPR, HIPAA)
- Enhanced team management

Enterprise pricing is not published and requires a sales conversation. Expect pricing in the range of $25-40+/user/month depending on size and contract length.

## Annual vs Monthly Billing

All prices above are billed annually. Monthly billing rates:

| Plan | Annual | Monthly | Difference |
|------|--------|---------|------------|
| Standard | $10/month | $12/month | +20% |
| Teams | $16/month | $20/month | +25% |

Annual billing is clearly better if you plan to use Calendly long-term. The break-even point is under 10 months.

## Is Calendly Worth It?

**Free is worth it if**: You have exactly one meeting type and don't mind Calendly branding.

**Standard at $10/month is worth it if**: You need multiple event types or want to automate reminders and follow-ups. The workflow automation alone — reducing no-shows by 30-50% — typically recovers its cost quickly for professionals who charge by the hour.

**Teams at $16/month is worth it if**: You manage a team sharing meeting load and need routing, round-robin, or CRM auto-logging. The math works easily for sales teams where one extra booked meeting covers months of subscription costs.

## Bottom Line

Calendly's Standard plan at $10/month is easy to recommend for anyone sharing scheduling links professionally. The Teams plan at $16/month has a clear ROI for sales teams through routing and round-robin alone.
