Descript changed video editing with its text-based approach — edit a transcript, and the video follows. But at $24/month for Hobbyist and with specific limitations on export quality, you might want an alternative that better fits your editing style or budget.

## 1. Runway

**Best for:** AI-powered video creation and effects

Runway goes beyond editing with generative AI video capabilities. Use Gen-3 Alpha to create video from text, apply AI-powered VFX, and edit with tools like inpainting and motion tracking.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Standard $12/mo, Pro $28/mo, Unlimited $76/mo
- **Why choose over Descript:** AI video generation, advanced VFX tools
- **Trade-off:** No transcript-based editing; steeper learning curve

[Read our Runway review →](/blog/runway-review-2026)

## 2. CapCut

**Best for:** Free video editing with TikTok integration

CapCut offers surprisingly powerful editing for free, including AI captions, background removal, and text-to-speech. Its direct TikTok integration makes it the default choice for short-form creators.

- **Pricing:** Free; Pro $7.99/mo
- **Why choose over Descript:** Free tier is more generous; native TikTok workflow
- **Trade-off:** Less suited for long-form content and podcast editing

[Read our CapCut review →](/blog/capcut-review-2026)

## 3. DaVinci Resolve

**Best for:** Professional-grade editing at zero cost

DaVinci Resolve is a full Hollywood-grade editor, color grader, and audio post-production suite — completely free. The paid Studio version ($295 one-time) adds AI features like magic mask and speed warp.

- **Pricing:** Free; Studio $295 one-time purchase
- **Why choose over Descript:** Professional color grading, no subscription cost
- **Trade-off:** Complex interface; no AI transcription-based editing

## 4. Otter.ai

**Best for:** Transcription and meeting notes (not video editing)

If you use Descript primarily for transcription rather than video editing, Otter.ai is a dedicated alternative. It transcribes meetings in real-time, generates summaries, and integrates with Zoom and Teams.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Pro $16.99/mo, Business $30/mo
- **Why choose over Descript:** Better live transcription; meeting-focused features
- **Trade-off:** No video editing capabilities at all

[Compare Descript vs Otter.ai →](/compare/descript-vs-otter-ai)

## 5. Adobe Premiere Pro

**Best for:** Industry-standard video editing

Premiere Pro remains the professional standard for video editing. With AI-powered features like auto-transcription, scene edit detection, and Firefly-enhanced tools, it bridges traditional editing with AI capabilities.

- **Pricing:** $22.99/mo (single app); $54.99/mo (Creative Cloud)
- **Why choose over Descript:** Industry standard; deeper editing capabilities
- **Trade-off:** Expensive subscription; steeper learning curve

## 6. Podcastle

**Best for:** Podcast production with AI enhancement

Podcastle is built specifically for podcasters, offering AI voice cloning, background noise removal, and multi-track recording. It fills the same niche as Descript's podcast features but with a more focused toolset.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Storyteller $11.99/mo, Pro $23.99/mo
- **Why choose over Descript:** Purpose-built for podcasts; cheaper for audio-only
- **Trade-off:** Limited video editing capabilities

## 7. Synthesia

**Best for:** AI avatar videos without any filming

If you use Descript to avoid appearing on camera, Synthesia takes it further — no camera needed at all. Type a script, choose an AI avatar, and get a professional video. Different approach, similar goal.

- **Pricing:** Free tier; Starter $22/mo, Creator $67/mo
- **Why choose over Descript:** No filming or editing needed; fully AI-generated
- **Trade-off:** No editing of existing footage; avatar-based only
