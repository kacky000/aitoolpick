# QuillBot Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

AI paraphrasing and writing tool for rewording, grammar checking, summarizing, and translating text. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the ai writing space.

## What is QuillBot?

AI paraphrasing and writing tool for rewording, grammar checking, summarizing, and translating text. Founded in 2017, it has established itself as a significant player in the ai writing category. With a user rating of 4.3/5, it primarily serves Students, Academics, Non Native Speakers, Writers, Researchers.

## Key Features

- **Paraphrasing**
- **Grammar Check**
- **Summarizer**
- **Translator**
- **Citation Generator**
- **Browser Extension**
- **Word Plugin**

## Pricing

QuillBot offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Premium**: $9.95/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Excellent paraphrasing with multiple modes
- Integrated grammar checker
- Works within [Google Docs](/tools/google-docs/) and Word
- Affordable premium plan

### Cons

- Paraphrasing can change meaning
- Free tier limits paragraph length
- Less powerful than full AI assistants

## Who is QuillBot Best For?

QuillBot is ideal for Students, Academics, Non Native Speakers, Writers, Researchers. If you fall into any of these categories, QuillBot deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
