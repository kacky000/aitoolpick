# Gamma vs Tome 2026: Best AI Presentation Tool?

Gamma and Tome both turn a prompt into a finished presentation, but they take different paths. Gamma is a flexible AI deck and document builder, while Tome leans into narrative, design-forward storytelling.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Gamma** if you want fast, editable decks plus docs and webpages from one tool. Choose **Tome** if you want sleek, story-driven presentations with strong visual polish out of the box.

## Pricing Compared

| Plan | Gamma | Tome |
|------|-------|------|
| Free | Yes (credit-based) | Yes (limited) |
| Entry paid | ~$8–10/mo | ~$16/mo |
| Output | Decks, docs, sites | Narrative decks |

Gamma's credit-based model and lower entry price make it friendly for frequent creators. Tome positions itself as a more premium, design-led tool.

## Where Gamma Wins

- **Versatility**: Generate slide decks, documents, and simple webpages.
- **Fast editing**: Card-based layout that's quick to restructure.
- **Affordable**: Lower entry price and a usable free tier.
- **Export options**: Easy sharing as links, PDFs, or PowerPoint.

## Where Tome Wins

- **Visual storytelling**: Polished, narrative-first layouts.
- **Design defaults**: Decks look refined with minimal tweaking.
- **Brand feel**: Great for pitch and sales storytelling.
- **Media-rich slides**: Smooth handling of images and embeds.

## Head-to-Head

| Factor | Gamma | Tome |
|--------|-------|------|
| AI generation | Excellent | Very good |
| Versatility (docs/sites) | Excellent | Limited |
| Visual polish | Very good | Excellent |
| Editing speed | Excellent | Good |
| Entry price | Lower | Higher |

## Who Should Choose What?

**Choose Gamma if you:**
- Need decks, docs, and pages from one affordable tool
- Iterate on content frequently
- Want quick restructuring and export flexibility

**Choose Tome if you:**
- Build pitch and sales narratives that must look premium
- Value design polish over format flexibility
- Want story-driven slides with minimal manual styling

## The Bottom Line

**Gamma** is the flexible, affordable all-rounder—decks, docs, and pages in one place. **Tome** is the design-forward storyteller—best when polish and narrative matter most. Pick Gamma for speed and range; pick Tome for visual impact.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can Gamma export to PowerPoint?

Yes—Gamma supports exporting to PowerPoint and PDF, plus shareable web links.

### Is Tome only for presentations?

Tome focuses on narrative presentations and storytelling; Gamma covers a wider range of document types.

### Which is cheaper?

Gamma generally has a lower entry price and a credit-based free tier, making it the budget-friendly option.
