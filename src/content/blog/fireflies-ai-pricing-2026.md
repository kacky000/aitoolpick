**[Fireflies.ai](/tools/fireflies-ai/)** is the meeting assistant built for teams that live in their CRM — it joins calls, transcribes them, and pushes notes into Salesforce, HubSpot, and Slack automatically. Here's exactly what Fireflies costs in 2026, including the AI-credit system that determines your real ceiling.

## Fireflies.ai Pricing in 2026

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Storage minutes |
|------|---------|--------|-----------------|
| Free | **$0** | $0 | 800 min |
| Pro | **$18/mo** | $10/user/mo | 3,000 min |
| Business | **$29/mo** | $19/user/mo | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Custom | $39/user/mo | Unlimited + security |

Annual billing offers a steep 40–45% discount — Pro drops from $18 to $10/user/month. The free plan is unusually generous compared to [Otter.ai](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026)'s 300 minutes.

## What Each Plan Actually Gets You

- **Free ($0)** — 800 minutes of storage, unlimited transcription, and basic AI summaries. Enough to evaluate Fireflies seriously before paying.
- **Pro ($18/mo, $10 annual)** — 3,000 storage minutes, unlimited transcription, AI summaries with the AskFred assistant, and integrations with CRMs, Slack, and Zapier. The standard tier for individuals and small teams.
- **Business ($29/mo, $19 annual)** — **Unlimited storage**, team analytics, conversation intelligence, and admin controls. The right tier for sales and customer-facing teams.
- **Enterprise ($39/user annual)** — Adds SSO, advanced security, custom data retention, and a dedicated success manager.

## The Hidden Cost: AI Credits

Fireflies meters its AI features with a **shared workspace credit pool**: Pro gets 20, Business 30, Enterprise 50. Teams that lean heavily on AI summaries, action items, and AskFred queries can burn through these and need add-on credit bundles, which run roughly **$0.06–$0.10 per credit**. Factor this in if AI is central to your workflow.

## Is Fireflies.ai Worth It?

### It's worth it if:

- Your team runs **lots of calls** and needs notes auto-synced to a CRM
- You want the **most generous free tier** to evaluate before paying
- You'll commit **annually** — the 40–45% discount is significant
- You need **unlimited storage** for a searchable meeting archive (Business)

### Look elsewhere if:

- You mostly take **in-person, live notes** — [Otter.ai](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026)'s real-time capture fits better
- Your priority is **sales-rep coaching and scorecards** — [tl;dv](/blog/tldv-pricing-2026) specializes there
- You need **certified-accuracy transcripts** — [Rev](/blog/rev-pricing-2026)'s human transcription is the standard

## Tips to Lower Your Fireflies.ai Cost

1. **Always pay annually** — the 40–45% discount cuts Pro from $18 to $10/user.
2. **Monitor AI credits** — heavy AskFred users should track usage before buying add-on bundles.
3. **Start free** — 800 storage minutes is enough to validate the workflow.
4. **Right-size storage** — only jump to Business ($29) when 3,000 Pro minutes genuinely run out.

## The Bottom Line

Fireflies.ai in 2026 is the best-value meeting assistant for **CRM-connected teams**, thanks to deep integrations, unlimited storage on Business, and the steepest annual discount in the category. Most teams belong on **Pro ($10/user annual)**; sales orgs that need conversation intelligence step up to Business. Watch the AI-credit pool if summaries are core to your work — that's the one cost the sticker price hides. For pure live note-taking, [Otter.ai](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026) is still worth a look.
