# How to Switch from Grammarly to QuillBot (2026 Guide)

Thinking about moving from Grammarly to QuillBot? Whether you want stronger paraphrasing, lower pricing, or a different feature mix, this guide walks you through the switch step by step.

## Why People Switch

- **Paraphrasing**: QuillBot's paraphraser and modes are a core strength.
- **Pricing**: QuillBot is often cheaper for premium features.
- **Summarizing & citations**: Built-in tools beyond grammar checking.
- **Bundled toolkit**: Paraphraser, summarizer, and grammar in one place.

Grammarly still leads on real-time grammar coaching, tone detection, and broad app integrations—so the switch isn't right for everyone.

## Before You Switch: Compare Your Needs

| Need | Grammarly | QuillBot |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Grammar checking | Excellent | Good |
| Paraphrasing | Basic | Excellent |
| Tone & style | Strong | Moderate |
| Summarizing | Limited | Built-in |
| Price | Higher | Lower |

If grammar and tone are your priority, you may want to stay. If paraphrasing and value matter more, QuillBot is a strong move.

## Step-by-Step: Making the Switch

### Step 1: Audit Your Grammarly Usage

Note which features you actually use—grammar, tone, plagiarism, or integrations—so you know what to replace.

### Step 2: Create a QuillBot Account

Sign up at QuillBot and explore the free tier first to confirm it meets your needs.

### Step 3: Install the Browser Extension

Add the QuillBot extension to Chrome or your editor of choice so it works across your writing apps.

### Step 4: Recreate Your Workflow

Set your default paraphrasing mode, enable the grammar checker, and test it on a real document.

### Step 5: Cancel Grammarly (Optional)

Once QuillBot covers your needs, cancel your Grammarly subscription from account settings to avoid double billing. Consider running both during a trial period first.

## Tips for a Smooth Transition

- Keep Grammarly active for a week while you adjust.
- Learn QuillBot's paraphrasing modes (Standard, Fluency, Formal).
- Use the summarizer for long research documents.
- Check the integrated citation generator if you write academic content.

## The Bottom Line

Switching from Grammarly to QuillBot makes sense if you value paraphrasing and pricing over Grammarly's grammar coaching and integrations. Test QuillBot's free tier before fully committing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is QuillBot cheaper than Grammarly?

QuillBot's premium plan is typically lower-priced than Grammarly Premium, though features differ.

### Can QuillBot replace Grammarly for grammar?

QuillBot includes a grammar checker, but Grammarly is generally stronger for real-time grammar and tone.

### Can I use both tools together?

Yes—many writers use Grammarly for grammar and QuillBot for paraphrasing during a transition.
