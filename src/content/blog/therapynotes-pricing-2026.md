TherapyNotes is one of the most widely used practice management platforms built specifically for behavioral health. If you're a therapist, counselor, or group practice owner trying to budget for software, the plan names and per-clinician math can be confusing. This **TherapyNotes pricing** guide breaks down exactly what you'll pay in 2026 and who each plan is for.

## TherapyNotes Pricing at a Glance

| Plan | Price (per month) | Best for |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| Solo | $49 / first clinician | Independent therapists |
| Group | $59 / first clinician + per-clinician add-ons | Group practices |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large multi-site organizations |

TherapyNotes does **not** offer a free plan, but it does include a free trial so you can test notes, scheduling, and billing before committing.

## Solo Plan — $49/month

The Solo plan is designed for a single practitioner running their own practice. For $49/month it includes:

- Unlimited electronic notes and treatment plans
- Scheduling with automated appointment reminders
- Integrated billing and insurance claim management
- A secure client portal for intake and telehealth
- To-do lists and document storage

For a solo therapist, this is effectively all-in. The reminder texts and emails are bundled in (a set monthly allotment), which is something competitors often charge extra for.

## Group Plan — $59/month + per-clinician

The Group plan starts at $59/month for the first clinician, then adds a per-clinician fee for each additional provider (with reduced rates for interns and supervisors). It adds:

- Role-based access for clinicians, schedulers, and billers
- Supervisor/intern relationships and co-signing of notes
- Shared scheduling and centralized billing across the practice

This is where TherapyNotes shines: group practices get genuine multi-user workflows rather than a single login shared by the team.

## Enterprise Plan — Custom

For large organizations running multiple locations, the Enterprise tier offers custom pricing, dedicated support, and onboarding assistance. You'll need to contact sales for a quote based on clinician count.

## What's Included vs. What Costs Extra

Most core features are bundled, but watch for:

- **Appointment reminders** beyond the monthly allotment may incur small overage fees
- **Credit card processing** runs through an integrated payment partner with standard transaction rates
- **Electronic claims** are included, but some clearinghouse functions depend on payer setup

See the full plan-by-plan tier comparison on our [TherapyNotes pricing page](/pricing/therapynotes/).

## Is TherapyNotes Worth It in 2026?

For behavioral health specifically, TherapyNotes remains a strong value. The note templates are designed around clinical documentation standards (intake, progress, psychotherapy notes), which generic practice software doesn't match. At $49/month for solo practitioners, it sits in the middle of the market — cheaper than [Jane App](/pricing/jane-app/) for insurance-heavy practices but priced similarly to SimplePractice's mid tier.

If you bill insurance and need rock-solid clinical notes, the price is easy to justify. If you're a wellness coach who doesn't bill insurance, a lighter tool may serve you for less.
