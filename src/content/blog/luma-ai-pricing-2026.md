Luma AI's Dream Machine is one of the top AI video generators in 2026. Here's a complete breakdown of every plan, what you actually get, and whether it's worth the price.

## Luma AI Pricing at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Credits/Month | Video Quality |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 30 credits | 720p, watermarked |
| **Standard** | $23.99/month | 120 credits | 1080p, no watermark |
| **Pro** | $79.99/month | 400 credits | 1080p, priority queue |
| **Premier** | $399.99/month | 2,000 credits | 1080p, highest priority |

Annual billing discount: Save ~20% (Standard drops to ~$19.20/month).

---

## Free Plan — What You Get

The free tier lets you test Luma AI before committing:

- **30 credits/month** (resets monthly)
- **720p resolution**
- **Watermarked output**
- Standard generation queue (can be slow during peak hours)
- Access to all generation modes (text-to-video, image-to-video, video extension)

The free plan is enough to evaluate video quality but not enough for regular content creation.

---

## What Does 1 Credit Cost?

Credit consumption varies by generation type:

| Generation Type | Credits Used |
|---|---|
| 5-second video (standard) | 5 credits |
| 5-second video (HD) | 10 credits |
| Video extension (+5 sec) | 5 credits |
| Image-to-video | 5 credits |

**Standard plan math:** 120 credits ÷ 5 credits per video = **24 standard videos/month** or 12 HD videos.

---

## Standard Plan ($23.99/month)

Best for: Creators who need regular video output without watermarks.

- 120 credits/month
- **1080p resolution**
- No watermark
- Standard queue priority
- Download in MP4

At $23.99/month with 24 standard videos, you're paying roughly **$1 per video clip** — competitive in the AI video market.

---

## Pro Plan ($79.99/month)

Best for: Professional creators, agencies, marketers.

- 400 credits/month
- 1080p resolution
- No watermark
- **Priority queue** — faster generation during peak times
- Commercial use rights included

The jump from Standard to Pro is steep ($56 more for 280 additional credits). It's worth it if queue times matter for your workflow.

---

## Premier Plan ($399.99/month)

Best for: Studios, agencies, and high-volume producers.

- 2,000 credits/month
- Highest priority queue
- Dedicated support
- API access

At $400/month, this is enterprise territory. Most individual creators won't need it.

## Is Luma AI Worth It?

**For creators:** Standard at $23.99/month is reasonable if you produce video content regularly. The 1080p output and no watermark are worth the upgrade from free.

**For businesses:** Pro or Premier only makes sense if you need a high volume of professional video assets. At these price points, compare against hiring a video editor.

**For testing:** The free plan with 30 credits is enough to evaluate whether Luma's quality matches your needs before spending.
