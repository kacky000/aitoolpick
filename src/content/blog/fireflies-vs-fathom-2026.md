Fireflies and Fathom are two of the most-recommended AI meeting assistants, but they aim at different users. Fathom built its reputation on a genuinely free, fast notetaker that just works on calls; Fireflies leans into search, conversation intelligence, and CRM automation for revenue teams. Here's how **Fireflies vs Fathom** compares in 2026.

## Quick Comparison

| | Fireflies | Fathom |
|---|-----------|--------|
| **Strength** | Search + CRM | Free, frictionless notes |
| **Best for** | Sales & CS teams | Solo users, small teams |
| **Integrations** | Deep CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot) | Core CRM + Zapier |
| **Free tier** | Yes (limited minutes) | Yes (generous) |
| **Summaries** | Topic + action items | Clean, fast highlights |

## Where Fathom Wins

Fathom's biggest advantage is how little it asks of you. The free plan is unusually generous, the recording bot is reliable, and summaries land seconds after the call ends. For individuals, founders, and small teams who mostly want "record this call and give me clean notes," Fathom removes nearly all setup friction. There's no learning curve and no pressure to upgrade just to get usable output.

## Where Fireflies Wins

Fireflies treats every meeting as a searchable, analyzable record. Its conversation intelligence — talk-time ratios, topic tracking, and keyword alerts — plus deep CRM integrations make it the stronger pick for sales and customer teams that mine calls for insight weeks later. If your value comes from logging activity automatically and searching across hundreds of past calls, Fireflies pulls ahead. See the [Fireflies pricing details](/pricing/fireflies-ai/) or the full [Fireflies review](/blog/fireflies-ai-review-2026/).

## Pricing

Fathom's free tier covers most solo use, with paid plans adding team features and advanced AI. Fireflies offers a free tier with monthly transcription limits and prices its CRM and conversation-intelligence features into higher tiers. If budget is the deciding factor and you work alone, Fathom is hard to beat; if you're equipping a sales org, Fireflies' paid value scales better.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Choose Fathom** if you want the most generous free notetaker with zero friction.
- **Choose Fireflies** if you need searchable history, analytics, and CRM automation.
