Superhuman built its name on being the fastest email client money can buy — and in 2026 it has layered serious AI on top of that speed. But at **$30/month with no free tier**, it's also one of the priciest **AI email clients** available. This **Superhuman review** breaks down what you get and whether it's worth it.

## What Is Superhuman?

Superhuman is a premium email client for Gmail and Outlook focused on speed, keyboard control, and AI assistance. It's designed for people who process huge volumes of email and want to move through their inbox as fast as humanly possible — hence the famous onboarding that coaches you on shortcuts.

## Key Features

- **Blazing speed** — sub-100ms interactions, keyboard-driven everything
- **Superhuman AI** — drafting, summaries, and inbox queries
- **Auto Drafts** — AI pre-writes replies in your voice (Business plan)
- **Ask AI** — natural-language search across your whole inbox (Business plan)
- **Split inbox** — separate important mail from noise
- **Calendar & scheduling** — built-in, no extra app
- **Productivity coaching** — guided habits to hit inbox zero

## Pricing

Superhuman has **no free plan**. Starter is **$30/month** ($25 billed annually), and Business is **$40/month** with the heavier AI and CRM features (Auto Drafts, Ask AI, HubSpot/Salesforce). Enterprise is custom.

For the full tier-by-tier analysis, see our [Superhuman pricing guide](/blog/superhuman-pricing-2026/).

## Pros

- **Genuinely the fastest** — nothing else feels this responsive
- **Polished AI** — drafting and summaries are reliable and well-integrated
- **Gmail and Outlook** — works for both ecosystems
- **Great for high volume** — the more email you get, the more it pays off

## Cons

- **Expensive** — $30/mo is several times the cost of rivals
- **No free tier** — you can't try it long-term for free
- **Overkill for light users** — modest email volume doesn't justify the price

## Who Should Use Superhuman?

- **High-volume professionals** — execs, founders, salespeople, recruiters
- **Speed obsessives** — anyone who feels slowed down by their current client
- **Outlook or Gmail power users** who'll pay for polish

If your volume is modest or you're price-sensitive, a tool like Shortwave delivers most of the value for far less.

## Bottom Line

Superhuman is the best AI email client for **speed-obsessed, high-volume professionals** who'll happily pay a premium. The AI is polished and the speed is unmatched — but at $30/month with no free tier, it's hard to justify unless email is a core part of your day. Everyone else should start with a cheaper alternative.
