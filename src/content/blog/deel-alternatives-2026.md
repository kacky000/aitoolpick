Deel is the market leader for global hiring, but it's not always the best fit. Here are the top Deel alternatives in 2026.

## Why Look for a Deel Alternative?

- **Price**: $599/employee/month (EOR) or $49/contractor/month can add up
- **Complexity**: Deel's feature set is powerful but can be overwhelming
- **Support**: Some users report slower response times at scale
- **Specific needs**: Better HRIS integration, specific country coverage, or simpler contractor management

## Top Deel Alternatives

### 1. Remote — Best Alternative for EOR

Remote is the closest competitor to Deel and often beats it on customer support and entity ownership.

**Pricing:** $599/employee/month (EOR), $29/contractor/month (basic free)

**Why choose Remote over Deel:**
- Owned legal entities in more countries (no third-party partners)
- Stronger IP protection clauses by default
- Better customer support NPS scores
- Simpler pricing (fewer add-ons)

**Why Deel might still win:**
- Deel covers 150+ countries vs. Remote's 80+
- Deel's free HRIS (Deel HR) is included
- Better crypto payroll options

[Remote vs. Deel full comparison →](/blog/remote-vs-deel-2026/)

### 2. Oyster HR — Best Value Alternative

Oyster HR is $100/month cheaper than Deel for EOR and focuses on ethical, sustainable global employment.

**Pricing:** $499/employee/month (EOR), $29/contractor/month

**Why choose Oyster:**
- $100/month savings per employee
- Better Greenhouse and BambooHR integrations
- "Thoughtful Employer" framework for equitable global benefits
- Good for EU-heavy hiring

**Limitations:** Smaller country coverage (130 vs. 150+), less brand recognition for contractors.

### 3. Rippling — Best if You Need Full HR Stack

Rippling isn't just an EOR — it's an all-in-one workforce platform that handles US payroll, global EOR, IT, and benefits in one system.

**Pricing:** ~$8/user/month base + EOR module (custom)

**Why choose Rippling:**
- Single platform eliminates the need for separate HRIS
- Best automated onboarding/offboarding
- Unique IT management integration (Jamf-level device management)
- Fastest growing HR platform

**Limitations:** EOR country coverage is narrower; more expensive overall if you need EOR only.

[Full Rippling review →](/blog/rippling-review-2026/)

### 4. Papaya Global — Best for Enterprise Compliance

Papaya Global serves enterprise clients with complex multi-country payroll and compliance needs.

**Pricing:** ~$650/employee/month (EOR), custom for enterprise

**Why choose Papaya:**
- Best enterprise-grade compliance engine
- 160+ country coverage
- Strong workforce intelligence reporting
- Integrates with SAP, Workday, Oracle

**Limitations:** Higher price point, primarily suited for companies with 200+ employees.

### 5. Horizons — Best for Contractor + EOR Combo

Horizons (formerly Horizons Global) is a newer player offering competitive EOR pricing with strong contractor management.

**Pricing:** $499/employee/month (EOR), $49/contractor/month

**Why choose Horizons:**
- Competitive $499 EOR pricing
- Good for companies mixing contractors and employees
- Faster onboarding than larger platforms

### 6. Multiplier — Best for APAC Hiring

Multiplier focuses on Asia-Pacific markets where other EORs have weaker coverage.

**Pricing:** $400/employee/month (EOR in some markets), custom for others

**Why choose Multiplier:**
- Strongest coverage in Southeast Asia (Singapore, India, Philippines)
- Competitive pricing for APAC
- Built-in benefits and insurance in APAC markets

**Limitations:** Less robust in North America and Europe.

### 7. Plane — Best Budget Contractor Tool

Plane (formerly Pilot) focuses on contractor payments and compliance at a much lower price than Deel.

**Pricing:** $39/contractor/month

**Why choose Plane:**
- Much cheaper than Deel for contractor-only use cases
- Simple, clean interface
- Good for startups with many freelancers

**Limitations:** Limited EOR capabilities — not a full Deel replacement.

## Quick Decision Guide

| Your Situation | Best Alternative |
|----------------|-----------------|

## The Bottom Line

Deel is excellent but not the only option. If you're hiring in 80 countries or fewer, **Remote** matches Deel's EOR quality with better support. If budget matters, **Oyster** saves $1,200/year per employee. If you're building a global team and want everything in one platform, **Rippling** is worth the premium.
