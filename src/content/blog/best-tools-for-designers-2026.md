Designers juggle mockups, feedback rounds, asset management, and client communication daily. Here are the tools that actually make that workflow smoother in 2026.

## Design & Prototyping

### Figma — Best for UI/UX Design Teams
- **Price:** Free / $15/editor/month
- **Why designers love it:** Real-time collaboration, Dev Mode for handoff, massive plugin library, auto-layout
- **Best for:** Product design teams, UI/UX designers, design systems

Figma dominates collaborative interface design. If you work with developers, the Dev Mode alone justifies the price.

[Compare Figma with alternatives →](/alternatives/figma/)

### Canva — Best for Marketing Design
- **Price:** Free / $15/user/month (Pro)
- **Why designers love it:** 500K+ templates, drag-and-drop simplicity, Brand Kit, Magic Resize
- **Best for:** Social media managers, marketers, non-designers who need quality output

Canva won't replace Figma for product design, but for marketing materials, presentations, and social posts, it's unbeatable.

[Compare Canva with alternatives →](/alternatives/canva/)

## Collaboration & Whiteboarding

### Miro — Best for Design Workshops
- **Price:** Free / $8/member/month
- **Why designers love it:** Infinite canvas, workshop templates, voting and timer for design sprints
- **Best for:** Design thinking workshops, user journey mapping, brainstorming sessions

[Compare Miro vs Figma →](/compare/figma-vs-miro/)

## Project Management

### Notion — Best for Design Documentation
- **Price:** Free / $10/user/month
- **Why designers love it:** Design brief templates, project databases, wiki for design systems documentation
- **Best for:** Organizing design projects, maintaining style guides, team knowledge bases

### Linear — Best for Design-Dev Collaboration
- **Price:** Free / $8/user/month
- **Why designers love it:** Clean UI (designers appreciate good design), Git integration, fast keyboard shortcuts
- **Best for:** Product teams where designers and developers share a workflow

[Compare Notion vs Linear →](/compare/linear-vs-notion/)

## AI Assistants

### Claude — Best for Design Writing
- **Price:** Free / $20/month
- **Why designers love it:** Excellent at writing UX copy, generating microcopy variations, analyzing user research
- **Best for:** UX writing, design documentation, research synthesis

### ChatGPT — Best for Design Ideation
- **Price:** Free / $20/month
- **Why designers love it:** Image generation (DALL-E), brainstorming partner, competitive analysis
- **Best for:** Mood boards, concept exploration, generating placeholder content
