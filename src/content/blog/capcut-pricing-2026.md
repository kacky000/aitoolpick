**[CapCut](/tools/capcut/)** is free for most editing, with a Pro tier that unlocks AI features, higher exports, and a cloud library. The hard part isn't the price — it's understanding which features are genuinely free, which are Pro-gated, and the commercial-use rules that catch businesses off guard. Here's the full breakdown.

## CapCut Plans and Pricing in 2026

| Plan | Price | Right for |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Casual editing, learning |
| Pro | ~$10/month or ~$75/year | Frequent creators, businesses |
| Commercial / Enterprise | Custom | Brands, agencies |

Pro is billed monthly or annually (annual saves ~35%). CapCut is available on mobile, desktop, and web, and your Pro subscription works across all of them.

## Free Plan: Surprisingly Complete

CapCut's free tier is one of the most capable free video editors available:

- Full timeline editing, multi-track
- Huge library of free templates, effects, transitions
- Auto-captions and basic text-to-speech
- Up to 1080p export, no forced watermark on most exports
- Basic AI tools

For personal social content — TikTok, Reels, Shorts — the free plan covers the vast majority of what creators need. Most people never strictly *need* Pro for personal use.

## Pro Plan (~$10/month)

Pro targets volume creators and unlocks the AI-heavy features:

- **Advanced AI tools** — AI background removal, AI effects, more powerful text-to-speech voices
- 4K / higher-bitrate exports
- Cloud storage and asset library across devices
- Premium templates, effects, fonts, and stock assets
- No ads, priority rendering
- More AI generation credits

If you publish daily, the time saved by Pro's AI background removal, auto-editing, and premium template access pays for itself quickly. For occasional editing, the free plan is enough.

## The Commercial-Use Catch (Read This)

This is the part that surprises businesses. Many of CapCut's templates, music tracks, effects, and stock assets are **licensed for personal use only**. Using them in branded or monetized content can create licensing issues even on a paid Pro plan.

For commercial work you need to:
- Use only assets explicitly cleared for commercial use, or
- Bring your own licensed music and footage, and
- Review CapCut's commercial terms for your region

Brands and agencies should treat CapCut's stock library cautiously and rely on owned or properly licensed assets.

## The Hidden Cost: Subscription vs One-Time Tools

CapCut Pro is a recurring subscription. Over two years at ~$75/year you've spent ~$150 — close to a one-time DaVinci Resolve Studio license ($295) that never expires. For creators who edit constantly and value CapCut's social templates, the subscription is justified. For occasional editors, the free plan plus a one-time tool can be cheaper long-term.

## Is CapCut Worth the Price?

**For personal social content: stay free.** The free plan handles TikTok, Reels, and Shorts editing for the vast majority of creators.

**Free → Pro is worth it** if you publish frequently and use AI background removal, premium templates, or 4K export regularly. The time savings justify ~$10/month for daily creators.

**For businesses: proceed carefully.** Pro removes feature limits but does *not* automatically grant commercial rights to every asset. Budget for licensed music/footage separately, or you risk takedowns on monetized content.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is CapCut free?

Yes. CapCut's free plan includes full timeline editing, templates, auto-captions, and up to 1080p export with no forced watermark on most exports. Pro adds AI tools, 4K, and cloud storage.

### Can I use CapCut for commercial / business videos?

You can edit commercially, but many CapCut templates, music tracks, and stock assets are licensed for personal use only — even on Pro. For branded content, use only commercially cleared assets or bring your own licensed media.

### Is CapCut Pro worth it?

For frequent creators, yes — AI background removal, premium templates, and 4K export save significant time for ~$10/month. Occasional editors are usually fine on the free plan.
