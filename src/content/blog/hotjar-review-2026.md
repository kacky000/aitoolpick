# Hotjar Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Behavior analytics tool with heatmaps, session recordings, and surveys to understand how users interact with your site. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore its features, pricing, and how it compares to alternatives in the analytics space.

## What is Hotjar?

Behavior analytics tool with heatmaps, session recordings, and surveys to understand how users interact with your site. Founded in 2014, it has established itself as a significant player in the analytics category. With a user rating of 4.4/5, it primarily serves Ux Designers, Product Managers, Marketers, Conversion Optimizers.

## Key Features

- **Heatmaps**
- **Session Recordings**
- **Surveys**
- **Feedback Widgets**
- **Funnels**
- **User Interviews**
- **Dashboards**

## Pricing

Hotjar offers the following pricing structure:

- **Free Plan**: Available with basic features
- **Plus**: $32/month
- **Business**: $80/month
- **Scale**: $171/month

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Heatmaps show exactly where users click
- Session recordings reveal UX issues
- Easy to install (one script tag)
- Surveys and feedback widgets included

### Cons

- Can slow down site if not configured well
- Session recordings take time to review
- Limited to web — no mobile app analytics

## Who is Hotjar Best For?

Hotjar is ideal for Ux Designers, Product Managers, Marketers, Conversion Optimizers. If you fall into any of these categories, Hotjar deserves serious consideration for your workflow.
