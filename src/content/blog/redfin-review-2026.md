# Redfin Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Redfin is technology-powered real estate brokerage offering lower listing fees, detailed market data, and a modern home search experience with salaried agents. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Redfin?

Redfin is technology-powered real estate brokerage offering lower listing fees, detailed market data, and a modern home search experience with salaried agents. It is designed for home buyers, home sellers, cost conscious sellers and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the real estate space.

## Pros

- Lower listing fees than traditional brokerages (1-1.5%)
- Highly accurate home value estimates (Redfin Estimate)

## Cons
