Replit and Windsurf both put an AI agent at the center of coding, but they target different workflows. Replit is a browser-based platform that builds *and hosts* your app in one place. Windsurf (formerly Codeium) is a local agentic IDE that lives on your machine with your own toolchain. Choosing in 2026 comes down to whether you want an all-in-one cloud workshop or a powerful agent inside a familiar desktop editor.

## Quick Comparison

| | Replit | Windsurf |
|---|--------|----------|
| Where it runs | Browser / cloud | Local desktop IDE |
| AI agent | Replit Agent | Cascade |
| Hosting | Built-in, always-on | None (your own) |
| Paid entry | Core $20/mo | Pro $20/mo |
| Best for | Build + deploy in one place | Local dev with agentic AI |

## Replit: Build and Ship in the Cloud

Replit removes setup entirely — open a browser, prompt the Agent, and deploy to always-on hosting without touching a terminal:

- **Starter (Free):** browser IDE, short Agent tasks
- **Core ($20/mo):** $20 of usage credits, uninterrupted Agent runs, always-on deploys
- **Pro ($100/mo flat):** for teams up to ~15

The killer feature is that building and hosting happen in the same place. Details in our [Replit pricing breakdown](/blog/replit-pricing-2026/) and [Replit free vs paid guide](/blog/replit-free-vs-paid-2026/).

## Windsurf: Agentic AI in Your Local IDE

Windsurf brings its Cascade agent into a desktop editor, so you keep your local files, extensions, and environment:

- **Free:** 25 prompt credits/month, 5 Cascade sessions/day, unlimited Tab autocomplete
- **Pro ($20/mo):** unlimited Cascade within quota, all models
- **Pro Plus ($35/mo):** priority access to flagship models
- **Teams ($40/user)**

Windsurf raised Pro to $20 in its March 2026 quota overhaul. Full details in [Windsurf free vs paid](/blog/windsurf-free-vs-paid-2026/). Unlike Replit, you bring your own hosting and toolchain.

## Pricing Showdown

| Scenario | Replit | Windsurf |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Free trial of the agent | Starter (free) | Free (5 Cascade/day) |
| Solo developer | $20/mo (Core) | $20/mo (Pro) |
| Heavy daily use | $30–50 (credits) | $35 (Pro Plus) |
| Small team | $100/mo flat (≤15) | $40/user (Teams) |

At $20 the entry plans match, but the value differs: Replit's includes hosting; Windsurf's plugs into your existing local setup.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Want zero setup and instant deploys?** Replit.
- **Prefer your local files, tools, and editor?** Windsurf.
- **Need built-in hosting?** Replit — Windsurf has none.
- **Already have a polished local dev environment?** Windsurf's Cascade slots right in.
- **Small team that just wants to ship?** Replit Pro's flat $100 beats per-seat for ≤15 people.

## Verdict

**Replit is a cloud workshop; Windsurf is a local power tool.** If you value building and hosting in one browser tab with no configuration, Replit wins. If you want an aggressive AI agent inside the desktop IDE and toolchain you already trust, Windsurf is the better fit. ## FAQ

**Does Windsurf host my app like Replit?** No — Windsurf is a local IDE with no hosting; Replit includes always-on deployment.

**Are they the same price?** Entry tiers are both $20/mo, but Replit Core includes usage credits and hosting while Windsurf Pro is quota-based local AI.

**Which has a better free plan?** Both are usable — Replit for short cloud builds, Windsurf for 5 daily Cascade sessions plus unlimited autocomplete.

**Can I use both?** Yes — some developers prototype in Replit and do heavier local work in Windsurf.
