# Slack Pricing 2026: Complete Plan Breakdown and Comparison

Slack remains one of the most popular workplace communication tools, but its pricing structure can be confusing. This guide breaks down every Slack plan in 2026 so you can pick the right one without overpaying.

## Slack Pricing at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Message History | File Storage | Key Features |
|------|-------|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 90 days | 5 GB total | 1:1 huddles, 10 integrations |
| **Pro** | $8.75/user/month | Unlimited | 10 GB/user | Group huddles, unlimited integrations |
| **Business+** | $12.50/user/month | Unlimited | 20 GB/user | SAML SSO, data exports, 24/7 support |
| **Enterprise Grid** | Custom pricing | Unlimited | 1 TB/user | Unlimited workspaces, HIPAA compliance |

*Prices shown are annual billing. Monthly billing is approximately 25% higher.*

## Free Plan: What You Actually Get

Slack's free plan is generous enough for small teams and side projects:

- **90-day message history** (increased from the old 10K message limit)
- **5 GB total file storage** for the entire workspace
- **1:1 huddles** (audio calls with screen sharing)
- **10 app integrations** (Slack Connect with one external org)
- **Basic search** within the 90-day window

**Best for**: Small teams (under 10 people), personal projects, or testing Slack before committing to a paid plan.

**Limitations to watch**: The 90-day message limit means you'll lose valuable context over time. The 10-integration cap is restrictive if you use multiple SaaS tools.

## Pro Plan ($8.75/user/month): The Sweet Spot

The Pro plan removes the most painful free plan limitations:

- **Unlimited message history** — never lose a conversation
- **10 GB storage per user** — enough for most teams
- **Group huddles** with up to 50 participants
- **Unlimited integrations** — connect every tool in your stack
- **Slack Connect** with unlimited external organizations
- **Custom workflows** with Workflow Builder

**Best for**: Growing teams of 10-100 people who need reliable communication without enterprise complexity.

**Cost example**: A 25-person team pays $218.75/month ($2,625/year) on annual billing.

## Business+ Plan ($12.50/user/month): Compliance and Control

Business+ adds the features that IT and compliance teams require:

- **SAML-based SSO** for centralized authentication
- **Data loss prevention** integrations
- **Compliance data exports** for regulatory requirements
- **24/7 support** with 4-hour first response time
- **Custom retention policies** for messages and files
- **Domain claiming** to manage all Slack accounts on your domain

**Best for**: Companies with 100-500 employees, especially those in regulated industries needing SSO and compliance features.

**Cost example**: A 100-person team pays $1,250/month ($15,000/year) on annual billing.

## Enterprise Grid: For Large Organizations

Enterprise Grid is Slack's offering for large, complex organizations:

- **Unlimited workspaces** organized under one umbrella
- **Cross-workspace channels** and DMs
- **HIPAA compliance** support
- **99.99% uptime SLA**
- **Custom branding** and data residency options
- **Dedicated customer success team**

**Best for**: Organizations with 500+ employees or those requiring HIPAA compliance.

**Pricing**: Custom quotes only. Expect $15-25/user/month depending on volume.

## Hidden Costs to Consider

### 1. App Add-ons
Some Slack apps charge separately (e.g., Salesforce integration, Polly surveys).

### 2. Slack AI
Slack AI is an add-on at $10/user/month that adds AI-powered search summaries, channel recaps, and thread summaries. It's not included in any standard plan.

### 3. Overages
File storage overages aren't charged automatically — Slack will prompt you to upgrade or clean up. But running out of storage can be disruptive.

## How to Save on Slack

1. **Annual billing** saves approximately 25% compared to monthly
2. **Audit active users** quarterly — remove inactive accounts
3. **Start with Pro** — most teams don't need Business+ features
4. **Use free plan strategically** for cross-team or external workspaces
5. **Negotiate Enterprise pricing** — Slack offers discounts for multi-year commitments

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

- **Solo or tiny team (1-5)**: Free plan is sufficient
- **Small team (5-25)**: Pro plan — the best value
- **Mid-size (25-100)**: Pro plan unless you need SSO (then Business+)
- **Large company (100-500)**: Business+ for compliance and SSO
- **Enterprise (500+)**: Enterprise Grid for multi-workspace management

## FAQ

### Is Slack free plan enough for a startup?
For early-stage startups with under 10 people, yes. Upgrade to Pro when you hit the integration limit or need message history beyond 90 days.

### Does Slack offer nonprofit pricing?
Yes. Eligible nonprofits get 85% off the Pro plan. Apply through Slack's nonprofit program.

### Can I switch plans mid-billing cycle?
Yes. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Is Slack AI worth the extra $10/user/month?
For teams that spend significant time searching through Slack history, the AI summaries can save hours weekly. Try the free trial before committing.
