**[Asana](/tools/asana/)** and **[ClickUp](/tools/clickup/)** are two of the biggest names in project management, and they compete directly for the same teams. On price, though, they're not close — ClickUp undercuts Asana at every paid tier, and the gap on a real team budget is significant.

This guide compares every plan, the free-tier limits that matter most, and exactly how much each tool costs a growing team.

## Pricing Overview

### Asana Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Right For |
|------|-------|----------|
| Personal (Free) | $0 | Individuals, up to 2 collaborators |
| Starter | $10.99/user/mo (annual) | Small teams |
| Advanced | $24.99/user/mo (annual) | Teams needing reporting & automation |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large organizations |

### ClickUp Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Billed As | Right For |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| Free Forever | $0 | — | Individuals, small teams |
| Unlimited | $7/user/mo | Annually ($10 monthly) | Small to mid-size teams |
| Business | $12/user/mo | Annually | Mid-size teams, advanced needs |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Large org security |

## The Key Difference: Free Plan Caps and Per-Seat Price

Two things drive this comparison.

**The free plan.** Asana's Personal (free) plan caps collaboration at **2 users** — the moment you need a third person, you're forced to upgrade. ClickUp's Free Forever plan allows **unlimited members**, so a small team can run on it indefinitely.

**The per-seat price.** Asana Starter is **$10.99/user/month**; ClickUp Unlimited is **$7/user/month**. That's a 36% lower entry price for ClickUp, with a comparable or deeper feature set.

For cost-sensitive teams, both factors point the same direction.

## Cost Scenario: A 10-Person Team

- **Asana Starter**: 10 × $10.99 = **$109.90/month**
- **ClickUp Unlimited**: 10 × $7 = **$70/month**

ClickUp saves **~$40/month ($480/year)** at the entry tier for the same team size. Step up to mid-tier:

- **Asana Advanced**: 10 × $24.99 = **$249.90/month**
- **ClickUp Business**: 10 × $12 = **$120/month**

The mid-tier gap is even larger — ClickUp Business costs less than half of Asana Advanced.

## Free Plan Comparison

- **Asana Personal (Free)**: Unlimited tasks and projects, but **capped at 2 users** — the dealbreaker for any real team.
- **ClickUp Free Forever**: Unlimited members and tasks, 100 automations/month, 5 spaces, 60MB storage.

If you're a team of three or more and want to stay free as long as possible, ClickUp is the only one of the two that lets you.

## Where AI Pricing Differs

- **ClickUp Brain** is a **$5/user/month add-on** on any paid plan.
- **Asana** bundles its AI features into paid tiers, but its higher base price means the all-in cost can still land above ClickUp Unlimited + Brain.

Factor the Brain add-on in if AI matters — even then, ClickUp Unlimited ($7) + Brain ($5) = $12/user roughly matches Asana Starter alone, while including far more.

## Verdict

**Choose ClickUp** if price is a real factor. It's cheaper at every tier, its free plan supports unlimited members, and its feature depth matches or exceeds Asana's. For most cost-conscious teams, it's the better value.

**Choose Asana** if you prefer its cleaner, more opinionated interface and your team finds it easier to adopt. The simplicity is real — you're paying a premium for a more guided experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Asana or ClickUp cheaper?

ClickUp is cheaper at every tier. Its entry plan is $7/user vs Asana's $10.99, and ClickUp Business ($12) costs less than half of Asana Advanced ($24.99).

### How many users does Asana's free plan allow?

Asana's current Personal (free) plan caps collaboration at 2 users. (Legacy accounts created before late 2025 may retain a higher 10-user limit.)

### Does ClickUp's free plan limit team size?

No. ClickUp Free Forever allows unlimited members, so small teams can use it indefinitely — unlike Asana's 2-user cap.

### Which has better value for a growing team?

ClickUp, on price. A 10-person team pays $70/month on ClickUp Unlimited vs $109.90 on Asana Starter — about $480/year in savings.
