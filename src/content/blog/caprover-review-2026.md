CapRover is open-source paas (platform as a service) for deploying applications with one-click apps, automatic ssl, docker support, and nginx load balancing on your own servers. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is CapRover?

CapRover is open-source paas (platform as a service) for deploying applications with one-click apps, automatic ssl, docker support, and nginx load balancing on your own servers. It is designed for indie developers, self hosters, startups and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the devops space.
