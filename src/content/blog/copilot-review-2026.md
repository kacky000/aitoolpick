Microsoft Copilot is microsoft's ai assistant integrated across windows, edge, and microsoft 365 apps for chat, search, and content creation. It is designed for microsoft 365 users, enterprise, students and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the ai chatbot space.

## Pros

- Free access to GPT-4 via Bing integration
- Deep integration with Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, Teams)
- Real-time web search with citations
- Image generation with DALL-E 3 included

## Cons

- Microsoft 365 Copilot requires enterprise license
- Less customizable than ChatGPT
- Responses can be overly cautious or generic
