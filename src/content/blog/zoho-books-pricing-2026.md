**[Zoho Books](/tools/zoho-books/)** is the accounting tool that quietly undercuts the big names — it has a genuine **free plan** and scales affordably for businesses already in the Zoho ecosystem. Here's exactly what Zoho Books costs in 2026, what each tier includes, and whether the free plan is enough.

## Zoho Books Pricing in 2026

As of March 1, 2026, US monthly pricing:

| Plan | Monthly cost | Best for |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Free | **$0** | Businesses under the revenue threshold |
| Standard | **$15/mo** | Freelancers and small businesses |
| Professional | **$40/mo** | Growing teams needing automation |
| Premium | **$60/mo** | Established businesses with complex needs |

Annual billing knocks roughly 10–20% off the monthly rates above.

## What the Free Plan Includes

Zoho Books' free tier is unusually complete: invoicing, expense tracking, bank reconciliation, and core reports — available to businesses **under an annual revenue threshold**. For early-stage freelancers and micro-businesses, it's one of the best no-cost options alongside [Wave](/blog/wave-pricing-2026). See our [best free accounting software](/blog/best-free-accounting-software-2026) roundup.

## What You Get as You Scale

- **Standard ($15):** More users, automated workflows, project billing
- **Professional ($40):** Purchase orders, sales orders, inventory, deeper automation
- **Premium ($60):** Custom domains, advanced analytics, vendor portal

The biggest reason to choose Zoho Books over rivals is the **Zoho ecosystem** — if you run Zoho CRM, Mail, or Inventory, the data flows automatically, removing integration headaches.

## Smart Buyer Tips

1. **Start free** if you're under the revenue cap — it's fully functional.
2. **Only pay for Professional** if you need inventory or sales orders.
3. **Maximize the value** by adopting other Zoho apps you already need.
4. **Prepay annually** for the 10–20% discount.

## The Bottom Line

Zoho Books in 2026 is the **best-value accounting tool for Zoho users and budget-conscious small businesses** — a real free plan, a $15 paid entry, and clean scaling to $60. If you're not in the Zoho ecosystem, weigh it against [QuickBooks alternatives](/blog/best-quickbooks-alternatives-2026), but on raw price-to-feature, Zoho Books is hard to beat.

Yes — Zoho Books offers a genuinely free plan for businesses under an annual revenue threshold, including invoicing, expenses, and reports. Above the cap, plans start at $15/mo.

### How much does Zoho Books cost in 2026?

Free $0, Standard $15/mo, Professional $40/mo, and Premium $60/mo as of March 1, 2026. Annual billing reduces these by roughly 10–20%.
