Krisp and Otter both make meetings better with AI, but they solve different problems. Krisp is best known for **real-time noise cancellation** that strips background sound from your calls. Otter.ai is a **transcription and meeting-notes** tool that turns conversations into searchable text and summaries. The good news: they're not mutually exclusive — but if you're picking one, here's how they compare in 2026.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Krisp | Otter.ai |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Core job** | Noise cancellation | Transcription & notes |
| **Real-time transcript** | Yes (added) | Yes |
| **Meeting summaries** | Yes (AI notes) | Yes (strong) |
| **Noise removal** | Best-in-class | None |
| **Works across apps** | Any audio app | Zoom/Meet/Teams + upload |
| **Best for** | Call clarity | Records & action items |

## Where Krisp Wins

Krisp's noise cancellation is its standout. It sits at the **system audio layer**, so it cleans up your microphone (and even incoming audio) in *any* app — Zoom, Meet, Teams, Discord, or a phone call through your computer. For people working from noisy environments, it's the clearest way to sound professional. Krisp has since added AI meeting notes and transcription too, making it more of an all-in-one call assistant. See its plans on the [Krisp pricing page](/pricing/krisp-ai).

## Where Otter Wins

Otter is the deeper **note-taker**. Its transcription is fast and accurate, it auto-joins meetings, and it produces strong summaries with action items you can search and share. Teams that need a reliable record of every conversation — sales calls, interviews, standups — get more from Otter's transcript-first design and collaboration features. See the [Otter.ai pricing breakdown](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026/) for tier details.

## The Overlap

Both tools now offer real-time transcripts and AI summaries, so there's growing overlap. The deciding question is which **core** capability you can't live without: pristine audio (Krisp) or a thorough, searchable record (Otter). Many heavy meeting users actually run Krisp for clean audio *and* Otter for notes.

## Pricing

Both offer free tiers with usage caps and step up to paid plans for unlimited use and team features. Krisp meters by minutes of noise cancellation and notes; Otter by transcription minutes per month. Estimate your weekly meeting load before choosing a tier.

## Which Should You Choose?

- **Choose Krisp** if call clarity and removing background noise is the priority.
- **Choose Otter** if accurate transcripts, summaries, and action items matter most.
