You don't need a subscription to keep clean books. A handful of tools offer **genuinely free accounting** — not crippled trials — that cover invoicing, expense tracking, and reporting. Here are the **best free accounting software options in 2026**, and the exact point where free stops being enough.

## The Free Picks

| Tool | Free plan covers | Paid upgrade | Best for |
|------|------------------|--------------|----------|
| **[Wave](/tools/wave/)** | Accounting + unlimited invoices | Pro $16/mo | Freelancers, micro-businesses |
| **[Zoho Books](/tools/zoho-books/)** | Full books under revenue cap | Standard $15/mo | Zoho users, small businesses |

Note: **[QuickBooks](/tools/quickbooks/)**, **[Xero](/tools/xero/)**, and **[FreshBooks](/tools/freshbooks/)** have no permanent free plan — only trials. If free is non-negotiable, Wave and Zoho Books are your real options.

## 1. Wave — Best Free Accounting Overall

Wave is the rare tool that gives you **real double-entry accounting and unlimited invoicing at $0**, with no client caps. You only pay for add-ons: payments processing, payroll, or the **Pro plan ($16/mo)** for automated bank imports and receipt scanning.

- **Free includes:** Accounting, unlimited invoices/estimates, dashboards
- **Costs extra:** Payroll, payment processing, automated imports (Pro)
- **Best for:** Freelancers, side hustlers, micro-businesses

Full details in the [Wave pricing guide](/blog/wave-pricing-2026) and [Wave review](/blog/wave-review-2026).

## 2. Zoho Books — Best Free for Growing Businesses

Zoho Books offers a **free plan for businesses under an annual revenue threshold**, with surprisingly complete features. When you cross the cap, Standard is just **$15/mo** — and if you already run Zoho CRM or Mail, everything connects.

- **Free includes:** Invoicing, expenses, reports (under revenue cap)
- **Costs extra:** Higher volume, advanced automation
- **Best for:** Zoho ecosystem users, small businesses

See the [Zoho Books pricing guide](/blog/zoho-books-pricing-2026).

## When Free Stops Being Enough

Upgrade (or move to a paid tool) when you hit any of these:

1. **You need payroll** — free tiers don't include it.
2. **You need automated bank feeds** — Wave gates this behind Pro.
3. **You cross Zoho's revenue cap** — the free plan expires by design.
4. **You need multi-currency or inventory** — that's [Xero](/blog/xero-pricing-2026) territory.
5. **Your accountant wants QuickBooks** — see [QuickBooks vs Xero](/blog/quickbooks-vs-xero-2026).

## The Bottom Line

**Wave is the best free accounting software for 2026** — real books, unlimited invoicing, zero cost. **Zoho Books** wins if you live in the Zoho suite. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there truly free accounting software?

Yes. Wave offers free double-entry accounting and unlimited invoicing with no trial expiry. Zoho Books has a free plan for businesses under a revenue threshold.

### Is Wave really free?

Wave's core accounting and invoicing are free forever. You only pay for payment processing, payroll, or the Pro plan ($16/mo) for automated bank imports.

### What's the catch with free accounting software?

Free tiers typically exclude payroll, automated bank feeds, and premium support, and some (like Zoho Books) cap usage by revenue. For most freelancers, the free features are more than enough to start.
