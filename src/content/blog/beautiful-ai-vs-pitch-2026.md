Both [Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) and [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) aim to replace PowerPoint and Google Slides with modern, better-designed alternatives. But they take fundamentally different approaches: Beautiful.ai uses AI to ensure every slide looks professional automatically, while Pitch focuses on real-time team collaboration with design-quality templates. Here's how they compare in 2026.

## Core Philosophy

**Beautiful.ai**: "You can't [make](/tools/make/) an ugly slide." Smart templates automatically adjust layouts, spacing, and alignment as you add content. The AI prevents design mistakes.

**Pitch**: "Design-quality presentations, built together." Professional templates with full creative control and real-time collaboration that feels like [Google Docs](/tools/google-docs/) for presentations.

## Pricing Comparison

| Plan | Beautiful.ai | Pitch |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | No free plan | Yes (unlimited presentations) |
| Individual/Pro | $12/user/mo | $8/user/mo |
| Team | $40/user/mo | $19/user/mo (Business) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

**Winner: Pitch** — Has a free plan AND cheaper paid plans at every tier. Beautiful.ai's lack of a free plan is a notable gap.

## Design Quality

### Beautiful.ai

Every slide uses "smart" templates that:
- Automatically adjust spacing when you add/remove items
- Maintain alignment and proportional sizing
- Apply consistent color and typography rules
- Prevent common design mistakes (overcrowding, misalignment, poor contrast)

The result: consistently attractive [slides](/tools/slides-com/) even from non-designers. The downside: less creative control. You work within the template's constraints.

### Pitch

Templates are beautifully designed but give you full control:
- Move elements freely (no auto-adjusting constraints)
- Custom fonts, colors, and layouts
- More animation options
- Greater flexibility for unique designs

The result: higher creative ceiling but also higher possibility of making design mistakes. Suited for teams with some design sense.

**Winner: Beautiful.ai** for non-designers who want guaranteed good-looking [slides](/tools/slides-com/). **Pitch** for teams with design capability who want creative freedom.

## Collaboration

### Beautiful.ai

- Shared workspaces
- Comments on slides
- Version history
- Presentation sharing via link
- Viewer analytics

### Pitch

- **Real-time** collaborative editing (like Google Docs)
- Live cursors showing who's editing what
- Video recording and embedding
- Comments and assignments
- Version history with named versions
- Presentation analytics (who viewed what, for how long)

**Winner: Pitch** — The real-time collaboration is significantly more advanced. If your team builds decks together (not just one person creating and sharing), Pitch's collaboration is vastly superior.

## AI Features

### Beautiful.ai

- **DesignerBot**: Generates entire presentations from a text prompt
- **Smart layouts**: AI-driven auto-formatting (the core product)
- **Content suggestions**: AI recommends text improvements

### Pitch

- **AI slide generation**: Create slides from prompts
- **Smart templates**: Less aggressive than Beautiful.ai but helpful
- **Limited AI editing**: Some AI-assisted text refinement

**Winner: Beautiful.ai** — AI is more deeply integrated into the core experience. The auto-formatting itself is an AI feature that works on every slide, every time.

## Templates

| Aspect | Beautiful.ai | Pitch |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Template count | 60+ smart templates | 100+ templates |
| Customizability | Limited (by design) | Full creative control |
| Quality | Consistently high | Varies (most are excellent) |
| Industry-specific | Limited | Yes (startup, sales, marketing) |

**Winner: Pitch** for variety and flexibility. **Beautiful.ai** for guaranteed consistency.

## Presentation Delivery

### Beautiful.ai
- Present from browser
- Share via link with analytics
- Export to PDF/PowerPoint
- No video recording

### Pitch
- Present from browser
- Video recording and embedding in slides
- Screen recording overlay
- Viewer engagement analytics
- Export to PDF/PowerPoint
- Embed presentations on websites

**Winner: Pitch** — Video recording, engagement analytics, and embedding options [make](/tools/make/) Pitch better for async presentation delivery.

## Best Use Cases

### Choose Beautiful.ai for:
- **Sales teams** who need consistently branded decks without designers
- **Executives** creating presentations quickly without worrying about design
- **Non-designers** who want a foolproof way to make attractive slides
- **Standardized presentations** where brand consistency matters more than creativity

### Choose Pitch for:
- **Startup fundraising** (designed with pitch decks in mind)
- **Marketing teams** collaborating on campaigns in real-time
- **Remote teams** building decks together synchronously
- **Sales** with presentation analytics to track prospect engagement
- **Teams** that value collaboration over automation

## Who Should Skip Both?

If you need maximum creative control, complex animations, or extensive offline capability, traditional tools like PowerPoint or Keynote still serve those needs better. For simple presentations with real-time collaboration and zero cost, [Google Slides](/tools/google-slides/) remains hard to beat.

## The Verdict

**[Pitch](/tools/pitch/)** (4.4/5) wins overall for most teams in 2026. The combination of a free plan, real-time collaboration, beautiful templates, presentation analytics, and video features makes it the more versatile choice at a lower price point.

**[Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/)** (4.3/5) wins for organizations that prioritize design consistency and want AI to handle all visual decisions. If your team struggles to make presentations look good, Beautiful.ai's guardrails are genuinely valuable — the auto-formatting prevents every common design mistake.

**Our recommendation**: Start with Pitch's free plan. If you find your team consistently makes ugly slides despite good templates, that's when Beautiful.ai's AI formatting becomes worth the premium.
