# Duolingo Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Duolingo is gamified language learning app with 40+ languages, ai-powered practice, and bite-sized daily lessons. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Duolingo?

Duolingo is gamified language learning app with 40+ languages, ai-powered practice, and bite-sized daily lessons. It is designed for language learners, students, travelers and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the education space.
