# Whereby Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Whereby is browser-based video conferencing platform requiring no downloads or logins for guests, with embeddable video rooms for websites and apps. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Whereby?

Whereby is browser-based video conferencing platform requiring no downloads or logins for guests, with embeddable video rooms for websites and apps. It is designed for small teams, consultants, telehealth providers and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the video conferencing space.
