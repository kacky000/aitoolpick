# n8n vs Zapier vs Make: Which Automation Tool Wins in 2026?

Workflow automation went from "nice to have" to "core infrastructure" in 2026. With AI agents now triggering most internal workflows, the choice of platform matters more than ever. Here is the honest comparison between the three platforms most teams shortlist.

## TL;DR

| Need | Pick |
|------|------|
| Fastest setup, most apps | **Zapier** |
| Visual builder, complex logic, best price/perf | **Make** |
| Self-host, AI-native, no per-task pricing | **n8n** |

## Pricing in 2026

- **Zapier**: Free for 100 tasks. Starter $19.99/mo, Professional $49/mo, Team $69/mo. Pricing is per task — costs scale linearly with volume.
- **Make**: Free 1,000 ops. Core $9, Pro $16, Teams $29. Operations are cheaper than tasks; complex scenarios cost less.
- **n8n**: Self-hosted is free forever. Cloud starts at $20/mo for 2,500 executions (not tasks — full workflows). Best deal for high-volume teams.

## Ease of Use

Zapier is still the most beginner-friendly. If you have never built an automation, you can ship one in five minutes. Make has a slight learning curve because of its visual canvas, but advanced users prefer it. n8n is the most technical — you need to think in nodes and data structures.

## AI Features

All three added native AI nodes in 2026.
- **Zapier**: Tight integration with OpenAI, Anthropic, and a built-in "AI Actions" tool.
- **Make**: Strongest AI orchestration — built-in branching based on LLM output.
- **n8n**: Most flexible — you can self-host with your own model weights.

## App Coverage

- Zapier: 7,000+ apps (largest)
- Make: 2,000+ apps
- n8n: 500+ official, plus community-built nodes

If your stack is unusual, Zapier wins. If you live in standard SaaS, Make or n8n is fine.

## Self-Hosting

Only n8n offers true self-hosting. For privacy-sensitive workflows (healthcare, finance, internal HR), this is decisive.

## Who Should Pick What

- **Solo founders**: Zapier — speed beats price at low volume.
- **Agencies with many client workflows**: Make — best cost per scenario.
- **Engineering teams or privacy-first orgs**: n8n.

## Bottom Line

There is no single winner in 2026. The right pick depends on your task volume, technical depth, and whether self-hosting matters. Most teams end up using two — Zapier for client-facing automations and n8n for internal infrastructure.
