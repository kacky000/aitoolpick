Hiring internationally without an Employer of Record (EOR) is a compliance minefield. Here are the best EOR services in 2026.

## What Is an Employer of Record?

An EOR legally employs workers in countries where you don't have a legal entity. The EOR handles payroll, taxes, benefits, and compliance — you handle the day-to-day work.

**When you need an EOR:**
- Hiring a full-time employee in a new country
- Expanding globally without opening a subsidiary
- Staying compliant with local labor laws

## Detailed Comparisons

### Remote — Best Overall EOR for SMBs

Remote is the go-to EOR for companies that want predictable pricing, solid customer support, and a clean UI.

**Pricing:**
- EOR: $599/employee/month (or $299 with annual commitment in some markets)
- Contractor Management: $29/contractor/month (free for basic)
- Global Payroll: Custom

**Strengths:**
- Transparent flat-rate pricing
- Owned entities in most countries (no third-party partners = fewer issues)
- IP protection built-in
- Strong integrations (HRIS, payroll)

**Weaknesses:**
- Fewer countries than Deel (80 vs 150+)
- No HRIS for core HR

**Best for:** Series A–C companies hiring globally for the first time.

### Deel — Best Coverage & Contractor Management

Deel covers 150+ countries and is the most flexible platform for mixing contractors and employees.

**Pricing:**
- EOR: $599/employee/month
- Contractor Management: $49/contractor/month (or free for simple contracts)
- HRIS: Free for up to 200 employees

**Strengths:**
- 150+ country EOR coverage (most in the industry)
- Best contractor management platform
- Free built-in HRIS (Deel HR)
- Crypto payroll options
- Built-in compliance tools (background checks, equipment)

**Weaknesses:**
- Uses third-party partners in some countries
- Customer support can be slower than Remote
- Complex pricing with many add-ons

**Best for:** Companies with a mix of contractors and full-time employees across many countries.

### Oyster HR — Best Value EOR

Oyster combines competitive EOR pricing with strong integrations and a focus on ethical employment.

**Pricing:**
- EOR: $499/employee/month (lower than Remote/Deel)
- Contractor: $29/contractor/month
- Scale: Custom for 10+ employees

**Strengths:**
- $100/month cheaper than Remote/Deel
- Strong Greenhouse, BambooHR, Workday integrations
- Equity grants and benefits management
- "Thoughtful Employer" certification program

**Weaknesses:**
- Smaller country coverage (130 vs 150+)
- Less brand recognition than Deel/Remote

**Best for:** Cost-conscious companies hiring in major markets (US, UK, EU, Canada, APAC).

### Rippling — Best for All-in-One HR + EOR

Rippling isn't just an EOR — it's a full workforce management platform that includes EOR capabilities.

**Pricing:**
- Core platform: ~$8/user/month
- EOR: Added as a module (custom pricing)
- Full platform: Typically $15–25/user/month with modules

**Strengths:**
- One platform for US payroll, global EOR, IT, benefits
- Automated onboarding/offboarding
- Best device management integration
- Fastest growing HR platform

**Weaknesses:**
- EOR coverage is narrower than dedicated providers
- Pricing can escalate with modules
- Overkill for companies just needing EOR

**Best for:** Companies wanting a single platform for domestic + global workforce management.

## EOR vs. Entity Setup: Which Is Right?

| Situation | Use EOR | Set Up Entity |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| 1–5 employees in a new country | ✅ | ❌ Too costly |
| Long-term 10+ employees in one country | Maybe | ✅ Lower cost at scale |
| Testing a new market | ✅ | ❌ |
| Compliance expertise needed fast | ✅ | ❌ |

**Rule of thumb:** EOR costs $600/month per employee. A local entity typically costs $5,000–$20,000 to set up. Break-even point is usually 8–15 employees in a single country.

## Key Compliance Features to Look For

1. **Owned entities** vs. partner networks (owned = more reliable)
2. **IP assignment** clauses in employment contracts
3. **Benefits administration** (health insurance, local mandates)
4. **Background checks** integration
5. **Equity management** (critical for startups giving stock options)

## Bottom Line

- **Best overall:** Remote ($599, clean pricing, owned entities)
- **Widest coverage:** Deel ($599, 150+ countries, best for contractors too)
- **Best value:** Oyster ($499, strong integrations)
- **All-in-one HR:** Rippling (more expensive but replaces multiple tools)
