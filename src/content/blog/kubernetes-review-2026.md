# Kubernetes Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Kubernetes is open-source container orchestration platform for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications across clusters of machines. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Kubernetes?

Kubernetes is open-source container orchestration platform for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications across clusters of machines. It is designed for platform teams, large organizations, microservices architectures and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the devops space.

## Pros

- De facto standard for container orchestration
- Highly extensible with custom resources and operators
- Automatic scaling and self-healing capabilities
- Multi-cloud and on-premises deployment support
- Massive community and ecosystem

## Cons

- Notoriously complex to set up and manage
- Overkill for simple applications
- Steep learning curve even for experienced engineers
