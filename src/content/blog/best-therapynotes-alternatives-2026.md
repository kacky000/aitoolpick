TherapyNotes is a strong behavioral-health platform, but it isn't the right fit for everyone. Maybe you want a lower entry price, a more polished client portal, or multi-discipline charting for a mixed clinic. Here are the **7 best TherapyNotes alternatives** to consider in 2026, with honest notes on who each one suits.

## Quick Comparison

| Tool | Starting price | Best for |
|------|----------------|----------|
| SimplePractice | $29/mo | Solo therapists wanting polish |
| Jane App | $54/mo | Multi-discipline clinics |
| Practice Better | $25/mo | Health & wellness coaches |
| SimplePractice (Plus) | $99/mo | Telehealth + small groups |

## 1. SimplePractice — Best Overall Alternative

[SimplePractice](/pricing/simplepractice/) is the most direct alternative. It matches TherapyNotes on notes, scheduling, and insurance billing while offering a more polished client portal and online booking. Pricing runs $29 (Starter), $69 (Essential), and $99 (Plus with telehealth).

**Choose it if:** you want a modern client experience and flexibility beyond pure therapy. See our full [SimplePractice review](/blog/simplepractice-review-2026/) and the [TherapyNotes vs SimplePractice comparison](/blog/therapynotes-vs-simplepractice-2026/).

## 2. Jane App — Best for Multi-Discipline Clinics

[Jane App](/pricing/jane-app/) shines for clinics running physiotherapy, massage, acupuncture, and mental health under one roof. Its booking engine is best-in-class. Pricing starts at $54/month (Base), $79 with insurance features.

**Choose it if:** you run a multi-practitioner clinic and need top-tier scheduling and charting.

## 3. Practice Better — Best for Wellness Coaches

[Practice Better](/pricing/practice-better/) targets health and wellness practitioners — nutritionists, dietitians, and coaches — with client management, programs, and telehealth. Pricing starts at $25/month, making it the budget-friendly pick.

**Choose it if:** you're a coach or dietitian who doesn't need behavioral-health-specific clinical notes.

## 4. SimplePractice Plus — Best for Telehealth-First Practices

If video sessions are central to your work, SimplePractice's **Plus** plan ($99) bundles telehealth, group features, and advanced billing. It's pricier but consolidates everything in one place.

**Choose it if:** telehealth and a small team are your priority.

## 5. Jane App (Insurance Tier) — Best for Insurance-Heavy Clinics

Jane's **Insurance** tier ($79) adds robust claim handling for North American clinics. Combined with its scheduling, it's a strong choice for busy, insurance-billing practices.

**Choose it if:** you process high claim volumes across practitioners.

## 6. Practice Better (Professional) — Best All-in-One for Programs

Practice Better's **Professional** tier ($59) adds programs, protocols, and group coaching tools — ideal if you sell structured wellness programs alongside 1:1 sessions.

**Choose it if:** you run group programs or memberships.

## 7. SimplePractice Starter — Best Budget Entry

For brand-new, cash-only practices, SimplePractice's **Starter** plan ($29) is the cheapest way to get scheduling, notes, and a basic portal without insurance overhead.

**Choose it if:** you're just launching and watching every dollar.

## How to Choose

- **Pure behavioral health, insurance billing:** TherapyNotes is still excellent — but SimplePractice Essential is the closest alternative.
- **Multi-discipline clinic:** Jane App.
- **Wellness coaching / nutrition:** Practice Better.
- **Telehealth-first:** SimplePractice Plus.

The best TherapyNotes alternative depends on your discipline and budget. SimplePractice is the closest like-for-like swap, Jane App wins for multi-discipline clinics, and Practice Better is the value pick for wellness coaches.
