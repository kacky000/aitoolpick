Beautiful.ai doesn't offer a free plan. That's the first thing most people notice — and it's a fair concern when tools like [Canva](/tools/canva/) and [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) let you build presentations at no cost. So the real question is whether the paid plans deliver enough to justify the spend.

Here's a full breakdown of what each Beautiful.ai plan costs, what's included, and who each one makes sense for.

## Beautiful.ai Pricing at a Glance

| Plan | Price | Billing | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Pro | $12/user/mo | Monthly or annual | Individuals, freelancers |
| Team | $40/user/mo | Monthly or annual | Small teams, startups |
| Enterprise | Custom | Annual | Large orgs, strict compliance |

All plans are priced per user. There's no flat-rate option, so costs scale linearly with headcount.

## Pro Plan — $12/user/month

The Pro plan is the entry point. At $12 per user per month, it's cheaper than most presentation tools at this tier.

**What's included:**

- Unlimited presentations
- Full smart template library
- AI slide generation — describe what you want and the tool builds a draft
- Auto-layout engine that reformats content as you add or remove elements
- Export to PDF and PowerPoint
- Basic analytics (view counts, time spent per slide)
- Revision history

**What's missing:**

- No shared team workspace
- No brand controls (custom fonts, locked color palettes)
- No centralized asset library
- No advanced collaboration features

**Verdict:** Pro works well for solo users — consultants putting together client decks, freelancers building pitch presentations, or anyone who needs polished slides fast without wrestling with alignment grids. The AI generation and auto-layout are available at this tier, which is where most of the time savings come from.

## Team Plan — $40/user/month

Team is where Beautiful.ai starts to make sense for organizations. The jump from $12 to $40 per user is steep, but it unlocks features that matter when multiple people touch the same presentations.

**Everything in Pro, plus:**

- Shared team workspace with folders and permissions
- Brand controls — lock down colors, fonts, and logos so every deck stays on-brand
- Centralized asset library for images, icons, and approved media
- Real-time collaboration with comments and slide-level feedback
- Advanced analytics — track who viewed each deck, which slides got the most attention
- Custom slide themes
- Priority support

**Verdict:** The brand controls alone justify the upgrade if you're tired of team members going off-brand. The shared workspace and collaboration tools turn Beautiful.ai from a personal tool into a team platform. For a team of five, you're looking at $200/month — not trivial, but competitive with what you'd pay for Pitch or a Canva Teams subscription.

## Enterprise — Custom Pricing

Enterprise is the usual "call us" tier. No published price, which typically means north of $40/user/month with volume discounts for larger contracts.

**Additional features:**

- SSO/SAML authentication
- Advanced admin controls and user management
- Custom onboarding and training
- Dedicated account manager
- Enhanced security and compliance (SOC 2, GDPR)
- API access

**Verdict:** Relevant for companies with 50+ users or strict IT requirements. If you need SSO or compliance certifications, this is the only option. Negotiate hard — enterprise pricing is always flexible.

## No Free Plan — Is That a Dealbreaker?

It depends on what you need. Beautiful.ai used to offer a limited free tier, but that's gone. Today, the cheapest way in is $12/month on Pro.

For casual users who build a few presentations per year, that's hard to justify when free alternatives exist. [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) gives you AI-generated presentations at no cost, and Canva's free tier includes basic presentation templates.

But if you're building presentations regularly — weekly client updates, monthly board decks, sales pitches — the time saved by Beautiful.ai's auto-layout and AI generation can easily be worth $12/month. The tool eliminates the formatting busywork that eats up hours in PowerPoint or Google Slides.

Beautiful.ai's main advantage is the auto-layout engine. Every other tool makes you drag elements around manually. Beautiful.ai reformats the entire slide when you change content, keeping everything balanced. If you've ever spent 20 minutes nudging text boxes in PowerPoint, you know why this matters.

[Gamma](/tools/gamma/) is the strongest free alternative, especially for quick AI-generated decks. But it lacks the design polish and brand controls that Beautiful.ai offers at the Team tier.

## Which Plan Should You Pick?

- **Solo freelancer or consultant** → Pro ($12/mo). You get AI generation, auto-layout, and unlimited decks. That's everything you need for one-person workflows.

- **Small team (3–10 people)** → Team ($40/mo). The brand controls and shared workspace prevent the chaos that happens when everyone builds slides independently. Budget: $120–$400/month.

- **Large organization with IT requirements** → Enterprise. If your company requires SSO, SOC 2, or centralized admin controls, this is the path. Get a quote and negotiate.

- **Occasional user / tight budget** → Skip Beautiful.ai for now. Use [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) or Canva's free tier. Come back when presentation volume justifies the cost.

## How to Save on Beautiful.ai

1. **Annual billing** — Paying yearly typically saves 10–20% compared to monthly
2. **Start with Pro** — Test the workflow before committing a team to the $40/user tier
3. **Audit seat count** — Only pay for users who actively build presentations, not everyone who occasionally views them

## Bottom Line

Beautiful.ai earns its price through one thing: it makes every slide look good without manual formatting. The Pro plan at $12/month is a fair deal for regular presenters. The Team plan at $40/month is worth it when brand consistency and collaboration matter.

No free plan means it's not for everyone. But for people who build presentations as a core part of their job, the time savings pay for themselves quickly.
