Speechify is the most popular text-to-speech app for listening to content — not for creating voiceovers, but for consuming documents, articles, and books hands-free. The free plan is generous, but the Premium plan unlocks the best voices and speed options. Here's whether it's worth paying for.

## Speechify Pricing Plans 2026

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 | Casual readers |
| Premium | $11.58/month (billed annually) | Regular users |
| AI Studio | Varies | Content creators |
| Enterprise | Custom | Organizations |

*Note: If billed monthly, Premium is approximately $29/month. Annual billing saves about 60%.*

## What Each Speechify Plan Includes

### Free Plan ($0)
- Basic TTS voices (standard quality)
- Up to 5x listening speed
- Web app + Chrome extension
- Import PDFs, web articles, emails
- Limited to shorter documents

**Best for**: Casual users who want to try TTS without committing.

### Premium Plan ($11.58/month, billed annually)
- 30+ AI voices (much more natural)
- Up to 4.5x speed (optimized for comprehension)
- Unlimited document length
- OCR scanning (take a photo of physical text)
- Offline listening
- Priority support
- All platforms (iOS, Android, Mac, Chrome)

**Best for**: Students, professionals, ADHD learners, commuters who listen daily.

### AI Studio (Content Creation)
- Create and publish audio content
- Monetize audio articles
- Publishers and content teams

**Best for**: Bloggers and publishers who want to add audio to articles.

### Enterprise
- Admin controls
- SSO integration
- Usage analytics
- Bulk licensing
- Custom voice options

**Best for**: Schools, universities, accessibility programs, large organizations.

## Is Speechify Premium Worth $11.58/Month?

**Yes, if you:**
- Listen to content for more than 30 minutes per day
- Find standard voices distracting (Premium voices are noticeably better)
- Need to scan physical documents (OCR feature)
- Listen while commuting and need offline mode
- Have ADHD or reading difficulties (TTS significantly improves retention)

**No, if you:**
- Only occasionally want to hear an article read aloud
- Prefer natural reading pace (under 1.5x speed)
- Just need a simple TTS for occasional use

At $11.58/month ($139/year), it's about $0.38/day. For daily users, it's excellent value. For occasional use, the free tier is fine.

## Speechify Free vs Premium: What's the Real Difference?

The biggest differences in practice:

| Feature | Free | Premium |
|---------|------|---------|
| Voice quality | Standard (robotic) | AI voices (natural) |
| Speed | Up to 5x (but harder to understand) | Up to 4.5x (optimized) |
| Document length | Limited | Unlimited |
| Offline listening | No | Yes |
| OCR scanning | No | Yes |
| iOS/Android app | Yes | Yes + all features |

**The voice quality gap is the biggest reason to upgrade.** Free voices are noticeably robotic; Premium AI voices are dramatically more natural and easier to listen to for long periods.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Speechify have a free plan?**  
Yes — basic voices and standard features. Unlimited listening time on web app, limited on mobile.

**Can Speechify read any document?**  
It reads PDFs, web articles, emails, Google Docs, EPUB books, and more. OCR (Premium) can even read physical paper.

**Is Speechify worth it for ADHD?**  
Many users with ADHD report significant improvement in comprehension and retention when listening to text rather than reading. At $11.58/month, it's worth trying the trial.

**What speed should I use Speechify at?**  
Most users settle between 1.5x-2.5x after a few weeks of practice. Starting at 1.25x and gradually increasing works well.
