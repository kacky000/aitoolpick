Ahrefs introduced a game-changing $29 Starter plan in January 2026, cutting the entry price by nearly 80%. Here's everything you need to know before choosing a plan.

## Ahrefs Pricing Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Best For |
|------|---------|--------------------|----------|
| Starter | $29 | N/A (monthly only) | Beginners, solopreneurs |
| Lite | $129 | ~$108 | Freelancers, small sites |
| Standard | $249 | ~$208 | Growing content teams |
| Advanced | $449 | ~$374 | SEO agencies, large sites |
| Enterprise | $1,499+ | $1,499 (annual only) | Enterprise teams |

Annual billing saves approximately 17% (equivalent to 2 months free).

## Plan-by-Plan Breakdown

### Starter — $29/month

The newest and cheapest plan. Monthly billing only (no annual option).

**What you get:**
- Site Explorer (limited to 1,000 rows per report)
- Keywords Explorer (basic access)
- Up to 5 projects
- 1,000 crawl credits/month
- 3 months of historical data

**What you don't get:**
- Content Explorer
- Rank Tracker
- API access
- Batch analysis

**Verdict:** Good for site owners doing occasional backlink checks or keyword research. Not suitable for active SEO campaigns.

### Lite — $129/month ($108/month annual)

The entry-level professional plan.

**What you get:**
- Full Site Explorer access
- Keywords Explorer
- Site Audit (up to 5,000 pages/month)
- Rank Tracker (750 keywords)
- 5 projects
- 6 months of historical data
- 1 user seat

**Verdict:** Best for freelancers managing 1–2 client sites. The 750 keyword limit is the main constraint.

### Standard — $249/month ($208/month annual)

The most popular plan for growing teams.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Lite
- Content Explorer
- Rank Tracker (2,000 keywords)
- Site Audit (up to 100,000 pages/month)
- 2 years of historical data
- 1 user seat (additional users: $60/month each)

**Verdict:** The sweet spot for content marketers and in-house SEO teams. Content Explorer alone justifies the upgrade from Lite.

### Advanced — $449/month ($374/month annual)

For agencies and large teams.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Standard
- 5 user seats included
- Rank Tracker (5,000 keywords)
- Google Looker Studio integration
- Advanced Web Explorer
- 3 years of historical data
- Access logs analysis

**Verdict:** Makes sense when you have 3+ team members actively using Ahrefs. The per-seat cost drops significantly compared to adding users to Standard.

### Enterprise — from $1,499/month (annual commitment)

Custom solutions for large organizations.

**What you get:**
- Unlimited everything (within fair use)
- Custom user seats
- SSO/SAML authentication
- API access at scale
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom onboarding

**Verdict:** For agencies managing 50+ sites or enterprise SEO teams. Pricing is custom beyond $1,499.

## Is There a Free Trial?

No free trial, but Ahrefs offers a **7-day trial for $7** (Lite plan only, occasionally available). The Starter plan at $29 is effectively the lowest-risk entry point.

## Which Plan Should You Choose?

- **Just starting out / checking a site occasionally** → Starter ($29)
- **Freelancer with 1–2 clients** → Lite ($129)
- **In-house SEO or content team** → Standard ($249) — best value
- **Agency with multiple clients** → Advanced ($449)
- **Enterprise or very large agency** → Enterprise ($1,499+)

## How to Save on Ahrefs

1. **Annual billing**: Saves ~$500/year on Standard
2. **Starter plan first**: Test if Ahrefs fits your workflow before upgrading
3. **Negotiate**: Enterprise pricing is flexible — don't accept the first quote
