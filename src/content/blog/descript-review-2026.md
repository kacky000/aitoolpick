AI-powered audio and video editor that lets you edit media by editing text transcripts. Founded in 2017, it has established itself as a significant player in the video editing category. With a user rating of 4.5/5, it primarily serves Podcasters, Youtubers, Content Creators, Marketers, Educators.

Descript offers the following pricing structure:
