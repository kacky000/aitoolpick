# Affinity Designer Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

Affinity Designer is professional vector graphic design software and illustrator alternative with no subscription, offering precise vector and raster design in one tool. In this review, we cover everything you need to know before subscribing.

## What is Affinity Designer?

Affinity Designer is professional vector graphic design software and illustrator alternative with no subscription, offering precise vector and raster design in one tool. It is designed for illustrators, graphic designers, budget conscious professionals and anyone looking for a reliable solution in the design space.
