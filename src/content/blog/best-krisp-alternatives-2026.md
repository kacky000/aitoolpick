Krisp made its name with AI noise cancellation, and it now bundles meeting transcription and notes too. But depending on whether you care most about clean audio, accurate transcripts, or automatic summaries, another tool may serve you better. Here are the **6 best Krisp alternatives in 2026**, with the use case each one wins.

## Quick Comparison

| Tool | Best for | Noise cancellation | Free tier |
|------|----------|--------------------|-----------|
| **Otter** | Live transcription | No | Yes |
| **Fireflies** | Meeting search + CRM | No | Yes |
| **Fathom** | Free AI notetaker | No | Yes |
| **Otter + native** | Notes inside meetings | No | Yes |
| **Zoom AI Companion** | Zoom-native summaries | Built-in | With plan |
| **NVIDIA Broadcast** | Free noise removal | Yes | Yes |

## 1. Otter — Best for Live Transcription

Otter delivers real-time transcripts you can read as the meeting happens, plus automatic summaries and action items. If transcription is your priority over audio cleanup, it's the strongest pick. See the [Otter pricing breakdown](/blog/otter-ai-pricing-2026/) or read the full [Otter review](/blog/otter-ai-review-2026/). For a direct head-to-head, see [Krisp vs Otter](/blog/krisp-vs-otter-2026/).

## 2. Fireflies — Best for Search and CRM

Fireflies records, transcribes, and makes every past meeting searchable, with strong CRM integrations for sales teams. If you need to mine conversations later, it's the most complete archive. See the [Fireflies pricing details](/pricing/fireflies-ai/).

## 3. Fathom — Best Free Notetaker

Fathom's AI notetaker is genuinely free for individuals, generating summaries and highlights without a paywall. For solo users who want notes without cost, it's the value pick.

## 4. Zoom AI Companion — Best for Zoom Users

If your meetings already live in Zoom, its built-in AI Companion summarizes calls and surfaces action items with zero extra software, and Zoom's own audio processing handles background noise.

## 5. NVIDIA Broadcast — Best Free Noise Removal

For Krisp's core feature alone — removing background noise — NVIDIA Broadcast does it free on RTX hardware, with excellent quality. If noise cancellation is all you need, it's hard to beat.

## How to Choose

- **Want live transcripts?** Otter.
- **Need searchable meeting history?** Fireflies.
- **On a budget?** Fathom or NVIDIA Broadcast.
- **Live in Zoom?** Zoom AI Companion.

Krisp is still the cleanest choice when audio quality is the whole job, but if transcription, summaries, or cost matter more, one of these fits better. For the broader field, see our roundup of the [best AI meeting assistants](/blog/best-ai-meeting-assistants-2026/).
